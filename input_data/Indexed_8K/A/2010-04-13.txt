Item 7.01.              Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
Agilent Technologies' planned acquisition of Varian, Inc. remains subject to regulatory approvals and other customary closing conditions.  Agilent currently expects that the transaction will close in either late April 2010 or, more likely, early May 2010.
