Item 8.01                  Other Events.
 
On May 12, 2010, the European Commission confirmed to Agilent Technologies, Inc. ("Agilent") that the conditions set forth in its clearance decision of January 10, 2010 for the proposed acquisition by Agilent of Varian, Inc. (the "Merger") to proceed have now been met. The closing of the Merger remains subject to regulatory approval by the United States Federal Trade Commission ("FTC") and the satisfaction or waiver of certain other customary closing conditions and is expected to occur promptly after receipt of FTC clearance.
