Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
The following information is furnished pursuant to Item 7.01, "Regulation FD Disclosure."
On November 29, 2006, Alain J. P. Belda, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Alcoa Inc. ("Alcoa"), gave a presentation on the strength of the aluminum industry and Alcoa at the Bear Stearns Commodities and Capital Goods Conference in New York, New York, beginning at approximately 8:30 a.m. Due to technical difficulties with the audio portion of the live webcast presentation, listeners may not have been able to hear the live presentation. A transcript of Mr. Belda's presentation is attached hereto as Exhibit 99 and is incorporated herein by reference.
