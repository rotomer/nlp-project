Item 8.01    Other Events.
 
Abbott Laboratories and Zimmer, Inc. have completed the sale of Abbott's spine business to Zimmer for approximately $360 million in cash. 
Abbott expects the sale of its spine business to result in a one-time pretax gain of at least $150 million and intends to treat this gain as a specified item in the fourth quarter 2008.
