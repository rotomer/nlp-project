Item 5.02  Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Appointment of Certain Officers
 
Greg W. Linder, Vice President and Controller, has informed Abbott that he will retire from the Company, effective February 28, 2013.
 
Robert E. Funck, 51, has been appointed Vice President and Controller, effective March 1, 2013.  Mr. Funck has served as the Company's Vice President, Chief Ethics and Compliance Officer since 2009, as Vice President, Internal Audit from 2007 to 2009, and as Vice President and Treasurer from 2005 to 2007. Mr. Funck became a corporate officer in 2005.  Mr. Funck joined the Company in 1987.
