 Item 8.01. Other Events.
    ACE Limited confirmed that in connection with the previously disclosed investigations pertaining to underwriting practices in ACE's excess casualty unit, ACE terminated three employees of that unit and suspended two other employees of that unit.  All of these actions were taken in the fourth quarter of 2004.  Two of the employees were terminated as a result of conduct discovered in the investigation.  A third employee, who initially had been suspended, was subsequently terminated in the fourth quarter of 2004 because of a failure to cooperate with the investigation.
