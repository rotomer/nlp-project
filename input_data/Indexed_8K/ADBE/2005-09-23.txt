Section 5  Corporate Governance and Management
 
Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officer; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On September 1, 2005, Adobe Systems Incorporated filed a current report on Form 8-K to report that  Edward W. Barnholt had been elected as a new member of Adobe's Board of Directors. Pursuant to Instruction 2 to Item 5.02 of Form 8-K, this amendment on Form 8-K/A is being filed to report that on September 20, 2005, Adobe's Board of Directors appointed Mr. Barnholt to the Executive Compensation Committee and Nominating and Governance Committee of the Board of Directors effective December 3, 2005.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
