(d)       On September 10, 2007, Automatic Data Processing, Inc., a Delaware corporation, announced that Messrs. Eric C. Fast and Gregory L. Summe had been elected to the Registrant's Board of Directors on September 10, 2007.
Mr. Fast has been named to serve as a member of the Audit Committee of the Board of Directors. Mr. Fast is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Crane Co.
Mr. Summe has been named to serve as a member of the Nominating/Governance Committee of the Board of Directors. Mr. Summe is the Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President of PerkinElmer, Inc.
These elections increase the size of the Registrant's board to 11 directors.
