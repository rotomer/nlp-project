(d)       On April 30, 2008, Automatic Data Processing, Inc., a Delaware corporation, announced that Charles H. Noski and Sharon T. Rowlands had been elected to the Registrant's Board of Directors on April 30, 2008.
Mr. Noski, who was formerly chief financial officer and vice chairman of the board of AT&T Corporation, is presently a director of Microsoft Corporation, Morgan Stanley, and Air Products and Chemicals, Inc. Ms. Rowlands was president and chief executive officer of Thomson Financial until earlier this month.
These elections increase the size of the Registrant's board to 12 directors.
