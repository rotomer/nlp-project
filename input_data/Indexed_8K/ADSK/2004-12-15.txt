Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
On December 9, 2004, the Board of Directors of Autodesk, Inc. (the "Company") approved amendments to the Company's form of Indemnification Agreement to be executed by the Company and each of its executive officers and directors. In general, the amendments concern the circumstances under which the Company must advance expenses to an indemnitee and the obligations of an indemnitee to return to the Company expenses previously advanced by the Company. The revised indemnification agreement is expected to be entered into by the Company and all of the Company's executive officers and directors.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
 
Date: December 15, 2004
