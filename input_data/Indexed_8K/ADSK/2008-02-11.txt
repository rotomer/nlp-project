Resignation of Andrew D. Miller
On February 6, 2008, Andrew D. Miller announced his resignation as the Company's Vice President of Finance and Chief Accounting Officer, effective as of the second week of April 2008. Mr. Miller has accepted a position to become the chief financial officer of Cepheid, a public company in Sunnyvale, California. Following Mr. Miller's resignation, Alfred J. Castino, the Company's Chief Financial Officer, will also assume the responsibilities of Chief Accounting Officer.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: February 11, 2008
