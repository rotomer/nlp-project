On August 3, 2010, the Human Resources Committee of American Electric Power Company, Inc. granted Nicholas K. Akins, Venita McCellon-Allen, Robert P. Powers and Brian X. Tierney 41,380 restricted stock units each to better ensure the retention of these CEO succession candidates. The restricted stock units will vest, subject to the officers continuous employment, in three equal installments on August 3, 2013, August 3, 2014 and August 3, 2015. The restricted stock units will be issued under the Companys Amended and Restated Long-Term Incentive Plan.
