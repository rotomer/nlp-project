Effective November 12, 2011, the Board elected Robert P. Powers as Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer of American Electric Power Company, Inc. (Company).   Mr. Powers, 57, was elected President- AEP Utilities of the Company in 2008.  He has previously served as Executive Vice President- AEP Utilities East from 2006-2008 and Executive Vice President- Generation from 2003- 2006.
