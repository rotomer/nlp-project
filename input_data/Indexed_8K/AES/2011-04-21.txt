On April 21, 2011, The AES Corporation (the "Company") held its Annual Meeting of Stockholders in Arlington, Virginia.
The results of the matters voted on at the Annual Meeting are provided below.
: The election of eleven directors to hold office for a one-year term expiring at the annual meeting in 2012 and until their respective successors are elected and qualified:
Proposal 1
 
There were no abstentions in Proposal 1.
: The ratification of Ernst & Young LLP as AES' Independent Registered Public Accounting Firm for the year 2011.
Proposal 2
 
: The consideration of a (non-binding) advisory vote on executive compensation.
Proposal 3
 
: The consideration of a (non-binding) advisory vote on the frequency of the advisory vote on executive compensation.
Proposal 4
 
In accordance with the Board's recommendation as set forth in the 2011 proxy statement, and based on the voting results for this proposal, the Company determined that an advisory vote to approve the compensation of the named executive officers of the Company will be conducted on an annual basis, until the next advisory vote on this matter is held.
