     Aflac was recently informed that it was not selected in a completed bid process to provide its products to the employees of Wal-Mart through payroll deduction starting in benefit plan year 2010. As of December 31, 2008, Wal-Mart was one of more than 427,700 Aflac U.S. payroll accounts and accounted for .6% of total consolidated (2.0% of total U.S.) annualized premiums in force at year-end and 1.1% of consolidated (1.8% of U.S.) new annualized premium sales during 2008. Aflac's current contract with Wal-Mart will end on December 31, 2009. Wal-Mart employees will have the option to keep their current Aflac insurance policies, which are individual guaranteed-renewable products, on a direct-bill basis.
