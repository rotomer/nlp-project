Section 5 - Corporate Governance and Management Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers. On April 21, 2005, American International Group, Inc. (AIG) was notified by Frank J. Hoenemeyer, director of AIG, that he will retire from the Board of Directors following AIG's 2005 Annual Meeting of Shareholders.
