Section 5 - Corporate Governance and Management Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers, Election of Directors, Appointment of Principal Officers. On February 7, 2006 and February 10, 2006, respectively, American International Group, Inc. (AIG) was notified by Carla A. Hills and William S. Cohen, directors of AIG, that they will not stand for reelection to the Board of Directors at AIG's 2006 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Ms. Hills is a member of the Audit Committee and the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of AIG's Board of Directors. Mr. Cohen is a member of the Public Policy and Social Responsibility Committee of AIG's Board of Directors.
