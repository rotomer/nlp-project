Section 5  Corporate Governance and Management
 
On May 16, 2012, AIG held its Annual Meeting of Shareholders at which the shareholders voted upon (i) the election of twelve nominees as directors by the holders of AIG's common stock, par value $2.50 per share (AIG Common Stock), for a one-year term; (ii) a non-binding shareholder resolution to approve executive compensation; and (iii) the ratification of the appointment of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as AIG's independent registered public accounting firm for 2012.
The shareholders elected all twelve director nominees presented, approved the non-binding shareholder resolution to approve executive compensation and approved the ratification of the appointment of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as AIG's independent registered public accounting firm for 2012.
