Section 8  Other Events
Item 8.01. Other Events.
On November 21, 2012, American International Group, Inc. ("AIG") issued a press release announcing its entry into a non-binding term sheet with The People's Insurance Company (Group) of China Limited ("PICC Group") and PICC Life Insurance Company Limited ("PICC Life") with respect to the proposed establishment of a joint venture insurance agency company between AIG and PICC Life. AIG also announced that it has agreed to subscribe for approximately US$500 million worth of H shares in PICC Group's initial public offering in Hong Kong. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
