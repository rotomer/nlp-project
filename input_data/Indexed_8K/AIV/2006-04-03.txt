ITEM 8.01. Other Events.
     On March 31, 2006, Apartment Investment and Management Company ("Aimco") redeemed all 2 million outstanding shares of its privately held 8.5% Class X Cumulative Convertible Preferred Stock. The redemption price per share was $25.00 plus an amount equal to accumulated and unpaid dividends thereon to the redemption date of $0.53125, for a total redemption price of $25.53125 per share. The aggregate redemption price of $51,062,500 was paid in cash.
