Item 8.01 Other Events.
On September 30, 2007, Apartment Investment and Management Company redeemed all 1,904,762 outstanding shares of its privately held 8.1% Class W Cumulative Convertible Preferred Stock (the "Class W"). The redemption price per share was $54.613125 (which includes a redemption price per share of $53.55 (which is 102% of the $52.50 per share liquidation preference) plus $1.063125 (which is the per share amount of accumulated, accrued and unpaid dividends on the Class W through the redemption date)) or an aggregate redemption price of $104,025,005, which was paid in cash.
