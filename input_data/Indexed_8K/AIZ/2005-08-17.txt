Section 5  Corporate Governance and Management
Item 5.02 Election of Directors
On Friday, August 12, 2005, the Board of Directors of the registrant elected Charles John Koch to the Assurant, Inc. Board of Directors. Mr. Koch was elected to fill a vacancy in Class II and will serve on the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors. There are no arrangements or understandings between Mr. Koch and any other person pursuant to which Mr. Koch was selected as a director.
Assurant made public the election of Mr. Koch through a press release issued on Tuesday, August 16, 2005 which is available on our website at www.assurant.com.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
