Item 5.02.    Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
On Monday, May 15, 2006, the Board of Directors of the Registrant elected Juan N. Cento to the Assurant, Inc. Board of Directors. Mr. Cento was elected to fill a vacancy in Class III and will serve on the Compensation Committee. There are no arrangements or understandings between Mr. Cento and any other person pursuant to which Mr. Cento was selected as a director.
Assurant made public the election of Mr. Cento through a press release issued on Wednesday, May 17, 2006, which is available on our website at www.assurant.com.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
