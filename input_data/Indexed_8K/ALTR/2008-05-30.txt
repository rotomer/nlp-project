Item  8.01.  Other Events
On May 2, 2008, Altera Corporation entered into a contract to conduct certain share repurchases pursuant to a plan (the Plan) complying with Rule 10b5-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the Exchange Act).  The Plan covers the period from June 2, 2008 to July 18, 2008, unless the Plan is earlier terminated.  Repurchases made under the Plan will comply with the requirements of Rule 10b5-1(c)(1)(i) and, to the extent applicable, Rule 10b-18 under the Exchange Act.  Depending upon prevailing market conditions and other factors, there can be no assurance that shares of Alteras common stock will be purchased pursuant to the Plan.  In no event will the cumulative amount of Altera common stock purchased under the Plan exceed the greater of $100 million or 7 million shares.
 
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
