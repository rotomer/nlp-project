                        ALTERA UPDATES THIRD QUARTER GUIDANCE
   
., September 2, 2008Altera Corporation (NASDAQ: ALTR) today announced its mid-quarter update for the third quarter of 2008.
San Jose, Calif
The company reaffirms that it expects third quarter sales to be flat to 3 percent lower compared to the second quarter of 2008, in line with prior guidance.
The companys 65-nm FPGA products are experiencing strong sequential growth when compared to the similar period in the prior quarter.
The company will announce third quarter results after market close on October 14, 2008.
