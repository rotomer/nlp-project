ITEM: Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement

By letter dated January 13, 2009, Altera Corporation ("Altera") notified Arrow Electronics Inc. ("Arrow") that effective January 20, 2009, Altera is terminating the Inventory Advances Arrangement Letter Agreement dated October 15, 2004 between Altera and Arrow (the "Agreement"). The Agreement was filed as Exhibit 10.35 to Altera's Annual Report on Form 10-K filed March 11, 2005. Pursuant to the Agreement, Altera made certain non-interest bearing cash advances to Arrow, its distributor. The Agreement was terminated as the original purpose is no longer relevant. Arrow continues to be Altera's distributor.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Dated: January 19, 2009
