Item 5.    Other Events and Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
Applied Materials, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), has entered into a First Amendment to Rights Agreement, dated as of November 6, 2002 (the "Rights Agreement Amendment"), with Harris Trust and Savings Bank, as Rights Agent (the "Prior Rights Agent"), which amends the Rights Agreement, dated as of July 7, 1999 (the "Rights Agreement"), between the Company and the Prior Rights Agent. The Rights Agreement Amendment confirms, among other things, the appointment of Computershare Investor Services, LLC, as the successor Rights Agent to the Prior Rights Agent under the Rights Agreement, as amended. The Rights Agreement Amendment also sets forth various amendments to the Rights Agreement which were deemed appropriate or necessary to effect such appointment. The Rights Agreement Amendment is attached hereto as Exhibit 4.1 and is incorporated herein by reference. The foregoing description of the Rights Agreement Amendment is qualified in its entirety by reference to such exhibit.
