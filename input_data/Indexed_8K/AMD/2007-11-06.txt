On November 1, 2007, Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (the "Company") announced that the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board") appointed Derrick R. Meyer to the Board, effective November 1, 2007. Mr. Meyer has not been appointed to any committees of the Board. Mr. Meyer is also the Company's President and Chief Operating Officer.
On November 1, 2007, the Compensation Committee of the Board also recommended that the Board approve a $150,000 increase to Mr. Meyer's annual base salary, from $674,000 to $824,000, effective as of November 1, 2007. The Board approved this increase.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
