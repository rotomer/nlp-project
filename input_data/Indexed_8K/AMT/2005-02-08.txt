Item 5.02 Election of Director.
 
On November 17, 2004, American Tower Corporation (the "Company") filed a Form 8-K (the "Report") announcing that Gustavo Lara had been elected to the board of directors of the Company. This Form 8-K/A amends the Report to include the disclosure that, on February 4, 2005, the board of directors appointed Mr. Lara to the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
