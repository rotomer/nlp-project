Item 5.02 Election of Director.
On June 1, 2007, American Tower Corporation (the "Company") filed a Form 8-K (the "Report") announcing that JoAnn A. Reed had been elected to the board of directors of the Company. This Form 8-K/A amends the Report to include the disclosure that, on November 1, 2007, the board of directors appointed Ms. Reed to the Audit Committee.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
