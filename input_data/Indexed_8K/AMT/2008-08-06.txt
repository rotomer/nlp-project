On August 6, 2008, American Tower Corporation (the "Company") issued a press release announcing that it had completed its previously announced redemption of all of its outstanding 3.25% convertible notes due August 1, 2010 (the "Notes"). In accordance with the conversion provisions of the Notes and the Indenture dated as of August 4, 2003 between the Company and The Bank of New York Trust Company, N.A., as trustee, holders of the Notes had the right at any time until and including, but not after the close of business on August 5, 2008, to convert their Notes into shares of the Company's Class A common stock at a conversion rate of 81.808 shares of Class A common stock per $1,000 principal amount of Notes. Holders of all $4.7 million of the outstanding Notes converted their Notes into an aggregate of 384,332 shares of the Company's Class A common stock prior to redemption. As a result, as of August 6, 2008, none of the Notes remained outstanding.
A copy of the press release announcing the redemption of the Notes is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference.
