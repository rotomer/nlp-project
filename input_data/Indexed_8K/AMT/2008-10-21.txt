Between July 1, 2008 and October 21, 2008, American Tower Corporation (the "Company") issued an aggregate of 7,981,188 shares of its Class A common stock (the "Common Stock") upon conversion of convertible notes into shares of the Company's Common Stock and upon exercise of warrants to purchase shares of the Company's Common Stock, as follows:
 
 
 
 
No underwriters were engaged in connection with the above issuances of Common Stock by the Company. The shares of Common Stock issued upon conversion of the Company's convertible notes were issued in reliance on the exemption from registration set forth in Section 3(a)(9) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. The shares of Common Stock issued upon exercise of the Company's warrants were issued in reliance on the exemption from registration set forth in Sections 3(a)(9) and 3(a)(10) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, and in the case of the warrants assumed in the merger with SpectraSite, Inc., Section 1145 of the United States Code.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
