On September 23, 2010, American Tower Corporation's directly held, wholly owned subsidiary, American Towers, Inc., completed the redemption of the principal amount of its outstanding 7.25% senior subordinated notes due 2011. In accordance with the redemption provisions of the notes and the indenture dated as of November 18, 2003 between American Towers, Inc., as issuer, and The Bank of New York Mellon Trust Company, N.A. (formerly known as The Bank of New York Trust Company, N.A.), as trustee, the notes were redeemed at a price equal to 100.00% of the principal amount, plus accrued and unpaid interest on the notes up to, but excluding, September 23, 2010.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
