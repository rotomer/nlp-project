On November 13, 2008, the Board of Directors of Amazon.com, Inc. (the "Company") elected Alain Moni as a director of the Company, effective on that date, and also appointed him to the Leadership Development and Compensation Committee of the Board, effective January 1, 2009. Mr. Moni is President and Chief Operating Officer of Ingram Micro Inc. and a director of Jones Lang LaSalle Incorporated. In connection with his election, Mr. Moni was granted a restricted stock unit award under the Company's 1997 Stock Incentive Plan for 7,000 shares of common stock of the Company, to vest in three equal installments on the first, second and third anniversaries of his initial election assuming continued service as a director. Mr. Moni has also entered into an indemnification agreement with the Company in the same form its other directors have entered, which is filed as an exhibit to Amendment No. 1, filed April 21, 1997, to the Company's Registration Statement on Form S-1 (Registration No. 333-23795).
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
