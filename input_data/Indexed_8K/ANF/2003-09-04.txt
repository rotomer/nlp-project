Exhibit 99 ABERCROMBIE & FITCH REPORTS AUGUST SALES RESULTS NEW ALBANY, Ohio / September 4, 2003 - Abercrombie & Fitch (NYSE: ANF) today reported net sales of $190.4 million for the four-week period ended August 30, 2003, a 3% increase over last year's August sales of $185.2 million. August comparable store sales decreased 11% compared with the four-week period ending August 31, 2002. Year-to-date, the Company reported a net sales increase of 8% to $892.8 million from $827.1 million last year. Comparable store sales decreased 8% for the year-to-date period. Abercrombie & Fitch operated a total of 628 stores at the end of August, including 167 abercrombie stores and 113 Hollister Co. stores. The Company operates e-commerce websites at www.abercrombie.com, www.abercrombiekids.com, www.hollisterco.com and publishes the A&F Quarterly.
