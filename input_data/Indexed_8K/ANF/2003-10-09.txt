EXHIBIT 99 ABERCROMBIE & FITCH REPORTS SEPTEMBER SALES RESULTS NEW ALBANY, Ohio / October 9, 2003 - Abercrombie & Fitch (NYSE: ANF) today reported net sales of $149.6 million for the five-week period ended October 4, 2003, a 16% increase over last year's September sales of $129.3 million. September comparable store sales decreased 1% compared with the five-week period ended October 5, 2002. Year-to-date, the Company reported a net sales increase of 9% to $1.042 billion from $956.5 million last year. Comparable store sales decreased 7% for the year-to-date period. Abercrombie & Fitch operated a total of 641 stores at the end of September, including 169 Abercrombie stores and 121 Hollister Co. stores. The Company operates e-commerce websites at www.abercrombie.com, www.abercrombiekids.com, www.hollisterco.com and publishes the A&F Quarterly.
