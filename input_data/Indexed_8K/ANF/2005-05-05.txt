Exhibit 99.1 ABERCROMBIE & FITCH REPORTS APRIL SALES INCREASE OF 31%; COMPARABLE STORE SALES INCREASE OF 16% NEW ALBANY, OHIO, MAY 5, 2005: Abercrombie & Fitch Co. (NYSE: ANF) today reported net sales of $159.4 million for the four-week period ended April 30, 2005, a 31% increase over last year's net sales of $121.7 million for the four-week period ended May 1, 2004. April comparable store sales increased 16% over the same period last year. Year-to-date, the Company reported a net sales increase of 33% to $546.8 million from $411.9 million last year. Comparable store sales increased 19% for the year-to-date period. APRIL 2005 HIGHLIGHTS o Total Company net sales increased 31% o Total Company comparable store sales increased 16% o Abercrombie & Fitch comparable store sales increased 14% o Hollister Co. comparable store sales increased 16% o abercrombie comparable store sales increased 31% The Company operated 351 Abercrombie & Fitch stores, 167 abercrombie stores, 260 Hollister Co. stores and 5 RUEHL stores at the end of fiscal April. The Company operates e-commerce websites at www.abercrombie.com, www.abercrombiekids.com, and www.hollisterco.com.
