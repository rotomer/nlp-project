Exhibit 99.1
Abercrombie & Fitch (NYSE: ANF) today reported net sales of $251.1 million for the four-week period ended November 3, 2007, a 16% increase over net sales of $216.8 million for the four-week period ended October 28, 2006. October comparable store sales decreased 2% for the four-week period ended November 3, 2007, compared to the four-week period ended November 4, 2006. Total Company direct-to-consumer net sales increased 50% to $18.5 million for the four-week period ended November 3, 2007, compared to the four-week period ended October 28, 2006.
New Albany, Ohio, November 8, 2007:
Year-to-date, the Company reported a net sales increase of 16% to $2.521 billion from $2.179 billion last year. Comparable store sales decreased 1% for the year-to-date period. Year-to-date, the Company reported direct-to-consumer net sales increased 51% to $150.3 million.
October 2007 Developments
The Company operated a total of 358 Abercrombie & Fitch stores, 198 abercrombie stores, 431 Hollister Co. stores and 20 RUEHL stores in the United States at the end of fiscal October. The Company operates three Abercrombie & Fitch stores and three Hollister Co. stores in Canada, and one Abercrombie & Fitch store in London, England. The Company operates e-commerce websites at www.abercrombie.com, www.abercrombiekids.com, www.hollisterco.com and www.RUEHL.com.
