 Foundation Coal Holdings, Inc. (NYSE:FCL) today announced that it has elected Stephen Ko to its board of directors. Ko replaces Joshua Astrof, who resigned from the board.
Linthicum Heights, Maryland, June 3, 2005
 
According to Foundation president and chief executive officer, James F. Roberts, "Stephen has been involved with Foundation Coal through the acquisition and initial public offering of the company in 2004. His knowledge and background make him a welcome addition to the board."
 
Since 2002, Ko has been an associate in the Private Equity Group of The Blackstone Group L.P., an investment and advisory firm. His prior experience includes positions at Clayton, Dubilier & Rice, Inc., a private equity firm, and the Investment Banking Division of Goldman, Sachs & Co. Ko currently serves as a member of the Advisory Committee of Graham Packaging Holdings Company.
