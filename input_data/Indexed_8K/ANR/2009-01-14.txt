The maximum level of performance at which EBITDA Restricted Stock Units can be earned is one hundred percent (100%) of Actual EBITDA. Below the minimum level of performance, zero percent (0%) EBITDA Restricted Stock Units can be earned. EBITDA Restricted Stock Units will be straight line interpolated for performance falling between target and minimum levels.
