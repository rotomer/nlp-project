Item 1.04 Mine Safety  Reporting of Shutdowns and Patterns of Violation
On July 25, 2012, Alpha Coal West, Inc., a subsidiary of Alpha Natural Resources, Inc., received an imminent danger order issued under section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 alleging that a portion of the highwall had failed in one area of the Eagle Butte Mine (the Mine), located near Gillette, Wyoming, requiring withdrawal of the miners from the affected area of the Mine to a perimeter established by berms.  No injuries occurred as a result of the cited condition.
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
