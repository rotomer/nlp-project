ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS An international arbitrator hearing the claims made by Texaco China, B.V., against our subsidiaries Apache China Corporation LDC and Apache Bohai Corporation LDC, involving a 1998 farmout in Bohai Bay, People's Republic of China, issued an award against Apache China and Apache Bohai and in favor of Texaco China in the amount of $71 million, which includes interest. Apache believes that the finding of the arbitrator is unsupported by the facts and the law, and Apache expects to pursue an appeal of the award in federal court.
