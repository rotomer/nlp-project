On February 14, 2011, Apache Corporation (the "Company") filed a Current Report on Form 8-K under Item 5.02 disclosing that Messrs. Scott Josey and Chansoo Joung were elected to the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board"). At the time of filing, the Board had not yet determined the committee on which Messrs. Josey and Joung would serve.
The Company is filing this Current Report on Form 8-K/A to disclose that, on May 5, 2011 and effective on that date, the Board appointed Mr. Josey to the Executive Committee of the Company's Board and Mr. Joung to the Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee of the Company's Board.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
