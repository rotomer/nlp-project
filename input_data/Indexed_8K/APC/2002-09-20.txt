ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS. Anadarko Petroleum Corporation (the "Company") entered into a Terms Agreement dated September 17, 2002 with Deutsche Bank Securities Inc., Credit Suisse First Boston Corporation, JP Morgan Securities Inc., Salomon Smith Barney Inc., ABN AMRO Incorporated, BMO Nesbitt Burns Corp., Fleet Securities, Inc., The Royal Bank of Scotland plc and SunTrust Capital Markets, Inc., pursuant to which the Company issued $300 million aggregate principal amount of the Company's 5% Notes due October 1, 2012. The net proceeds from the Notes will be approximately $295 million, and will be used to pay down floating-rate debt.
