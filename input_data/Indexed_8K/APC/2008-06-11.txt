Anadarko Petroleum Corporation (the "Company") filed its Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2007 (the "2007 Form 10-K") on February 29, 2008. As a participant in the Securities and Exchange Commission's voluntary eXtensible Business Reporting Language (XBRL) program, the Company has attached as Exhibit 100 to this Current Report on Form 8-K the complete set of financial statements and the supplemental information on oil and gas exploration and production activities, excluding the notes to the financial statements, from the 2007 Form 10-K formatted in XBRL.
Pursuant to Rule 401 of Regulation S-T, users of this data are advised that the financial information contained in the XBRL-related documents is unaudited and that these are not the official publicly filed financial statements of the Company. The purpose of submitting these XBRL-related documents is to test the related format and technology. As a result, investors should continue to rely on the official filed version of the furnished documents and not rely on the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibit 100, in making investment decisions.
In accordance with Rule 402 of Regulation S-T, the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibit 100, shall not be deemed to be "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), or otherwise subject to the liability of that section, and shall not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document filed under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing.
