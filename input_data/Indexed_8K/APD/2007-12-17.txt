Item 8.01 Other Events.
The 2007 proxy statement of Air Products and Chemicals, Inc. (the "Company"), sent to shareholders on or about December 14, 2007 (the "Proxy Statement"), contained an error relating to the number of shares entitled to vote at the Company's Annual Meeting of Shareholders on January 24, 2008. The answer to the third question on page 1 of the proxy statement should have read in its entirety as follows: "As of the Record Date, which was November 30, 2007, 214,616,681 shares of Company common stock were issued and outstanding, which are the only shares entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting. Every owner of Company common stock is entitled to one vote for each share owned."
