Item 8.01. Other Events.
        On January 21, 2004, Amphenol Corporation (the "Company") announced that it would effect a 2-for-1 stock split of its Class A Common Stock for shareholders of record as of March 17, 2004. The additional shares were distributed on or about March 29, 2004.
        As a result of the stock split, the number of shares of Class A Common Stock that remain available for issuance under the following Registration Statements on Form S-8, which relate to the Company's equity-based compensation plans, will increase by one hundred percent: 333-86618 and 333-35901 and the number of shares of Class A Common Stock that remain available for sale on March 17, 2004 (the record date for the stock split) under the following Registration Statement on Form S-3, which relates to sales of Class A Common Stock by our shareholders, will increase by one hundred percent: 333-111687.
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
