Item 5.02.  Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On October 19, 2004, the Board of Directors of Amphenol Corporation (the "Company") appointed Diana G. Reardon, 45, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company.  Ms. Reardon formerly served as Vice President of the Company since January 2004, Treasurer of the Company since 1992 and Controller of the Company since 1994.  Ms. Reardon has been an employee of the Company since 1988.  She does not serve on the board of directors of any public companies.  Ms. Reardon replaces Edward G. Jepsen, who has announced his retirement from the position of Chief Financial Officer of the Company, effective October 19, 2004.  Mr. Jepsen will continue in his position as Executive Vice President of the Company.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
