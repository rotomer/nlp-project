Item 1.01.  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
 
On November 10, 2004, Amphenol Corporation (the "Company") entered into Amendment No. 2 to the Credit Agreement (the "Amendment"), dated as of November 10, 2004, among the Company, the Lenders listed therein, Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as Administrative Agent, and the Subsidiary Guarantors party thereto.  The Amendment, among other things, (i) reduces the LIBOR margin on the Tranche B term loan to 150 basis points, (ii) permits up to an additional $250,000,000 in term loans to be incurred under the Credit Agreement and (iii) amends certain negative covenants.
 
A copy of the Amendment is attached as Exhibit 10.1 hereto and is incorporated in this Item 1.01 by reference.
