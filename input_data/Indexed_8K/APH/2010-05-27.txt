On May 25, 2010, Amphenol Corporation (the "Company") entered into an amendment agreement (the "Amendment Agreement") with Amphenol Funding Corp., a Delaware corporation, Atlantic Asset Securitization LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, and Crdit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank New York Branch, f/k/a Calyon New York Branch, a French banking corporation, duly licensed under the laws of the State of New York ("Crdit Agricole"), which amends that certain Receivables Purchase Agreement, dated as of July 31, 2006 (the "Receivables Purchase Agreement"), among Amphenol Funding Corp., the Company, Atlantic Asset Securitization LLC and Crdit Agricole.  Pursuant to the Amendment Agreement, Crdit Agricole extended its commitment in its capacity as a Related Committed Purchaser (as defined in the Receivables Purchase Agreement) under the Receivables Purchase Agreement to May 24, 2011.
 
The above descriptions of the Amendment Agreement and Receivables Purchase Agreement are qualified in their entirety by reference to the Amendment Agreement and Receivables Purchase Agreement, which are filed as Exhibits 10.1 and 10.2, respectively, to this Current Report on Form 8-K, and are incorporated by reference herein.
