Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
On December 1, 2005, Apollo Group, Inc. appointed Mr. Brian Mueller to serve as its Chief Operating Officer. Mr. Mueller joined Apollo Group, Inc. in 1987. He most recently held the position of Chief Executive Officer of University of Phoenixs online campus from March 2002 to present. Mr. Mueller held the position of Chief Operating Officer and Senior Vice President of University of Phoenixs online campus from May 1997 to March 2002.
