Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
Apollo Group, Inc. is filing this amendment to its Current Report on Form 8-K filed on June 26, 2006, which reported that the Board of Directors of Apollo Group, Inc. elected Mr. George Zimmer and Mr. Daniel Diethelm to serve as Directors. Mr. Diethelm and Ms. Hedy F. Govenar have been appointed to serve on a Special Committee of the Board of Directors, which has been established to oversee the previously announced review of the Companys practices related to stock option grants.
