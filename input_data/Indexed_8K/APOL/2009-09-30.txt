Item 8.01 Other Events.
     On September 30, 2009, University of Phoenix, a subsidiary of Apollo Group, Inc., confirmed that it has entered into settlement discussions with plaintiffs to resolve the False Claims Act case, pending in the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of California. There is no assurance that a settlement will be reached. Pending the outcome of these discussions, University of Phoenix and the plaintiffs in this matter on September 30, 2009 jointly requested a stay of all litigation proceedings for 45 days. The court has not yet ruled on the request to stay.
