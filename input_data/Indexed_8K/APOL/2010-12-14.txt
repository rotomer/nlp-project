     On December 9, 2010, James R. Reis, a member of the Apollo Group, Inc. Board of Directors since January 2007, informed the Board of Directors that he has decided not to stand for reelection as a director when his term expires at the next annual meeting of our Class B shareholders. Mr. Reis stated that there were no disagreements with Apollo Group's operations, policies or practices that led to his decision.
     We are grateful to Mr. Reis for his diligent service and valuable contributions to the company during his tenure as a director.
