Item 8.01. Other Events.
     On December 12, 2008, the Board of Directors of Allegheny Technologies Incorporated (the "Company") approved a voluntary contribution of shares of the Company's common stock to the Company's U.S. defined benefit pension plan (the "Pension Plan") in the amount of $30 million in market value or 1.5 million shares, whichever is greater in value. The Board of Directors also had previously approved a voluntary contribution to the Pension Plan of approximately $30 million in cash. The Company intends for both contributions to be made in the fourth quarter of 2008.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Dated: December 18, 2008
