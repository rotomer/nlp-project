On September 9, 2011, the Board of Directors of Allegheny Technologies Incorporated (the "Company") established a Lead Independent Director position and designated Diane C. Creel to serve as Lead Independent Director. In her role as Lead Independent Director, Ms. Creel will, among other responsibilities, preside at executive sessions of the independent directors and serve as a point of contact for Company stockholders wishing to communicate with the Company's independent directors. Ms. Creel has served as a director of the Company since 1996. She is the Chair of the Nominating and Governance Committee and a member of the Finance Committee and the Personnel and Compensation Committee.
