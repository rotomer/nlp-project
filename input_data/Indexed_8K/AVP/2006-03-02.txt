ITEM: Other Events
Item 8.01 Other Events.
On March 2, 2006, Avon Products (China) Co. Ltd, a subsidiary of Avon Products, Inc. ("Avon China"), announced that it had received notification from China's Ministry of Commerce on being granted a direct selling business license in China. As a result of receiving the license, Avon China will now roll-out its previously tested single-level direct selling model nationwide.
 
     
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: March 2, 2006
