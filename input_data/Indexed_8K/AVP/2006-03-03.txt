Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
On February 28, 2006, Avon Products, Inc. (the "Company") entered into an Amendment to the Avon Products, Inc. Restricted Stock Unit Award Agreement dated March 11, 2004 of Robert J. Corti (the "Amendment"). The Amendment provides for the immediate vesting of Mr. Corti's outstanding restricted stock units, effective as of February 28, 2006, in connection with Mr. Corti's retirement from the Company. Reference is made to the complete text of the Amendment, which is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1
