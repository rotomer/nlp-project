Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
This Form 8-K/A amends the Form 8-K dated March 23, 2005 filed by Baxter International Inc. (the "Company"), which announced the elections effective March 21, 2005 and March 30, 2005, respectively, of Blake E. Devitt and Peter S. Hellman to the Board of Directors of the Company. At that time, it had not been determined on which committees of the Board Messrs. Devitt and Hellman would serve.
 
On July 26, 2005, the Board of Directors of the Company appointed Mr. Devitt to the Corporate Governance and Audit Committees and Mr. Hellman to the Finance and Audit Committees.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
 
 
Date: July 27, 2005
