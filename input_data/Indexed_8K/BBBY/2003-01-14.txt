ITEM 9. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE.
          On January 14, 2003, Bed Bath & Beyond Inc. filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended November 30, 2002. The certification by each of the Co-Principal Executive Officers and the Principal Financial Officer required pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes  Oxley Act accompanied such Quarterly Report.
          Copies of these certifications are attached hereto as Exhibits 99.1, 99.2 and 99.3.
 
          Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 

 
Exhibit 99.1
