ITEM 8.01   Other Events
     On October 19, 2005, BB&T Corporation ("the Company") entered into an Accelerated Share Repurchase Agreement with Bear, Stearns International Limited to facilitate the Company's purchase of 6,000,000 shares of its outstanding common stock. The initial purchase price of the shares was $40.76. The transaction was settled as planned on October 24, 2005.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
