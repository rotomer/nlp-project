ITEM 8.01   Other Events
     On September 13, 2006, Branch Banking and Trust Company ("Branch Bank"), a wholly owned subsidiary of BB&T Corporation (the "Registrant"), issued (i) $400 million aggregate principal amount of Fixed Rate Subordinated Notes due September 2016 and (ii) $350 million aggregate principal amount of Floating Rate Subordinated Notes due September 2016. The notes were issued pursuant to a Bank Note Program in which Branch Bank and certain of its affiliates may issue senior bank notes with maturities of more than one year from date of issue, senior bank notes with maturities ranging from 7 days to one year from date of issue and subordinated bank notes with maturities from 5 years to 30 years from date of issue.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date:       September 13, 2006
