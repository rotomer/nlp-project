ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS.
 
We announced in a press release on January 9, 2003, that we expect to realign our Musicland business into our domestic Best Buy stores operations.  As a result, the position of Musicland president has been eliminated, and Kevin Freeland, the former president of Musicland, has left the Company.
