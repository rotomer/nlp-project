Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure
.
 
Pursuant to Item 7.01, "Regulation FD Disclosure," information is being furnished with respect to a broadcast interview conducted by CNBC on October 20, 2004, with the registrant's Vice Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Brad Anderson. In the interview, Mr. Anderson misspoke regarding the registrant's forecasted revenue growth. The registrant continues to project, as previously disclosed, revenue for fiscal 2005 to increase by 11% to 13% from fiscal 2004 revenue. The registrant has not completed its revenue forecast for the next several years.  The registrant will provide its projected revenue growth rate for fiscal 2006 during its fourth-quarter fiscal 2005 earnings conference call scheduled for March 30, 2005.
 
Best Buy Co., Inc.'s Annual Report to Shareholders and its reports on Forms 10-K, 10-Q and 8-K and other publicly available information should be consulted for other important information about the registrant.
 
The information in this Current Report on Form 8-K,  hereto, shall not be deemed "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to liability of that section. The information in this Current Report shall not be incorporated by reference into any filing or other document pursuant to the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing or document.
