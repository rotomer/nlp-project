Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
(b) On May 7, 2007, Ari Bousbib notified Best Buy Co., Inc. that, for personal reasons, he will not stand for ratification at the registrant's 2007 Regular Meeting of Shareholders scheduled for June 27, 2007.  Mr. Bousbib intends to serve the remainder of his appointment as a director through the date of the 2007 Regular Meeting of Shareholders.
Mr. Bousbib has served as a director since August 2006.  He currently serves as a member of the Audit Committee of the registrant's Board of Directors.  Mr. Bousbib said he had no disagreements with the registrant.
Best Buy Co., Inc.'s Annual Report to Shareholders and its reports on Forms 10-K, 10-Q and 8-K and other publicly available information should be consulted for other important information about the registrant.
