On April 18, 2012, consistent with Best Buy Co., Inc.'s (the registrant's) director retirement policy within its Corporate Governance Principles, Mr. Rogelio Rebolledo tendered to the Board of Directors of the registrant, and the Board of Directors accepted, his letter stating that he would be retiring as a director of the Board at the end of his term on June 20, 2012, and would not stand for re-election at the Regular Meeting of Shareholders on June 21, 2012.
Mr. Rebolledo is a Class 1 Director. Mr. Rebolledo has served as a director since August 2006, and is a member of the Global Strategy Committee and the Nominating, Corporate Governance and Public Policy Committee.
Mr. Rebolledo did not indicate that he had any disagreements with the registrant.
