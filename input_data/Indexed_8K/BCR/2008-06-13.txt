On June 12, 2008, C. R. Bard, Inc. (the "Company") determined that it will discontinue the sale of its Salute II hernia fixation device. In connection with this event, the Company will record a non-cash charge anticipated to be in the range of $40 million to $45 million pre-tax in the second quarter of 2008 relating primarily to intellectual property, inventory and fixed assets. The final determination of the amount of the charge will be made in connection with the preparation of the Company's second quarter financial statements.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
