C. R. Bard, Inc. (the "Company") entered into a settlement agreement effective December 31, 2009 with one of its insurance carriers with respect to the Company's hernia products claims pursuant to which the Company secured a specified coverage commitment in future periods. In connection with this event, the Company determined that it will record a non-cash charge of $25 million pre-tax in the fourth quarter of 2009 relating to the write-off of a related insurance receivable.
