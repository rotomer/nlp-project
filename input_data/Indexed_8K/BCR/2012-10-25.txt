C. R. Bard, Inc. today announced that it has priced a public offering of 5-year senior notes in an aggregate principal amount of $500 million and with an interest rate of 1.375 percent per year. Bard intends to use the net proceeds from this offering for general corporate purposes, including repayment of commercial paper and acquisitions.
Merrill Lynch, Pierce, Fenner & Smith Incorporated, Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Wells Fargo Securities, LLC are joint book-running managers for this offering, which is expected to close on October 30, 2012.
