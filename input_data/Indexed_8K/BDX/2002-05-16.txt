Item 9. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE The following statement is being furnished by the Company under the provisions of Regulation FD: On May 15, 2002, Deborah J. Neff, President BD Biosciences of Becton, Dickinson and Company ("BD") exercised options to purchase 7,600 shares of BD. These options were scheduled to expire on May 17, 2002. Ms. Neff sold 3,900 shares of BD on May 15 to cover costs and tax obligations related to the stock option exercise. As a result of these transactions, Ms. Neff's direct ownership of shares of BD increased by 2,700 shares to 7,205 shares. Ms. Neff also has ownership interests in 3,091 shares in BD's Share Incentive Plan and 1,959 shares in BD's Deferred Compensation Plan. These transactions were reported on Forms 4 and Forms 144 dated May 15, 2002.
