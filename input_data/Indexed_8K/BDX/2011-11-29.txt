ITEM 5.02 DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR CERTAIN OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICERS; COMPENSATORY ARRANGEMENTS OF CERTAIN OFFICERS
Becton, Dickinson and Company ("BD") has elected Suketu Upadhyay, 42, as Senior Vice President and Controller, effective November 28, 2011. Mr. Upadhyay will serve as BD's principal accounting officer, succeeding William A. Tozzi, who has assumed another position within BD. Mr. Upadhyay has served as BD's Vice President of Finance  International since August 2010. Prior to joining BD in 2010, Mr. Upadhyay was employed with AstraZeneca, serving as the Global Head of R&D Finance, and also served in various leadership roles in commercial finance. Prior to that, Mr. Upadhyay was Head of Finance for Centocor, Inc., a former subsidiary of Johnson & Johnson.
