On February 22, 2012, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of Beam Inc. (the "Company") reluctantly accepted Pierre E. Leroy's resignation from the Board, effective immediately. In accordance with the Company's Corporate Governance Principles, on February 16, 2012 Mr. Leroy tendered his resignation from the Board due to a change in employment status after accepting the position of chief executive officer for a public safety products company. In connection with Mr. Leroy's resignation, the Board decreased the number of directors of the Company from nine to eight.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
