Item 5.02
            
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On October 16, 2006, the Board of Directors of Franklin Resources, Inc. (the "Company") approved the appointment of Kenneth A. Lewis as the Company's Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer, effective October 20, 2006. Upon the effectiveness of Mr. Lewis's appointment, he will be the Company's principal financial officer and principal accounting officer.
 
As previously reported in the Company's Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on September 12, 2006 (the "Prior Form 8-K"), James R. Baio, the Company's Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer, notified the Company on September 6, 2006 that he will end his employment on October 20, 2006.
 
Information with respect to Mr. Baio's resignation and with respect to Mr. Lewis was previously reported in the Prior Form 8-K, which is incorporated herein by reference.
