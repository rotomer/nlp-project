Item 8.01 Other Events.
On November 4, 2005, Mr. Alan R. Crain, Jr., Vice President and General Counsel of Baker Hughes Incorporated (the "Company"), entered into a Sales Plan with Morgan Stanley DW Inc. ("Sales Plan"). This Sales Plan is intended to comply with the requirements of Rule 10b5-1 promulgated by the Securities and Exchange Commission under Section 10(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. The Sales Plan covers the potential exercise of options and associated sale of up to 71,958 shares of the Company's Common Stock during the period commencing on December 6, 2005 and ending on March 3, 2007, unless terminated earlier under certain circumstances.
