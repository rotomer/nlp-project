Item 8.01 Other Events.
On March 10, 2006, Mr. Alan R. Crain, Jr., Vice President and General Counsel of Baker Hughes Incorporated (the "Company"), entered into a Sales Plan with Morgan Stanley DW Inc. ("Sales Plan"). This Sales Plan is intended to comply with the requirements of Rule 10b5-1 promulgated by the Securities and Exchange Commission under Section 10(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. The Sales Plan covers the potential sale of up to 8,544 shares of the Company's Common Stock during the period commencing on May 3, 2006 and ending on May 1, 2008, unless terminated earlier under certain circumstances.
