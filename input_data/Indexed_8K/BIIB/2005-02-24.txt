2005 Performance Goals
     On February 17, 2005, Registrant's Board of Directors, upon recommendation of the Compensation Committee, approved the 2005 corporate performance goals applicable to all of the Registrant's executive officers and other employees eligible to receive cash bonuses. The Registrant's Board of Directors also approved the individual performance goals and bonus targets of Dr. Rastetter and Mr. Mullen.
