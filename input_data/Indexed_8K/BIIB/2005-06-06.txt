Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
Change in Directors' Fees
     On June 2, 2005, the Compensation and Management Development Committee of the Board of Directors of Biogen Idec Inc. (the "Company") approved a decrease in the amount of fees that members of the Board of Directors will receive for participation in telephonic meetings of the Board of Directors from $2,500 to $1,250.
Approval of Equity Plans
     On June 3, 2005, at the Company's Annual Meeting of Stockholders, the Company's stockholders approved the Company's 2005 Omnibus Equity Plan (the "2005 Plan") and the amendment and restatement of the Company's 1995 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (the "ESPP"), including an increase in the number of shares available for issuance under the ESPP from 4,170,000 to 6,170,000 shares.
     A description of the material terms and conditions of the 2005 Plan and the ESPP is included in the Company's Definitive Proxy Statement on Schedule 14A for the Annual Meeting filed on April 15, 2005.
