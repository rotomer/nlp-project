Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
On June 7, 2010, Biogen Idec Inc. announced that Robert A. Hamm would retire as Chief Operating Officer on December 31, 2010. On August 6, 2010, Biogen Idec Inc. disclosed that as part of the transition process many of the duties and responsibilities Mr. Hamm has been performing as Chief Operating Officer have been assigned to other officers. Mr. Hamm will remain available to assist in an orderly transition until his previously disclosed retirement date of December 31, 2010.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: August 6, 2010
