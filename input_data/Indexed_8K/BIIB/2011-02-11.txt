Item 8.01 Other Events
On February 11, 2011, Biogen Idec Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has authorized the repurchase of up to 20 million shares of its common stock. The company expects to use this repurchase program principally to offset common stock issuances under its share-based compensation plans. This repurchase program does not have an expiration date.
