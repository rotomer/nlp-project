On May 16, 2011, Linda Gosden Robinson was appointed Senior Managing Director and Global Head of Marketing and Communications of BlackRock, Inc. (BlackRock or the Company).  Her appointment is part of the Companys ongoing effort to build BlackRocks global brand and further strengthen its marketing and communications efforts.
In connection with her appointment, Ms. Robinson has resigned from BlackRocks Board of Directors and withdrawn her name from nomination for reelection at the Company's annual meeting of stockholders, to be held on May 25, 2011.
