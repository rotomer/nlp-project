Bemis Company, Inc. Receives Subpoena from the U.S. Department of Justice
 
                On August 15, 2003, Bemis Company, Inc. received a subpoena from the U.S. Department of Justice in connection with the Department's criminal investigation into competitive practices in the label stock industry.
 
                In a press release dated April 15, 2003, and in the most recent Form 10-Q for the three months ended June 30, 2003, we indicated that we had received notice from the U.S. Department of Justice of its intent to initiate a criminal investigation into competitive practices in the label stock industry and that related subpoenas would be forthcoming.  We plan to fully cooperate with the Department's investigation.
 
 
 
 
 
                Pursuant to the requirements of Section 13 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned, thereunto duly authorized.
