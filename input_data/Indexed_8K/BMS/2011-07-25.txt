Item 5.02               Departure of Directors or Certain Officers.
 
On July 22, 2011, Bemis Company, Inc. (the "Company") announced that, Gene C. Wulf, Executive Vice President and a member of the Board of Directors of the Company, notified the Board of Directors of his decision to retire as a member of the Board of Directors and officer and employee of the Company effective at the close of business on November 30, 2011.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned, thereunto duly authorized.
