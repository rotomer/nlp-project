     On December 24, 2002, Broadcom Corporation and its wholly owned subsidiary Broadcom (BVI) Limited purchased approximately 150 domestic and foreign patents and patent applications from UNOVA, Inc. for $24 million in cash. Broadcom and its subsidiary funded the transaction from cash on hand.
     The patents purchased relate to wireless local-area networking and wireless communications including dual-radio access points and hierarchical networks. In addition, Broadcom purchased UNOVA's patents for dynamically switchable power supplies and personal video recorders.
