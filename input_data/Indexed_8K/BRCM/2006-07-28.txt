Item 8.01 Other Events.
On July 24, 2006 Broadcom Corporation decided to suspend purchasing shares of its Class A common stock under the share repurchase program that it adopted in February 2005 and amended in January 2006. The suspension will be in effect until after the company has filed its restated financial statements and amended periodic reports with the Commission.
