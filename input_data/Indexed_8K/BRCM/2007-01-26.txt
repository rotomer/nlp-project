EXHIBIT 99.1
NEWS RELEASE
IRVINE, Calif.  January 24, 2007  Broadcom Corporation (Nasdaq: BRCM) today announced that the Nasdaq Listing Qualifications Panel has notified the company that it has regained compliance with the Nasdaq requirement relating to the filing of periodic reports with the SEC and has evidenced compliance with all other Nasdaq Marketplace Rules.  Accordingly, the Panel determined to continue the listing of Broadcom's Class A common stock on the Nasdaq Global Select Market, and the review of the company's listing status has been closed.
About Broadcom
Broadcom Corporation is a global leader in semiconductors for wired and wireless communications. Our products enable the delivery of voice, video, data and multimedia to and throughout the home, the office and the mobile environment. Broadcom provides the industry's broadest portfolio of state-of-the-art system-on-a-chip and software solutions to manufacturers of computing and networking equipment, digital entertainment and broadband access products, and mobile devices.
