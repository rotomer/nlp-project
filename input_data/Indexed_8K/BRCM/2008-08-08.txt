Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
(c) As previously disclosed, on June 16, 2008, the Board of Directors of Broadcom Corporation elected Robert L. Tirva as Vice President and Corporate Controller, and designated him as Principal Accounting Officer. In connection with Mr. Tirvas election to these positions, on August 5, 2008 the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors granted Mr. Tirva an award of restricted stock units ("RSUs") covering 25,000 shares of Broadcoms Class A common stock. The RSUs will vest, and the underlying shares will become issuable, upon Mr. Tirvas continuation in service through the end of the three year period measured from August 5, 2008. The RSUs will vest in whole or in part on an accelerated basis in the event Mr. Tirvas employment terminates under certain prescribed circumstances.
