On November 14, 2005, Boston Scientific Corporation launched a public offering of two series of its senior notes under the Companys existing shelf registration statement. The two series of senior notes will have varied maturities and a combined aggregate principal amount of $750 million. Boston Scientific plans to use the proceeds to repay commercial paper borrowings and for other general corporate purposes.
