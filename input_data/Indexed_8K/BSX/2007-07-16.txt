On July 13, 2007, Boston Scientific Corporation (the Company) announced that an agreement had been reached to settle claims associated with a series of product communications issued by Guidant Corporation in 2005 and 2006. Boston Scientific acquired Guidant Corporation last year.
Subject to certain conditions, the Company will pay a total of $195 million.  The agreement includes approximately 4,000 claims of individuals that have been consolidated in the U.S. District Court for the District of Minnesota in a Multi-District Litigation, as well as an undetermined number, but not all, of additional similar claims throughout the country.
A copy of the press release is incorporated herein as Exhibit 99.1.
