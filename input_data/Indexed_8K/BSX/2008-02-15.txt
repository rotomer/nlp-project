Our Compensation Committee has approved retention awards for certain members of our Executive Committee in order to address concerns about retaining members of our senior management team during a challenging but critical period for the Company.  The individuals were permitted to select the form of the award among a combination of stock options, deferred stock units (DSUs) and cash.  The awards were issued on February 12, 2008 once the selections were processed and reviewed by the Compensation Committee.  The stock options and DSUs are subject to the standard terms and conditions under our 2003 Long-Term Incentive Plan but will vest ratably over two years, and the cash will be paid ratably over two years.
