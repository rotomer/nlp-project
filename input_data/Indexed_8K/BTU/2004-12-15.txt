Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On December 9, 2004, the Peabody Energy Board of Directors approved an amendment to the Peabody Energy Corporation Deferred Compensation Plan ("the Plan"). The Plan was amended as a result of new tax legislation (the American Job Creation Act of 2004) to no longer allow employees to begin participation in the Plan and to no longer allow new deferral elections to be made under the Plan on or after the effective date of Section 409A of the Internal Revenue Code (December 31, 2004).
