Item 8.01. Other Events.
On March 7, 2012, Peabody Energy Corporation ("Peabody" or the "Company") announced a new global organizational structure, with the creation of geographic business units in the Americas, Australia and Asia to reflect the growing size and scope of Peabody's global footprint. The new organization positions the Company for maximum success amid unique geographic market conditions and opportunities, by committing dedicated business unit teams to manage all aspects of the platform including safety, operations, marketing, project delivery and support functions.
