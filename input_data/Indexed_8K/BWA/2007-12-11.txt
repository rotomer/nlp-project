Item 8.01.
Other Events
  
 
On December 11, 2007, BorgWarner Germany GmbH, an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of BorgWarner Inc., informed BERU Aktiengesellschaft, Ludwigsburg ("BERU AG") as well as the German regulator under Sec. 21 et. seq. of the German Securities Trading Act (Wertpapierhandelsgesetz) that the percentage of voting rights in BERU AG which is held by BorgWarner Germany GmbH, exceeded 75%.  In connection with reaching the 75% voting rights threshold BorgWarner Germany GmbH requested that BERU AG join BorgWarner in the preparation of a domination and profit transfer agreement between BorgWarner Germany GmbH as controlling party and BERU AG as controlled party under the German Stock Corporation Act (Aktiengesetz).
