Item 5.02  Departure of Directors or Certain Officers
On March 22, 2010, Jeffrey L. Obermayer, Vice President and Controller of BorgWarner Inc. (the Company), resigned his positions with the Company, effective April 1, 2010, in order to pursue another opportunity.
Effective April 1, 2010, Ronald T. Hundzinski will assume the position of the Companys Vice President and Controller (Principal Accounting Officer).
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
