EXHIBIT 99.1 [CITIGROUP LOGO] CITIGROUP PURCHASES 5.6 MILLION SHARES FROM CHAIRMAN SANDY WEILL WEILL CONTINUES TO OWN 16.8 MILLION SHARES New York, NY, October 2, 2003 - Citigroup announced today that it had purchased approximately 5.6 million shares of Citigroup common stock from Mr. Weill at a price of $47.14, which was determined to be the lower of the day's volume weighted average price and the day's closing price. Mr. Weill decided to sell the stock in order to facilitate his financial planning in light of his new stage. An exception to the company's policy on purchasing shares from its employees was approved by the Board.
