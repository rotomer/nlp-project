Citigroup Inc. Current Report on Form 8-K Item 5. Other Events. On July 15, 2004, Citigroup Inc. announced that that its subsidiary in Mexico, Banamex, has reached an agreement with the Mexican financial authorities that will bring to a close the long-standing issue related to the sale of loans to FOBAPROA in 1995-1996. As Citigroup had reserved for this matter, the resolution with the Mexican government will not have an impact on the company's consolidated financial results. (NYSE symbol: C) July 15, 2004 Citigroup Announces Banamex Agreement With Mexican Financial Authorities New York, NY, July 15, 2004 -- Citigroup announces that its subsidiary in Mexico, Banamex, has reached an agreement with the Mexican financial authorities that will bring to a close the long-standing issue related to the sale of loans to FOBAPROA in 1995-1996, at the height of the Mexican financial crisis.
