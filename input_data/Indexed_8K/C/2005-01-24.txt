Citigroup Inc. Current Report on Form 8-K ITEM 5.02 DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS. (b) On January 24, 2005, Citigroup Inc. announced that William P. Hannon, Citigroup Inc.'s current Controller and Chief Accounting Officer, will retire effective March 21, 2005. Following his retirement, Mr. Hannon has agreed to serve as a senior advisor to the company. (c) On January 24, 2005, Citigroup Inc. announced that John C. Gerspach, the current Chief Financial Officer and Chief Administrative Officer of Citigroup Latin America, will be appointed Controller and Chief Accounting Officer of Citigroup Inc. effective March 21, 2005. Mr. Gerspach will also join the Citigroup Management Committee. Mr. Gerspach, 51, has served as Chief Financial Officer of Citigroup Latin America since July 2003 and has served as Chief Administrative Officer of Citigroup Latin America since April 2002. Prior to that, Mr. Gerspach served as Chief Administrative Officer of Citigroup e-Business from February 2000 to April 2002.
