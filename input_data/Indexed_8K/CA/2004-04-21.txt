On April 21, 2004 Standard & Poor's Ratings Services announced that it lowered its corporate credit rating on Computer Associates International, Inc. to BBB-/Watch Neg/A-3 from BBB+/Watch Neg/A-2 and that the ratings remain on CreditWatch with negative implications.
