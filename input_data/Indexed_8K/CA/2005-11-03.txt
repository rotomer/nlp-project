Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
Effective October 28, 2005, Robert W. Davis, the Chief Financial Officer of Computer Associates International, Inc. (the "Company"), also began to serve as the principal accounting officer of the Company. Douglas E. Robinson, who had served as the principal accounting officer before this change, continues to serve as Senior Vice President in a new role as the head of Sales Finance for the Company's Americas region. Information about Mr. Davis can be found in the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on February 4, 2005 as supplemented by the biographical information about Mr. Davis provided in the Company's amended Annual Report on Form 10-K/A filed on October 19, 2005.
