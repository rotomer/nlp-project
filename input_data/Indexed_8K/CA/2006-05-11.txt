Exhibit 99.1
ISLANDIA, N.Y., May 9, 2006  CA today announced that it has completed the acquisition of Cybermation, a privately-held provider of enterprise workload automation solutions, for $75 million in an all-cash transaction.
Cybermation specializes in software and services that modernize traditional job scheduling solutions and simplify the management of complex IT infrastructures. The acquisition extends CA's industry leading workload automation portfolio, which helps customers unify and simplify their IT environments by automating the scheduling and deployment of workloads across mainframe and distributed systems.
Cybermation will become a part of CA's Enterprise Systems Management business unit. CA will market the Cybermation solutions as standalone products while integrating them into its larger workload automation solutions portfolio.
