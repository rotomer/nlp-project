On September 5, 2008, George H. Conrades, a director of Cardinal Health, Inc. (the "Company") since 1999, informed the Company that he will not stand for re-election when his term expires at the 2008 annual meeting of shareholders. Mr. Conrades will continue to serve as a director until the 2008 annual meeting. Mr. Conrades has indicated that his reasons for deciding not to stand for re-election are personal and that he has no disagreement with the Company on any matter relating to the Company's operations, policies or practices.
