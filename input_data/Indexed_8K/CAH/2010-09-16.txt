On September 15, 2010, Cardinal Health, Inc. ("Cardinal Health") sold the remaining 30.5 million shares of common stock of CareFusion Corporation ("CareFusion") that it had retained when it separated its clinical and medical products businesses through the distribution to shareholders of 81% of the then outstanding CareFusion common stock. The aggregate proceeds from this sale were $705.9 million. During the fiscal year ended June 30, 2010, Cardinal Health sold 10.9 million shares of CareFusion common stock for $270.7 million.
