COOPER CAMERON TO WEBCAST CONFERENCE PRESENTATION
HOUSTON (August 30, 2005)  Cooper Cameron Corporation Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer Sheldon R. Erikson will appear at the Lehman Brothers 2005 CEO Energy/Power Conference on Wednesday, September 7, 2005. Erikson's remarks will be available on a live webcast.
The conference presentation will be accessible through a link on Cooper Cameron's website, located at , and is scheduled to begin at 1:45 p.m. Eastern time (12:45 p.m. Central). The presentation will also be archived for replay through Cooper Cameron's website.
www.coopercameron.com
Cooper Cameron Corporation (NYSE: CAM) is a leading international manufacturer of oil and gas pressure control equipment, including valves, wellheads, controls, chokes, blowout preventers and assembled systems for oil and gas drilling, production and transmission used in onshore, offshore and subsea applications, and provides oil and gas separation, metering and flow measurement equipment. Cooper Cameron is also a leading manufacturer of centrifugal air compressors, integral and separable gas compressors and turbochargers.
