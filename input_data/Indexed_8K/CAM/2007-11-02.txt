Item 8.01 Other Events.
A Special Committee of the Board of Directors of Cameron International Corporation set the Record Date and Distribution Date for the previously announced two-for-one stock split as December 17 and December 28, 2007, respectively. The two-for-one stock split is subject to approval by the Companys stockholders of an increase in authorized shares from 150,000,000 to 400,000,000 at a Special Meeting on December 7, 2007.
