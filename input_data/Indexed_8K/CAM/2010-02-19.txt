John D. Carne was elected Executive Vice President and James E. Wright was elected Senior Vice President of Cameron International Corporation by the Companys Board of Directors, effective March 1, 2010.  Mr. Carne is also President of the Companys Drilling & Production Systems group and Mr. Wright is also President of its Valves & Measurement group.
 
 
The Board of Directors also amended the Companys Bylaws to adopt a majority vote standard for non-contested election of directors.  No other changes were made to the Bylaws. The full text of the First Amendment to the Bylaws is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference.
 
The Amendment to the Bylaws is effective as of February 18, 2010.
