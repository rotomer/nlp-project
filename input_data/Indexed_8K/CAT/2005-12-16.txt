Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
December 14, 2005
 
Mr. Gordon Parker, a valued member of the board of directors of Caterpillar Inc. since 1995, notified the board of directors at a meeting held on December 14, 2005, of his intention to retire from the Caterpillar board effective June 14, 2006.
