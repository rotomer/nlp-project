February 10, 2006
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
PEORIA, Ill. - Caterpillar Inc. (NYSE: CAT) will conduct its 2006 annual meeting of stockholders on Wednesday, June 14 at 1:30 p.m., Central Daylight Time, in Chicago, Illinois. Meeting details and the procedure for admission will be included in the company's 2006 annual meeting proxy statement, which will be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on or about April 17, 2006 and mailed at the end of April to stockholders of record as of April 17
th

About Caterpillar
 
For more than 80 years, Caterpillar Inc. has been building the world's infrastructure and, in partnership with its worldwide dealer network, is driving positive and sustainable change on every continent. With 2005 sales and revenues of $36.33 billion, Caterpillar is a technology leader and the world's leading manufacturer of construction and mining equipment, diesel and natural gas engines and industrial gas turbines. More information is available at http://www.cat.com .
