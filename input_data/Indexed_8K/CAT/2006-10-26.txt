Caterpillar Inc. (NYSE: CAT) will offer a listen-only audio webcast of its analyst meeting with financial analysts and institutional shareholders, beginning at 8:00 a.m. Eastern Standard Time on Friday, November 3, 2006. Caterpillar Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Jim Owens and other members of the Caterpillar executive team will give presentations on the companys machine and engine businesses, and an update on the execution of the corporate strategy and business goals.
Following the webcast, a PDF version of the days presentations and handout materials will be available at
www.cat.com/Investor
. A replay following the event will also be available from the web site.
Webcast access:
The company will offer a simultaneous real-time, listen-only webcast of the event. The approximately three-and-a-half-hour webcast begins at 8:00 a.m. Eastern Standard Time on Friday, November 3. The call can be accessed through
www.cat.com/Investor
. Listeners should go to the web site at least 15 minutes before the live event to download and install any necessary audio software.
For those unable to participate in the live broadcast, the replay will be available through
www.cat.com/Investor
shortly after the live event. There is no charge to access the webcast.
