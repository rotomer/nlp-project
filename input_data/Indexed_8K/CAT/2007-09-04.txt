ITEM: Other Events
 
 
Item 8.01. Other Events
 
Continuing our long-standing commitment of providing optimal value to our customers and shareholders, Caterpillar will announce to Caterpillar dealers worldwide a 2008 general price action of 0 to 5 percent for most machinery and 0 to 6 percent for most engines.  This price action is in line with expected general economic conditions and industry factors.  This price action will be effective January 2008 and includes adjustments to list prices and trade terms.
