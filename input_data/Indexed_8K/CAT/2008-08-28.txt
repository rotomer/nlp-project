Item  7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
During a press conference held Thursday, August 28, 2008 in Beijing, Peoples Republic of China, Caterpillar Inc. Chairman and Chief Executive Officer James W. Owens updated previously announced guidance for 2008 that total sales and revenues would be about $50 billion.  In response to questions received, Owens indicated that Caterpillar expects total sales and revenues for 2008 to top $50 billion.
The furnishing of this information is not intended to constitute a representation that such furnishing is required by Regulation FD or that this information includes material information that is not otherwise publicly available. In addition, the Registrant does not assume any obligation to update such information in the future.
