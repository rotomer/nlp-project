On April 8, 2009, the Board of Directors (Board) of Caterpillar Inc. (Company) elected Ambassador Susan C. Schwab, effective June 1, 2009, as an independent director with a term expiring at the annual meeting of stockholders in 2010.   Ambassador Schwabs election was disclosed in a Form 8-K filed on April 13, 2009.
This Amendment No. 1 is being filed to report that on June 10, 2009, the Board appointed Ambassador Schwab to serve as a member of its Public Policy Committee.
