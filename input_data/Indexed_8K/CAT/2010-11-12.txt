(b) Resignation of Gail D. Fosler from the Caterpillar Inc. Board of Directors
 
 
On February 10, 2010, the Company filed an 8-K reporting that the Board of Directors (the Board) accepted Ms. Foslers resignation from the Board, effective as of the expiration of her current term at the Companys annual meeting of stockholders in 2011.
 
Ms. Fosler notified the Company on November 5, 2010 that she intends to retire from the Board effective December 31, 2010 in order to accelerate the expansion of her business, The GailFosler Group.  This filing modifies the Companys 8-K filing made on February 10, 2010.
