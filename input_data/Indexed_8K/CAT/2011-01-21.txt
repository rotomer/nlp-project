ITEM: Other Events
On January 20, 2011, the stockholders of Bucyrus International, Inc. (Bucyrus) voted to approve the proposal to adopt the Agreement and Plan of Merger (the Merger Agreement), dated as of November 14, 2010, among the Company, Bucyrus and Badger Merger Sub, Inc., a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company (Merger Sub), which provides for the merger (the Merger) of Merger Sub with and into Bucyrus upon the terms, and subject to the conditions set forth in the Merger Agreement.
