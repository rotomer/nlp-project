ITEM: Regulation FD Disclosure
 
 
During a media interview on August 12, 2011 Caterpillar Inc. (the Company) Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Douglas R. Oberhelman (i) confirmed the Companys 2011 sales and revenues and profit per share outlook that it previously released on July 22, 2011, and (ii) stated that the Companys order backlog, which was at a record level of $21.9 billion as of June 30, 2011, continued at a record level through early August 2011.  The backlog does not include any order backlog related to the Companys recent acquisition of Bucyrus International, Inc.
