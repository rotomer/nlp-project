Stock Repurchase Program Extension
At its meeting on December 14, 2011, the Board of Directors of Caterpillar Inc. (the Company) approved the extension of the Companys current stock repurchase program.  The program was originally adopted in February 2007 and was due to expire on December 31, 2011.  Approximately $3.75 billion of the original $7.5 billion authorized under the stock repurchase program remains available for future use.  The Company does not currently plan to engage in stock repurchases under the program.  The renewed program will expire on December 31, 2015 or, if earlier, upon the completion of the total authorized stock repurchases.
