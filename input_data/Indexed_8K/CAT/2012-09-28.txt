ITEM: Other Events
Item 8.01. Other Events.
Caterpillar is announcing price action of 0 - 3% worldwide on most machines. This price increase will be effective January 2013 and includes adjustments to list prices and merchandising support.
In addition, emissions-related price increases of up to 7% will apply on select models for applicable regions.
This price increase is a result of current industry factors as well as general economic conditions. Details by product will be released to dealers in the near future, and will vary across geographic regions and products.
