Item 7.01       Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
On May 9, 2005, The Chubb Corporation (Chubb) received a subpoena from the United States Attorney for the Southern District of New York as part of an investigation into the use of finite risk insurance and related products. Chubb believes this investigation involves a number of industry participants and that Chubb has not been singled out in being asked to provide such information.  Chubb intends to cooperate fully with this investigation.
