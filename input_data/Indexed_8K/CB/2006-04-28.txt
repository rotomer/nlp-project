Item 8.01.
Other Events.
                                         
 
The Annual Meeting of Shareholders of The Chubb Corporation (Corporation) was held on April 25, 2006. Matters submitted to shareholders at the meeting and the voting results thereof were as follows:
 
The shareholders of the Corporation elected each of the director nominees proposed by the Corporation's Board of Directors to serve until his or her successor is duly elected and qualified. The following is a breakdown of the voting results:
Election of Directors.
 
 
The shareholders of the Corporation approved the adoption of The Chubb Corporation Annual Incentive Compensation Plan (2006). The following is a breakdown of the voting results:
Adoption of The Chubb Corporation Annual Incentive Compensation Plan (2006).
 
 
The shareholders of the Corporation ratified the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the Corporation's independent auditor. The following is a breakdown of the voting results:
Appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as Independent Auditor.
 
The shareholders approved a proposal requesting that the Board of Directors initiate the appropriate process to amend the Corporation's certificate of incorporation to provide that director nominees be elected by the affirmative vote of a majority of the votes cast. The following is a breakdown of the voting results:
Implementation of Majority Voting Standard in the Election of Directors.
 
The shareholders of the Corporation rejected a proposal requiring increased disclosure of the Corporation's political contributions beyond that which is required by applicable law or otherwise already voluntarily disclosed by the Corporation. The following is a breakdown of the voting results:
Increased Disclosure of Political Contributions.
