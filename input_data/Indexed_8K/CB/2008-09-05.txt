On September 4, 2008, the Board of Directors of The Chubb Corporation (Chubb) elected John J. Kennedy as Senior Vice President and Chief Accounting Officer effective as of September 30, 2008 when he will succeed Henry B. Schram. Mr. Kennedy, 52, currently serves as the Deputy Manager of Chubb's Accounting Department and has held positions of increasing responsibility within Chubb's Accounting Department since joining Chubb in 1987. As previously reported, Mr. Schram will remain with Chubb to assist with the transition until his retirement at the end of the year.
