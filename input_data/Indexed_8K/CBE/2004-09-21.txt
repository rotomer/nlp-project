Sales Trends
Sales comparisons are presented to help investors understand the general tone of Cooper's business. These comparisons include the impacts of acquisitions, divestitures, currency movements, etc. and are not necessarily indicative of general business trends.
Specific questions regarding these sales trends should be directed to Richard Bajenski, Vice President, Investor Relations, bajenski@cooperindustries.com, (713) 209-8610.
Sales for the three months ended on the dates shown, compared to the same period in the prior year:
Observations on recent sales trends for the three months ended August 31, 2004.
Cooper Industries
Sales for the three months ended August 31, 2004 increased 11-13% compared with the prior year. Recent months' sales comparisons continue to reflect the weak market conditions of the prior year. The current economic rebound began to accelerate in earnest in September 2003. Translation also continues to have a positive impact on revenues.
Electrical Products
Sales for the three months ended August 31, 2004 increased 13-15% compared with the prior year.
Tools and Hardware
Sales for the three months ended August 31, 2004 improved 3-5% compared with the prior year.
Note: Includes impacts of acquisitions and divestitures, when applicable.
