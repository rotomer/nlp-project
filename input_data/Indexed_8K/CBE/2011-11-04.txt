Item 8.01 Other Events
Cooper Industries plc ("Cooper") currently has 3,734,747 shares that may yet be purchased under its share repurchase program that was approved by the Board of Directors in February 2009. On November 1, 2011, Cooper's Board increased the share repurchase authorization by 10 million shares. In addition, Cooper's Board has authorized the repurchase of shares issued from time to time under Cooper's equity compensation plans, matched savings plan and dividend reinvestment plan in order to offset the dilution that results from issuing shares under these plans.
