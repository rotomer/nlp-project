Exhibit 99.1 DELAWARE COURT ALLOWS BLOCKBUSTER SPLIT-OFF EXCHANGE OFFER TO PROCEED NEW YORK, NEW YORK, September 22, 2004 - Viacom Inc. (NYSE: VIA, VIA.B) said today that, with regard to the shareholder lawsuit filed on September 17, the Delaware Court of Chancery has denied plaintiff's request that a hearing be scheduled on a motion for a preliminary injunction. As a result, the exchange offer will be permitted to proceed without interference from this case.
