ITEM 7.01  REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
Michael Ramke has been appointed President (Interim) of Modeo for the foreseeable future, effective as of January 1, 2007. Mr. Ramke has more than 10 years of experience in the wireless communications industry. He currently serves as Modeo's vice president of marketing and business development and has been closely involved with Modeo since its inception, including active involvement with the development of Modeo's New York network. Mr. Ramke joined Crown Castle in 1996 as one of the Company's first employees in the role of vice president of corporate development.
