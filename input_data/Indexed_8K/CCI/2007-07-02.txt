ITEM 7.01 REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
As previously disclosed, the Company received a letter dated July 17, 2006, from the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") stating that the SEC is conducting an informal inquiry into various accounting matters related to the Company, including whether grants of stock options may have been backdated.
