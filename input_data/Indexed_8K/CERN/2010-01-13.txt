Effective January 7, 2010, Mr. Michael Valentine's position with Cerner Corporation (the "Company") as Executive Vice President and General Manager U.S. has been changed to Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer. The Company did not previously have a Chief Operating Officer.
Mr. Valentine, age 41, joined Cerner in December 1998 as Director of Technology. He was promoted to Vice President in 2000 and to the President of Cerner Mid America in January of 2003. In February 2005, he was named General Manager of the U.S. Client Organization and was promoted to Senior Vice President in March 2005. He was promoted to Executive Vice President in March 2007. Prior to joining the Company, Mr. Valentine was an executive in the telecommunication and technology industry groups with Accenture Consulting.
There are no arrangements or understandings between Mr. Valentine and any other person pursuant to which he was selected as an officer. Mr. Valentine does not have any direct or indirect material interests in any transaction with the Company or in any currently proposed transaction to which the Company is a party.
