Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

The senior notes are to be issued under an indenture dated as of June 20, 2005. A copy of the press release announcing the pricing of the 6.25% senior notes due 2018 was filed under Item 8.01 of our Form 8-K dated June 7, 2005.
