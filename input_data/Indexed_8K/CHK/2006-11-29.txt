CHESAPEAKE ENERGY CORPORATION ANNOUNCES
OFFERING OF 400 MILLION OF SENIOR NOTES
 
OKLAHOMA CITY, OKLAHOMA, NOVEMBER 27, 2006  Chesapeake Energy Corporation (NYSE:CHK) today announced that it intends to commence a public offering of 400 million of a new issue of senior notes due 2017. Chesapeake intends to use the net proceeds from the offering to repay outstanding indebtedness under its revolving credit facility. The senior notes will be offered pursuant to an effective registration statement filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. Chesapeake intends to list the notes on the Irish Stock Exchange for trading on its Alternative Securities Market.
Barclays Capital, Credit Suisse, Deutsche Bank Securities and Goldman Sachs International will act as joint book-running managers for the offering.
