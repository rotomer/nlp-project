CIGNA Corporation hereby amends its Current Report on Form 8-K filed February 7, 2005 pursuant to instruction 2 to Item 5.02 of Form 8-K to provide information that was not determined or available at the time of the February 1, 2005 report. The February 7, 2005 Form 8-K was filed in part to report the appointment of Donna F. Zarcone and William D. Zollars to the Companys Board of Directors.
 
 
Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
.
 
 
Effective February 25, 2005, the Board of Directors of CIGNA Corporation appointed Ms. Zarcone to its Audit Committee and Finance Committee and Mr. Zollars to its People Resources Committee and Finance Committee.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
