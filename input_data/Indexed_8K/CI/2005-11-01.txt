Item 5.02(b)
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
.
On November 1, 2005, Marilyn Ware resigned as a director of CIGNA Corporation. Ms. Ware resigned her directorship because the President of the United States nominated her to be Ambassador to The Republic of Finland.
At the time of her resignation, Ms. Ware was the Chairman of the Corporate Governance Committee and a member of the People Resources Committee.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
