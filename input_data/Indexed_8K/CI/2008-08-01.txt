On August 1, 2008, CIGNA issued a news release announcing results for the second quarter 2008.  The news release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated by reference herein.
 
 
 
           Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
 
 

Exhibit 99.1
 
 
 
 
- -- CIGNA Corporation (NYSE: CI) today reported net income of $272 million, or $0.97 per share, for the second quarter of 2008 compared with $198 million, or $0.68 per share, for the same period last year. Net income for the second quarter 2008 included gains from the GMIB business of $34 million after-tax, or $0.12 per shareprimarily related to favorable interest rate movements, and a special item charge of $52 million after-tax, or $0.18 per share related to a litigation matter.
