Mr. Robert H. Campbell, who has served on the Board of Directors of CIGNA Corporation since 1992, informed the Company on October 19, 2009 of his intention to retire from the Board effective December 31, 2009 in order to devote more time to other professional and personal commitments.   His decision to retire was not as a result of any disagreement with the Company or its management. Mr. Campbells term as a director was scheduled to expire at the April 2010 annual meeting of shareholders.
