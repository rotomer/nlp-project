Mr. Peter N. Larson, who has served on the Board of Directors of CIGNA Corporation since 1997, informed the Company on October 19, 2010 of his intention to retire from the Board effective
December 31, 2010.   His decision to retire was not as a result of any disagreement with the Company or its management.
Mr. Larsons term as a director was scheduled to expire at the April 2011 annual meeting of shareholders. Further, in accordance with the Companys Board Practices he would have been required to retire from the Board of Directors at that time.
