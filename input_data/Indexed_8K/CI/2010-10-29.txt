 CIGNA Corporation (NYSE: CI) today reported shareholders net income of $307 million, or $1.13 per share, for the third quarter of 2010 compared with shareholders net income of $329 million, or $1.19 per share, for the same period last year.  Shareholders net income included a loss of $44 million, or $0.16 per share, in the third quarter of 2010 from results of the VADBe and GMIB businesses within our Run-off Reinsurance segment,primar ily related to the impact of low levels of interest rates.  Shareholders net income in the quarter included favorable claim development in the Health Care business related to lower-than-expected utilization.  Third quarter 2009 results included income of $16 million, or $0.06 per share, from the GMIB business.
PHILADELPHIA, October 29, 2010
