ITEM: Regulation FD Disclosure
Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
On January 24, 2006, Cincinnati Financial Corporation and its subsidiaries completed the sale of all of its 13,175,164 shares of ALLTEL Corporation common stock, which no longer met the company's investment parameters because of recent publicly announced actions by ALLTEL. The company's investment department will allocate proceeds of the sale in line with the company's investment objectives.  
The information furnished in Item 7.01 of this report shall not be deemed "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that Section, nor shall such information be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
