Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On June 9, 2005, the Board of Directors of Colgate-Palmolive Company elected Mr. Howard B. Wentz, Jr. to the Board of Directors. Mr. Wentz previously served as a Colgate director from 1982 to 2004. With the addition of Mr. Wentz, the Board now has nine members. The Board had been reduced to eight when Carlos M. Gutierrez resigned in January of this year upon his confirmation as Secretary of the United States Department of Commerce. Mr. Wentz will serve as a member of the three board committees on which he served previously, which are the Nominating and Corporate Governance, Finance and Audit Committees.
