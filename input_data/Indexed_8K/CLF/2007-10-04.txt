Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On September 11, 2007, the Board of Directors of Cleveland-Cliffs Inc (the "Company") appointed Laurie Brlas as Chief Accounting Officer in addition to her current position as Senior Vice President - Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer. As previously disclosed, Ms. Brlas, 50, joined the Company in December 2006 as Senior Vice President  Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer. Ms. Brlas was previously employed with STERIS Corporation as Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer from 2000 to December 2006. No change was made to Ms. Brlas compensation as a result of her appointment as Chief Accounting Officer. Prior to September 11, 2007, Robert J. Leroux, Vice President and Controller, served as the Companys Chief Accounting Officer. Effective October 1, 2007, Mr. Leroux was appointed as Vice President  Finance.
