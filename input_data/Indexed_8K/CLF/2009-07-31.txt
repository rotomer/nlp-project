Item 5.03 Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year.
On July 31, 2009, Cliffs Natural Resources Inc. (the "Company") filed an amendment (the "Amendment") of its Articles of Incorporation with the Secretary of State for the State of Ohio. The Amendment, approved by the Company's Board of Directors at a meeting on July 14, 2009, removed Subdivision A-2 of the Articles of Incorporation effective July 31, 2009. Subdivision A-2 had set forth the terms of the Company's 3.25% Redeemable Cumulative Convertible Perpetual Preferred Stock, all of which have been fully converted.
