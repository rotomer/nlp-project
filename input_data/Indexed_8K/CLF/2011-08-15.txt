Item 8.01 Other Events.
On August 15, 2011, the Board of Directors of Cliff Natural Resources Inc. (the "Company") approved a new share repurchase plan pursuant to which the Company may purchase up to four million of its outstanding common shares. The new share repurchase plan replaces the Companys existing share repurchase plan. Under the new share repurchase plan, the Company may purchase its common shares from time to time in open market purchases or privately negotiated transactions and may make all or part of the purchases pursuant to accelerated share repurchases or Rule 10b5-1 plans. The new share repurchase plan expires on December 31, 2012.
