Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure
As previously disclosed, The Clorox Company (the Company) has modified its segment reporting. Beginning with fiscal year 2009, the Company is reporting the following four segments: Cleaning, Lifestyle, Household, and International. Segment disclosures provided in the Company's Annual Report on Form 10-K for fiscal year 2009, filed on August 25, 2009, reflect this modified reporting structure; prior periods were updated to conform to the fiscal year 2009 presentation.
Attached hereto as Exhibits 99.1, 99.2, and 99.3 are net sales and earnings from continuing operations before income taxes segment results, which reflect the modified reporting structure, for quarterly and fiscal year periods 2009, 2008, and 2007, respectively.
