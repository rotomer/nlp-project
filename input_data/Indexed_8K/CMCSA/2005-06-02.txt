Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
Shareholder Approval of the Comcast Corporation 2002 Restricted Stock Plan, as Amended and Restated
 
On January 11, 2005, the Board of Directors of Comcast Corporation (the Company) approved an amendment to the Comcast Corporation 2002 Restricted Stock Plan (the Plan) to permit non-employee directors of the Company to receive awards of restricted stock and restricted stock units under the Plan. This amendment was approved by the Board of Directors, subject to the receipt of shareholder approval. On June 1, 2005, the date of the Companys annual meeting of shareholders, shareholders of the Company approved this amendment as part of their approval of the Plan, as Amended and Restated.
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
