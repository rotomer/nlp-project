ITEM 9. Regulation FD Disclosure. As announced by Chicago Mercantile Exchange Holdings Inc. ("CME Holdings") on August 18, 2003, Mr. James J. McNulty will step down as CME Holdings' President and Chief Executive Officer when his contract expires on December 31, 2003. Under Mr. McNulty's employment agreement, he will not vest in the remaining 20% of his stock option that is currently unvested. As a result, CME Holdings' compensation expense for the fourth quarter of 2003 will be reduced by $2.6 million, the amount of stock-based compensation expense related to the unvested portion of the option that was previously recorded. No further expense will be incurred for the option.
