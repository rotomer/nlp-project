Item 8.01. Other Events.
On June 2, 2008, Chicago Board Options Exchange, Inc. (CBOE) and class representatives announced that they have reached an agreement in principle to settle the class action brought against CBOE on behalf of all full members in the Board of Trade of the City of Chicago, Inc. (CBOT) who claim an interest in CBOE through the exercise right privilege (ERP). CME Group Inc. is a named party in interest in the litigation and is the owner of 159 ERPs. Attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 is a letter outlining the terms of the settlement from Gordon B. Nash, Jr. sent behalf of the class representatives to the CBOT full members.
