Section 1 - Registrant's Business and Operations
Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
Effective July 12, 2007, the registrant, certain of its wholly-owned subsidiaries, and General Electric Capital Corporation, as Administrative Agent for certain financial institutions from time to time party thereto as Purchasers, amended the Receivables Purchase and Servicing Agreement dated as of January 15, 2004 among such parties to, among other things, extend the Agreement to July 9, 2010 and to increase the purchase limitation from $200 million to $400 million.  The amendment also provides the registrant with an option to increase the purchase limitation up to $500 million with consent of the Administrative Agent and the Purchasers.    
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: July 16, 2007
