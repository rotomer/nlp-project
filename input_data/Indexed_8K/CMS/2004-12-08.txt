Item 8.01. Other Events.
Shareholder Derivative Lawsuit Demand Letter On December 6, 2004, the Board of Directors of CMS Energy Corporation ("CMS Energy") received a demand on behalf of a shareholder that (i) CMS Energy commence legal action against the current directors and certain current officers of CMS Energy as well as certain former CMS Energy directors and officers to remedy alleged breaches of fiduciary duties by these officers and directors in connection with alleged improper business practices, including but not limited to round-trip energy trading at CMS Marketing, Services and Trading Company and CMS Energys restatement of its historical financial results as a result of the round-trip trading and (ii) the special litigation committee of the Board of Directors, which was formed in January 2003, disclose all relevant details regarding the nature of its investigation related to a previous shareholder demand.
