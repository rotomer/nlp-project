Item 8.01 Other Events.
On May 22, 2006, CMS Energy Brasil S.A. ("CMS Brasil"), an indirect subsidiary of CMS Energy Corporation ("CMS Energy") and owner of certain electric distribution and generation assets in Brazil, filed a prospectus with the Comissao de Valores Mobiliarios, the Brazilian equivalent of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, to register the sale of a portion of its capital stock, including stock owned indirectly by CMS Energy, in an initial public offering. After the offering CMS Energy expects to hold 25% to 35% of the outstanding capital stock and will retain a majority of the voting stock of CMS Brasil. CMS Energy cannot predict if or when the initial public offering will be completed.
