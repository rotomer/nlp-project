Item 8.01 Other Events.
On August 7, 2007, Consumers Energy Company and the parties in its natural gas rate case submitted a partial settlement agreement to the Michigan Public Service Commission ("MPSC") that would increase Consumers Energy Companys natural gas rates by approximately $50 million. In addition to this increase, the parties agreed to a briefing schedule regarding an additional $9.3 million rate increase in connection with an energy efficiency proposal. The partial settlement agreement and the energy efficiency proposal are subject to the MPSCs final approval. For more information and to obtain a copy of the agreement please visit the MPSCs website at: http://efile.mpsc.cis.state.mi.us/efile/viewcase.php?casenum=15190
