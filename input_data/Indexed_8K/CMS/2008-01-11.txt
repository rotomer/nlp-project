Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
As a result of CMS Energy Company ("CMS") exercising its existing rights under its $300 Million Seventh Amended and Restated Credit Agreement dated as of April 2, 2007 ("CMS Facility"), the lenders thereunder have increased their commitments under the CMS Facility to $550 million. The commitment increase under the CMS Facility was affected by an Assumption and Acceptance dated January 8, 2008, which is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1 and is incorporated herein by reference. The CMS Facility was previously filed as Exhibit 10.2 to the Form 8-K filed April 2, 2007, and is also incorporated herein by reference.
