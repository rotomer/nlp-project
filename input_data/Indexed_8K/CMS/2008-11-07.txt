Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
CMS Energy Corporation's ("CMS Energy") management will meet with investors at the Edison Electric Institute Financial Conference ("Conference") beginning Sunday, November 9, 2008. CMS Energy's president and chief executive officer and its chief financial officer will provide a business and financial outlook ("Presentation") at the Conference on Tuesday, November 11 beginning at 11 am EST. A webcast of the Presentation will be available at the CMS Energy website, www.cmsenergy.com. A copy of the CMS Energy Presentation is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.
In accordance with General Instruction B.2 of Form 8-K, the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibit 99.1, shall not be deemed "filed" for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth in such a filing.
