Item 8.01 Other Events.
On December 23, 2008, the Michigan Public Service Commission ("MPSC") approved a settlement agreement in Consumers Energy Companys ("Company") gas rate case, increasing natural gas rates by approximately $22.4 million annually. (MPSC Case No. U-15506). In addition to the gas rate order, the MPSC also approved a partial settlement agreement in the Companys gas depreciation case, reducing the Companys annual depreciation expense by approximately $19.6 million per year until the MPSC issues a final order in the case. (MPSC Case No. U-15629). For more information and to obtain a copy of the orders please visit the MPSCs websites at: http://efile.mpsc.cis.state.mi.us/efile/viewcase.php?casenum=15506 and http://efile.mpsc.cis.state.mi.us/efile/viewcase.php?casenum=15629.
