ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS. RATING AGENCY ACTION On December 10, 2003, Fitch, Inc. affirmed its outstanding credit ratings on CenterPoint Energy, Inc. ("CenterPoint Energy"), CenterPoint Energy Houston Electric, LLC and CenterPoint Energy Resources Corporation. In addition, the rating outlook for all three companies was revised to negative from stable. Fitch stated that this outlook revision reflects a higher degree of uncertainty over CenterPoint Energy's ability to monetize its interest in Texas Genco Holdings, Inc.
