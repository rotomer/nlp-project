     On September 27, 2007, James S. Brian, 60, Senior Vice President and Chief Accounting Officer for CenterPoint Energy, Inc. (the Company) announced his intention to retire from the Company on November 30, 2007. Mr. Brian has been with the Company and its predecessors for over 30 years and has served in a number of executive capacities during his tenure. The Company announced that Mr. Walter L. Fitzgerald, 50, who has served as Vice President and Controller of the Company for the last six years, will assume the duties of the Company's chief accounting officer effective December 1, 2007.
