Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
 
CONSOL Energy Inc. entered into an agreement with P. Jerome Richey to serve as CONSOL Energy's Vice President and General Counsel. Mr. Richey commenced his employment with CONSOL Energy on March 1, 2005. The agreement provides that Mr. Richey will receive a base salary of $280,000 annually and may be entitled to receive annual incentive compensation targeted at 50% of his base salary. Under the agreement, Mr. Richey also will be entitled to participate in CONSOL Energy's long term incentive compensation plan and will receive an initial grant of $300,000 in the form of stock options and restricted stock units. The agreement provides for two years guaranteed employment through March 1, 2007, subject to termination only for gross misconduct. A copy of Mr. Richey's agreement is included as Exhibit 10.58 to this Form 8-K.
