Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers.
 
In connection with the closing of the private sale by a subsidiary of CONSOL Energy Inc. ("CEI"), CNX Gas Corporation ("CNX Gas"), of approximately 24.3 million shares of CNX Gas common stock, Philip W. Baxter, as of August 2, 2005, has resigned from the Board of Directors of CEI, and will continue as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of CNX Gas. Mr. Baxter has been the Chairman of CNX Gas since the date of its formation of June 30, 2005.
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
 
Dated: August 8, 2005
