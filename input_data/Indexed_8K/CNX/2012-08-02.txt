On July 29, 2012, Consolidation Coal Company, a subsidiary of CONSOL Energy Inc. (the "Company") and the operator of the Company's Robinson Run Mine located in Mannington, West Virginia, received an imminent danger order (the "Order") from the Mine Safety and Health Administration with respect to the Robinson Run Mine under Section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977. The Order described an ignition that occurred during cutting operations related to maintenance on the longwall. No injuries resulted from the condition described in the Order and the Order was subsequently abated.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
