On September 5, 2012, CONSOL Pennsylvania Coal Company, LLC, a subsidiary of CONSOL Energy Inc. (the "Company") and the operator of the Company's Bailey Mine located in Greene County, Pennsylvania, received an imminent danger order (the "Order") from the Mine Safety and Health Administration with respect to the Bailey Mine under Section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977. No injuries resulted from the condition described in the Order and the Order was abated in less than an hour.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Dated: September 11, 2012
