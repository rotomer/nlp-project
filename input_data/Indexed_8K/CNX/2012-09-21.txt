On September 11, 2012, CONSOL Energy Inc. (the "Company") filed a Form 8-K (the "Original Form 8-K") reporting in Item 1.04 that on September 5, 2012, CONSOL Pennsylvania Coal Company, LLC, a subsidiary of the Company and the operator of the Company's Bailey Mine located in Greene County, Pennsylvania, received an imminent danger order (the "Order") from the Mine Safety and Health Administration ("MSHA") under Section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977.
The Company is filing this Form 8-K/A to amend Item 1.04 of the Original Form 8-K to provide additional context to the disclosure provided in the Original Form 8-K relating to the Order. Except for the additional information provided below, this Form 8-K/A does not modify or update the disclosures in Item 1.04 of the Original Form 8-K, which is incorporated herein by reference.
 
After further review by MSHA, the Order was vacated on September 19, 2012.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Dated: September 21, 2012
