Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
Capital One announced today that it expects to take a charge of approximately $80 million in the fourth quarter of 2007 in connection with the settlement of the American Express litigation that was described in the Company's Form 10-Q/A filed on November 9, 2007.
This report will not be deemed an admission as to the materiality of any information in the report that is required to be disclosed solely by Regulation FD. Furthermore, the information provided in this Item 7.01 shall not be deemed to be incorporated by reference into the filings of the Company under the Securities Act of 1933.
