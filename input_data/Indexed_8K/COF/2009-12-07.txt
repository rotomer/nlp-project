Capital One announced today that it has not repurchased any of the warrants to purchase shares of its common stock (the "Warrants") offered by the United States Department of Treasury (the "Treasury") in the Treasury's secondary public offering of the Warrants. The Treasury acquired a warrant to purchase 12,657,960 shares of Capital One's common stock as part of the Capital Purchase Program on November 14, 2008.
