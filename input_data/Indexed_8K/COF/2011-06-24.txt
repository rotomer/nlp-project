On June 20, 2011, Susan McFarland notified Capital One Financial Corporation (the "Company") that she will resign from her position as Executive Vice President, Finance and Principal Accounting Officer effective July 8, 2011. R. Scott Blackley, age 43, will serve as Principal Accounting Officer effective July 9, 2011. Mr. Blackley will continue to serve as Senior Vice President and Controller, a position he has held since he joined the Company in March 2011. For the six years prior to joining the Company, Mr. Blackley held various executive positions at Fannie Mae, a government-sponsored enterprise that was chartered by Congress in 1938 to support liquidity, stability and affordability in the secondary mortgage market, most recently as Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Capital Markets Group. Earlier in his career, Mr. Blackley held other leadership roles at America Online, KPMG and the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
