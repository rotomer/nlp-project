On February 23, 2007, the Board of Directors of Cabot Oil & Gas Corporation declared a two-for-one split of the Company's common stock in the form of a stock distribution. The stock dividend will be distributed on March 30, 2007 to shareholders of record on March 16, 2007. Additionally, the Board of Directors approved a 50 percent increase in dividend payments. The Company will increase its annual dividend to $0.12 per share following the split.
