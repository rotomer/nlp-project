Item 8.01 Other Events.
On April 12, 2007, the Company determined that it reported incorrect meeting attendance information for Mr. David M. Carmichael, Director, in its Proxy Statement for its 2007 Annual Meeting of Stockholders, on page 6, under the heading "Board of Directors and Committee Meeting Attendance". Mr. Carmichael attended 86% of the meetings of the Board of Directors and committees held while he was a member during 2006. Mr. Carmichael missed one board meeting due to illness.
