On July 22, 2008, the Company announced the appointment of Mr. Rhys J. Best to the Board of Directors, effective July 22, 2008, to serve until the next annual meeting of the shareholders. Mr. Best is expected to stand for election at the 2009 annual meeting. The Board of Directors has appointed Mr. Best to the Audit Committee and the Safety and Environmental Affairs Committee.
Mr. Best is a director of Crosstex Energy LP, Trinity Industries, Inc., Austin Industries, Inc. and McJunkin Red Man Corporation.
