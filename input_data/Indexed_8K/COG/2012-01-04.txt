Item 8.01. Other Events.
On January 3, 2012, the Board of Directors of Cabot Oil & Gas Corporation declared a two-for-one split of the Company's common stock in the form of a stock distribution. The stock dividend will be distributed on January 25, 2012 to shareholders of record on January 17, 2012. Additionally, the Board of Directors approved a 33 percent increase in dividend payments. The Company will increase its annual dividend on a pre-split basis to $0.16 per share.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: January 4, 2012
