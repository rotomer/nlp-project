Item 8.01. Other Events. ------------- Effective as of October 12, 2004, the Board of Directors of Coach, Inc. ("Coach") adopted resolutions directing Coach to take appropriate actions to amend and restate the Rights Agreement dated as of May 3, 2001 between Coach and Mellon Investor Services L.L.C. (the "Rights Agreement") to: (a) eliminate all requirements in the Rights Agreement that actions, approvals and determinations to be taken or made by Coach's Board of Directors be taken or made by a majority of the members of the Board of Directors who are not "Future Directors," and (b) make certain clarifying changes. The deletion of all references to "Future Directors" eliminates from the Rights Agreement what are commonly referred to as "slow hand" provisions. Item 9.01. Exhibits. --------- None
