Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
Registrant has been informed by Rockwell Automation, Inc. ("RAI") that RAI has decided to close the Rockwell Collins Stock Fund in RAIs 401(k) Savings Plan on March 31, 2006. As a result, participants investing in that fund will be required to transfer or sell all of their Rockwell Collins stock fund balances by March 31, 2006. We have been informed that RAI will be disposing of the Rockwell Collins common stock in open market transactions in an orderly process between now and March 31, 2006. As of June 7, 2005, the RAI Savings Plan held 10.9 million shares of Rockwell Collins common stock. We have been advised that RAI is terminating all of its non-RAI single stock funds as an investment option under its Savings Plan.
