Item 8.01  Other Events
In response to a question in its conference call for investors that occurred on September 13, 2004, Campbell Soup Company responded that it expected to spend $340 million in capital expenditures in fiscal 2005. The correct number of projected capital expenditures for fiscal 2005 is $380 million. The company expects capital expenditures to average approximately $340 million per year over the next three fiscal years.
