Item 8.01. Other Events
 
On January 28, 2011, Computer Sciences Corporation (the Company) was notified by the United States Securities and Exchange Commission that the Commission has commenced a formal civil investigation relating to the Company's previously disclosed accounting adjustments in the Companys Managed Services Sector segment, primarily in Europes Nordic region.  The Company is cooperating with the Commission's investigation.
