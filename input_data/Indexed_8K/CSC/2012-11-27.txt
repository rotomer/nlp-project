Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
Computer Sciences Corporation (Company) has agreed with Mr. Guy M. Hains that he will step down from his position as Vice President and General Manager of the Global Healthcare Industry Group, effective November 27, 2012. Mr. Hains will continue his employment with the Company until December 31, 2012 to ensure a smooth transition.
Effective November 27, 2012, Andrea Fiumicelli has been appointed Vice President and General Manager of the Global Healthcare Industry Group. Prior to his appointment as Vice President and General Manager of the Global Healthcare Industry Group, Mr. Fiumicelli served as Director of Healthcare Integration and Projects since 2011. He joined the Company as part of the 2011 acquisition of iSOFT, a leading healthcare software company where he served as CEO.
