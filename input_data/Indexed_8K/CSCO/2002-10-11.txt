(408) 525-4909 kgibbons@cisco.com CISCO SYSTEMS COMPLETES ACQUISITION OF AYR NETWORKS SAN JOSE, Calif., Oct. 11, 2002 -- Cisco Systems, Inc., today announced it has completed the acquisition of privately-held AYR Networks, Inc. of Palo Alto, California. AYR Networks develops high-performance distributed networking services and highly scalable routing software technologies. AYR Networks' technology and expertise will augment the continued evolution of Cisco IOS(R) Software, the network systems software for Cisco's routing and switching platforms.
