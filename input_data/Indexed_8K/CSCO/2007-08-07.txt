Departure of Directors or Certain Officers.
On August 6, 2007, Dennis D. Powell notified Cisco Systems, Inc. ("Cisco") of his decision to retire as Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Cisco, effective around mid-February 2008. Effective upon Mr. Powell's retirement, Cisco expects to appoint Frank A. Calderoni, currently Senior Vice President, Customer Solutions Finance of Cisco, to succeed Mr. Powell.
