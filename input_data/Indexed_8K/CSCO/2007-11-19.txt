On January 19, 2007, Cisco Systems, Inc. ("Cisco") filed a Current Report on Form 8-K under Item 5.02 to report the appointment of Brian L. Halla to its Board of Directors. Because Mr. Halla was not immediately appointed to serve on any board committees, disclosure of the board committees on which he would serve was not included in the filing in accordance with instruction no. 2 of the Instructions to Item 5.02.
Similarly, on March 23, 2007, Cisco filed a Current Report on Form 8-K under Item 5.02, among other items, to report, among other items, the appointment of Michael K. Powell to its Board of Directors. Because Mr. Powell was not immediately appointed to serve on any board committees, disclosure of the board committees on which he would serve was not included in the filing in accordance with instruction no. 2 of the Instructions to Item 5.02.
This Form 8-K/A is filed as an amendment (Amendment No. 1) to each of the foregoing Forms 8-K.
 
On November 15, 2007, Brian L. Halla was appointed as a member of the Compensation and Management Development Committee of the Board of Directors of Cisco.
On November 15, 2007, Michael K. Powell was appointed as a member of the Investment/Finance Committee of the Board of Directors of Cisco.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
