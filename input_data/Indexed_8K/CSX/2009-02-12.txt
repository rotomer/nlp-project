Item 5.02(b).  Departure of Directors
On February 10, 2009, Mr. Christopher Hohn notified the Governance Committee of the Board of Directors (the Board) of CSX Corporation (the "Company") that he did not wish to be included as a nominee for re-election as a director when his current term ends at the upcoming Annual Meeting of Shareholders currently scheduled for May 6, 2009 (the Annual Meeting).  As a result, the Board approved the reduction in the size of the Board from twelve directors to eleven directors, effective as of the date of the Annual Meeting.  Mr. Hohn has informed the Company that this decision resulted from his responsibilities in managing his business interests. 
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: February 12, 2009
