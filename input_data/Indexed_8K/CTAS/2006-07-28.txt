Item 1.01    Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement.
        On July 24, 2006 the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Cintas Corporation amended the compensation paid to its non-employee directors, namely, Gerald S. Adolph, Paul R. Carter, Gerald V. Dirvin, Joyce Hergenhan, Roger L. Howe and David C. Phillips. Under the new compensation arrangement for non-employee directors, the annual retainer was increased to $40,000. Board and Committee meeting fees remained at $2,750 and $1,200 per meeting respectively. Compensation for telephonic meetings of the Board and Committees will be at 50% of these amounts. Fees for Committee Chairmen remained at $8,000 for the Audit Committee Chairman and $5,000 for the other Chairpersons. The number of shares covered by annual options remained at 1,000.
