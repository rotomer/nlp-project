Item 8.01.        Other Events.
 
In the past, Cintas Corporation (the "Company") has engaged Frost Brown Todd LLC for a variety of legal services.  Scott D. Phillips, a partner of Frost Brown, is the son of David C. Phillips, a member of the Company's board of directors.  In September 2010, the Company committed to a provider of proxy voting and corporate governance services that it would not use Frost Brown for more than $10,000 of legal services during the twelve-month period starting from the date of such commitment.  During such twelve-month period, the Company did not utilize Frost Brown for legal services.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
