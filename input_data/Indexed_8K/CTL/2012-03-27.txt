Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On March 27, 2012, Edward A. Mueller announced his intention to resign from our Board of Directors, effective the date of our 2012 annual meeting of shareholders, which is scheduled for May 23, 2012. Mr. Mueller, who formerly served as chairman and chief executive officer of Qwest Communications International Inc. ("Qwest"), joined our Board in connection with our acquisition of Qwest on April 1, 2011 and is currently serving a term otherwise scheduled to end in 2013.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this current report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
