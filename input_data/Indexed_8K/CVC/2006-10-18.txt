Item 8.01    Other Events.
        On October 17, 2006, the Special Transaction Committee of the Board of Directors of Cablevision Systems Corporation, which was formed to evaluate and act on the proposal of the Dolan Family Group to acquire the outstanding publicly-held shares of common stock of Cablevision Systems Corporation, retained Lehman Brothers Inc. and Morgan Stanley as financial advisors to assist in considering the proposal.
         There can be no assurance that any agreement on financial and other terms satisfactory to the Special Transaction Committee will result from the Committee's evaluation or negotiation of the proposal or that any corporate transaction recommended by the Special Transaction Committee will be completed.
         The Special Transaction Committee previously retained the law firm Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP as its legal advisor.
