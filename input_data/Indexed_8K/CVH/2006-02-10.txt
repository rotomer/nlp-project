SECTION 3  SECURITIES AND TRADING MARKETS
 
ITEM 3.03  Material Modification to Rights of Security Holders
 
Coventry Health Care, Inc. (the "Company") allowed its Shareholder Rights Agreement (the "Rights Agreement"), to expire on February 7, 2006. The Rights Agreement, entered into on March 30, 1998, provided common shareholders the right to purchase shares of Common Stock at a discount upon the occurrence of certain events as set forth therein. The Company's Board of Directors voted to allow the Rights Agreement to expire in accordance with its terms. As a result, the stock purchase rights under the Rights Agreement have terminated and are no longer effective.
