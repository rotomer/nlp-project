SECTION 1  REGISTRANT'S BUSINESS AND OPERATIONS
 
ITEM 1.01  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
Upon the Compensation Committee's approval and recommendation, on December 28, 2006, the Board of Directors of Coventry Health Care, Inc. (the "Company") approved compensation payable to Mr. Allen F. Wise, Chairman of the Board, for 2007. Under the terms of Mr. Wise's Transition and Retirement Agreement dated September 22, 2004, Mr. Wise is entitled to receive compensation beginning in 2007 for his services as a director. The Committee and Board agreed that Mr. Wise will receive the standard annual directors' compensation of $225,000. In addition, he will receive a retainer of $125,000 for his services as non-executive Chairman during 2007. The terms and conditions of the Transition and Retirement Agreement remain in full force and effect.
