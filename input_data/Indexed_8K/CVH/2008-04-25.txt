Coventry Health Care Reports First Quarter Earnings of $0.81 per Diluted Share
Reiterates 2008 Guidance
 
BETHESDA, Md. (April 25, 2008) - Coventry Health Care, Inc. (NYSE: CVH) today reported operating results for the quarter ended March 31, 2008. Operating revenues totaled $2.94 billion for the quarter with net earnings of $125.0 million, or $0.81 per diluted share.
 
"Consistent with our comments in mid-March, we are pleased to confirm that our businesses continue to perform well and are fundamentally sound," said Dale B. Wolf, chief executive officer of Coventry. "We are on track for another year of industry leading revenue growth and remain very confident about our strategic positioning and growth prospects for the future."
