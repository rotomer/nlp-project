BETHESDA, Md. (April 30, 2010) - Coventry Health Care, Inc. (NYSE: CVH) today consolidated operating results for the quarter ended March 31, 2010.  Operating revenues totaled $2.9 billion for the quarter with net earnings of $97.3 million, or $0.66 earnings per diluted share (EPS).  Excluding the favorable impact of Medicare Advantage Private Fee-for-Service (MA-PFFS) resultsin the quarter, operating revenues totaled $2.9 billion for the quarter with net earnings of $71.9 million, or $0.49 EPS.
