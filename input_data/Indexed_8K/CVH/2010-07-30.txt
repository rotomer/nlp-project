BETHESDA, Md. (July 30, 2010) - Coventry Health Care, Inc. (NYSE: CVH) today reported consolidated operating results for the quarter ended June 30, 2010.  Operating revenues totaled $2.9 billion for the quarter with net earnings of $1.0 million, or $0.01 earnings per diluted share (EPS).  These results include a favorable impact from the Medicare Advantage Private Fee-for-Service (MA-PFFS) product of $0.18 EPS and an unfavorable impact related to the previously announced provider class action litigation in Louisiana of $1.18 EPS.  Excluding the impact of MA-PFFS resultsand the provider class action charge, earnings for the quarter were $149.2 m illion, or $1.01 EPS.  Additionally, the Company has increased guidance for full year 2010 as detailed on page 3.
