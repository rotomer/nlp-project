                            Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
Item 5.07
 
(a), (b), (d)  Coventry Health Care, Inc. (the Company) held its 2011 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the Meeting) on May 19, 2011.  An aggregate of 133,767,542 shares of Common Stock, or 90.23% of the Companys outstanding shares, were represented at the Meeting either in person or by proxy.  Five proposals were submitted for stockholder approval at the Meeting. Each of the proposals is described in detail in the Companys proxy statement distributed in connection with the Meeting. The final results for the votes regarding each proposal are set forth below.
 
 
 
 
The Board of Directors has considered the vote of the Companys shareholders as to the frequency of future advisory votes on executive compensation and intends to hold an annual advisory vote on executive compensation.
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this Report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
