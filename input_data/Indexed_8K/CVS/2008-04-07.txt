On April 4, 2008, CVS Caremark Corporation ("CVS Caremark") announced that Chris W. Bodine, Executive Vice President and President  CVS Caremark Health Care Services, has made the decision, for personal reasons, to scale back his day-to-day responsibilities and will begin transition to retirement in 2009. In connection with the planned transition, Mr. Bodine is assuming a new position as Special Advisor to the Chief Executive Officer of CVS Caremark. In this position he will retain responsibility for Trade Relations/Rx Purchasing and MinuteClinic, as well as an important role in the continued development of the Company's new health care model and roadmap for its long-term growth.
