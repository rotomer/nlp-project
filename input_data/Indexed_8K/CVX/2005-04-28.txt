Item 5.02 (b)
Departure of Director
Effective at the Corporation's Annual Meeting of Stockholders held on April 27, 2005, Senator J. Bennett Johnston retired as a Director of the Corporation. Senator Johnston retired pursuant to the Corporation's mandatory retirement policy for Directors under the Corporation's Corporate Governance Guidelines, which provides that non-employee Directors may not stand for re-election after reaching the age of 72.
