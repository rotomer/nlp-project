Item 5.03  Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year
Effective November 9, 2007, Dominion Resources, Inc. (the Company) amended its Articles of Incorporation (the Amendment) to increase the number of shares of common stock (the Common Stock) the Company is authorized to issue from 500,000,000 to 1,000,000,000.  The Amendment was filed in connection with and will effect the two-for-one split of the Companys Common Stock previously announced by the Company.  A copy of the Articles of Amendment is attached hereto as Exhibit 3.
