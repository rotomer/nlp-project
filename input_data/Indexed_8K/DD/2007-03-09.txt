Alain J.P. Belda and Charles M. Vest have elected to retire from DuPont's Board of Directors and will not stand for re-election at the Company's Annual Meeting of Stockholders on April 25, 2007. Their retirements were not due to any disagreement with the Company.
Concurrent with these retirements, Robert A. Brown, president of Boston University, and Bertrand P. Collomb, chairman and former chief executive officer of Paris-based Lafarge, will be nominees for election to DuPont's Board of Directors at the Company's Annual Meeting of Stockholders on April 25, 2007.
