Section 2  Financial Information
Item 2.03 Creation of a Direct Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant
On July 31, 2007, the Registrant entered into a structured stock repurchase agreement with a large financial institution in which the Registrant made an upfront payment of $1.1 billion. The financial institution agreed to deliver a certain number of shares based on the volume-weighted average price, less a specified discount at the end of the contract period. The contract is expected to settle no later than September 28, 2007. The Registrant will finance the purchase with short-term borrowings.
The Registrant announced the $5 billion stock repurchase plan in October 2005. As of June 30, 2007, the Registrant had repurchased $3.9 billion, or approximately 90 million shares. The Registrant previously stated it expected to complete the share repurchase plan by the end of 2007.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
