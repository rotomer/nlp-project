Deere & Company Raises Quarterly Dividend
 
Moline, Illinois  (May 26, 2010)  The Deere & Company Board of Directors declared a dividend payment of $.30 a share on common stock, payable August 2, 2010, to stockholders of record on June 30, 2010.  The new quarterly rate represents an increase of two cents per share over the previous level, or seven percent.
