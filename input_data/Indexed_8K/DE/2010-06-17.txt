DEERE INTERIM TIER 4 ENGINE SOLUTION & DEALER READINESS AND SUPPORT PLAN CONFERENCE CALL TO BE BROADCAST
ON WEBSITE
MOLINE, IL -- (17 June 2010)  Deere & Company today said its Interim Tier 4 Engine Solution & Dealer Readiness and Support Plan conference call would be broadcast over the Internet on Thursday, 24 June, beginning at 9:00 a.m. central time.
 
During the call, John Deere product technology experts will explain how equipment with advanced exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) engines will operate to bring world-class productivity and complete-fluid efficiency to customers.
 
In addition, John Deere will review a fully integrated dealer readiness plan using advanced diagnostic tools through ServiceADVISOR. Dealers are well-equipped to work individually with customers to optimize machinery use, fluid efficiency, and overall machinery productivity based on specific tasks.
 
The conference call can be accessed at . The recorded call will be available on the Deere website for a period of time afterward.
