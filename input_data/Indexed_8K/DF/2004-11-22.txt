Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
On November 18, 2004, our subsidiaries that are party to our receivables-backed facility entered into an amendment of the facility, pursuant to which the facility termination date was extended by one year to November 17, 2005. The amendment also modified a definition used in calculating the loss reserve, which we expect to slightly increase our borrowing capacity under the facility. The amendment is Amendment No. 4 to our Fourth Amended and Restated Receivables Purchase Agreement, and is by and among (1) Dairy Group Receivables, L.P., Specialty Group Receivables, L.P., and Dean National Brand Group, L.P., all of which are our subsidiaries, (2) the financial institutions that are party to our Receivables Purchase Agreement, (3) JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association, as agent, and (4) us, as provider of certain performance undertakings on behalf of our subsidiaries.
