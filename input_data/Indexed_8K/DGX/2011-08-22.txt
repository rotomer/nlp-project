Item 8.01.  Other Events
Biotechnology Value Fund, L.P. and affiliated entities (BVF) have withdrawn the assertion of appraisal rights in connection with 19,351,042 shares of common stock (Common Stock) of Celera Corporation (Celera) in connection with the merger of Celera and Spark Acquisition Corporation, a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company.  The Company accepted the shares of Common Stock surrendered by BVF and paid to BVF the same $8 per share merger consideration received by other holders of shares of Common Stock.
