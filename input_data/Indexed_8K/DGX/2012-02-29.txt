Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
On December 6, 2011, Quest Diagnostics Incorporated (the Company) filed a Current Report on Form 8-K (the Initial Filing) announcing the appointment of Mr. Timothy M. Ring to the Board of Directors (Board). At the time of the Initial Filing, it had not been determined to which committees of the Board Mr. Ring would be named. The Company hereby amends the Initial Filing to report that effective February 26, 2012, Mr. Ring was appointed to the Compensation Committee of the Board and the Quality, Safety and Compliance Committee of the Board.
