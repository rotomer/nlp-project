ITEM 5.02        DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS
 
As previously reported on a Form 8-K filed on November 16, 2004, as of April 4, 2005 Daniel L. Comas succeeded Patrick W. Allender as Chief Financial Officer of the Company. Mr. Allender remains Executive Vice President of the Company and is expected to continue with the Company at least through 2005 as he begins a gradual transition process toward retirement. Mr. Comas, 41 years old, has worked for Danaher since 1991, serving as Vice President-Corporate Development from 1996 until April 2004 and as Senior Vice President-Finance and Corporate Development from April 2004 until April 2005.
