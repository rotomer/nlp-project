CHESAPEAKE, Va. - June 19, 2006 - Dollar Tree Stores, Inc. (NASDAQ: DLTR), the nation's leading
operator of single-price point dollar stores
, will participate in the 16
th
Annual Wachovia Securities Nantucket Equity Conference, being held on June 19 - 22, 2006, at The Harbor House Village, in Nantucket, Massachusetts. Dollar Trees presentation is scheduled for Wednesday, June 21, at approximately 11:45 am EDT. Kent Kleeberger, the Companys Chief Financial Officer, will discuss Dollar Trees business and growth plans. A live audio-only web cast of the Companys presentation at the Wachovia conference will be available on Dollar Trees web site, www.DollarTree.com/medialist.cfm. A replay will be available within 24 hours of the presentation and can be accessed at the same location. This replay will be available until midnight June 28, 2006.
