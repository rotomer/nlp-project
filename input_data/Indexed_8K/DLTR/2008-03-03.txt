CHESAPEAKE, Va.  February 29, 2008  Dollar Tree Stores, Inc. (NASDAQ: DLTR), the nation's leading operator of single-price point dollar stores, will participate in the 29 Annual Raymond James Institutional Investors  Conference, being held on March 2  5, at The Hyatt Regency Grand Cypress  Hotel in Orlando, Florida.  Dollar Trees presentation is scheduled for Wednesday, March 5, at approximately 8:40 am EST.  Timothy J. Reid, the Companys Vice President of Investor Relations, will discuss Dollar Trees business and growth plans.  A live audio-only web cast of the Companys presentation at the Raymond James conference will be available on Dollar Trees web site, www.DollarTree.com/medialist.cfm.  A replay will be available within 24 hours of the presentation and can be accessed at the same location.  This replay will be available until midnight March 11, 2008.
