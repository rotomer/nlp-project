CHESAPEAKE, Va.  December 2, 2009  Dollar Tree, Inc. (NASDAQ: DLTR), the nation's leading operator of discount variety stores selling everything for $1 or less, will participate in the 10 Annual Wedbush Morgan California Dreamin Conference, being held on December 8  9, at The Fairmont Miramar Hotel in Santa Monica, California.  Dollar Trees presentation is scheduled for Tuesday, December 8, at approximately 2:15 pm PST.  Kevin Wampler, the Companys Chief Financial Officer, will discuss Dollar Trees business and growth plans.  A live audio-only web cast of the Companys presentation at the Wedbush Morgan conference will be available on Dollar Trees web site, www.dollartreeinfo.com/investors/news/events.  A replay will be available within 24 hours of the presentation and can be accessed at the same location.  This replay will be available until midnight December 15, 2009.
