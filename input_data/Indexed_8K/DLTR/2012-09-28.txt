Item 7.01.  Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
On September 28, 2012, Dollar Tree, Inc. (Company) sold its ownership interest in Ollies Holdings, Inc (Ollies).  The Company originally acquired its Ollies ownership interest in 2003.  The Company expects the sale to have a favorable impact on third quarter earnings with an increase to net income otherwise reported of approximately $0.16 to $0.17 per diluted share.
