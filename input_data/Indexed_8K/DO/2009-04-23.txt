Exhibit 99.1
Houston, Texas, April 23, 2009  Diamond Offshore Drilling, Inc. () today reported net income for the first quarter of 2009 of $348.6 million, or $2.51 per share on a diluted basis, compared with net income of $290.5 million, or $2.09 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period a year earlier. Revenues in the first quarter of 2009 were $885.7 million, compared with revenues of $786.1 million for the first quarter of 2008.
