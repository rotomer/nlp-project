Dover Corporation's shareholder rights plan expired on November 7, 2006 with the expiration of the Company's Amended and Restated Rights Agreement dated as of November 15, 1996, between the Company and Harris Trust Company of New York (the "Rights Agreement"). The Rights Agreement expired by its own terms and the Company's Board of Directors has decided not to renew it. The terms of the Rights Agreement and the common stock rights issued under it are described in the Company's Form 8-A/A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on November 15, 1996, and the exhibits thereto.
