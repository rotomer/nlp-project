Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
(d) New Director
On December 8, 2005, the Board of Directors of The Dow Chemical Company (the "Company") elected Geoffery E. Merszei Director of the Company.
At the present time, Mr. Merszei has been named as a member of the Finance Committee.
There is no arrangement or understanding between Mr. Merszei and any other person pursuant to which Mr. Merszei was elected as a director of the Company. There are no transactions in which Mr. Merszei has an interest requiring disclosure under Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
