Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
(d) New Director
On December 21, 2005, the Board of Directors of The Dow Chemical Company (the "Company") elected James A. Bell Director of the Company.
At the present time, Mr. Bell has been named as a member of the Audit Committee and the Environment, Health and Safety Committee.
There is no arrangement or understanding between Mr. Bell and any other person pursuant to which Mr. Bell was elected as a Director of the Company. There are no transactions in which Mr. Bell has an interest requiring disclosure under Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
