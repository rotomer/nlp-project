Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
(b) On February 9, 2006, the Board of Directors of The Dow Chemical Company (the "Company") nominated a slate of Directors for election at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders to be held on May 11, 2006. The slate reflected the decision of J. Michael Cook, a current Director of the Company, not to stand for re-election. His term will expire on May 11, 2006.
