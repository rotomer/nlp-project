Item 5.02.
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers, Election of Directors, Appointment of Principal Officers.
On September 25, 2006, DTE Energy Company (the "Company") received notice from Joe W. Laymon that he was resigning from the Company's Board of Directors, effective that same day. Mr. Laymon was a member of the Company's Organization and Compensation Committee and Public Responsibility Committee. Mr. Laymon stated he was resigning due to changes in management responsibilities at Ford Motor Company where he is currently the Group Vice President, Corporate Human Resources and Labor Affairs.
