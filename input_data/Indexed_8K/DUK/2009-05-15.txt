Item 7.01.  Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
On May 14, 2009, Duke Energy Ohio, Inc. was notified that it was one of the successful bidders in the FirstEnergy Ohio Utilities Standard Service Offer auction to supply the retail generation needs of FirstEnergy Corp.'s Ohio operating companies from June 1, 2009 through May 31, 2011 at a final wholesale price of $61.50 per megawatt hour.  The auction results have been accepted by the Public Utilities Commission of Ohio.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
