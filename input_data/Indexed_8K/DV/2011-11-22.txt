On November 22, 2011, Mr. Gary Butler notified the board of directors (the Board) of DeVry Inc. (DeVry) of his decision to resign for personal reasons, effective immediately, as a director of DeVry and as a member of the Compensation Committee and the Finance Committee of the Board.  There are no known disagreements between Mr. Butler and DeVry on any matters relating to DeVrys operations, policies or practices.
At present, the Board does not intend to fill the vacancy on the Board as a result of Mr. Butlers resignation.  The Board intends to reduce the size of the Board from 13 members to 12 members pursuant to the provisions of the Amended and Restated By-Laws of DeVry.
