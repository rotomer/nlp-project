On March 21, 2006, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of DaVita Inc. (the "Company") established the operating income target that will serve as the 2006 performance goal under the DaVita Inc. Executive Incentive Plan (the "Plan") for Kent J. Thiry, the Company's Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, and Joseph C. Mello, the Company's Chief Operating Officer. If the 2006 performance goal is achieved, Mr. Thiry and Mr. Mello will be eligible for a cash and restricted stock award for 2006 performance under the Plan. The Committee has the discretion to make such cash and/or restricted stock awards under the Plan as it believes to be in the best interests of the Company.
