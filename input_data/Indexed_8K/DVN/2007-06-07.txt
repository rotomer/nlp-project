Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
     On June 6, 2007, each non-management member of the Board of Directors of Devon Energy Corporation was granted 2,000 shares of restricted stock to vest 25% per year beginning June 6, 2008, and 3,000 stock options to purchase common stock of the Company at $79.57 per share, the closing price of the common stock as quoted by the New York Stock Exchange. Such options vest on June 6, 2007 and expire on June 5, 2015. The restricted stock and stock option grants were made under the Devon Energy Corporation 2005 Long-Term Incentive Plan.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereto duly authorized.
Date: June 7, 2007
