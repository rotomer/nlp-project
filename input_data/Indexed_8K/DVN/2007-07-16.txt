Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
     A $1,000,000,000 364-Day Senior Credit Facility, dated July 6, 2007, effective as of July 11, 2007, was entered into among Devon Energy Corporation as Borrower, Bank of America, Administrative Agent, and Banc of America Securities LLC and J.P. Morgan Securities, Inc. as Joint Lead Arrangers. The proceeds of the Senior Credit Facility shall be used for short to medium-term liquidity needs, including, but not limited to, commercial paper backstop and general corporate purposes.
