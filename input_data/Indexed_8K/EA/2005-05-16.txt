Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On May 12, 2005, at a regularly-scheduled meeting of the Board of Directors of Electronic Arts Inc. (the "Company"), William J. Byron, age 72, declared his intention to retire.
