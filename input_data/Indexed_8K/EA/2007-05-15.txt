On May 10, 2007, at a regularly-scheduled meeting of the Board of Directors of Electronic Arts Inc. (the "Company"), M. Richard Asher, age 75, declared his intention to retire. Mr. Asher has served as a Director of the Company since 1984 and his retirement will become effective at the Company's 2007 Annual Meeting of Stockholders, which is scheduled for July 26, 2007. Effective immediately upon Mr. Asher's retirement, the size of the Company's Board of Directors will be reduced to nine directors.
