ITEM 9. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
     On May 15, 2003, eBay Inc. filed its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the three months ended March 31, 2003 with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Accompanying such report were certifications of eBay Inc.'s Chief Executive Officer, Margaret C. Whitman, and Chief Financial Officer, Rajiv Dutta, pursuant to 18 U.S.C.  1350 adopted pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002. The text of each of these certifications is set forth below:
I, Margaret C. Whitman, hereby certify pursuant to 18 U.S.C.  1350 adopted pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 that:
/s/ MARGARET C. WHITMAN
Margaret C. Whitman
Chief Executive Officer
I, Rajiv Dutta, hereby certify pursuant to 18 U.S.C.  1350 adopted pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 that:
/s/ RAJIV DUTTA
Rajiv Dutta
Chief Financial Officer
The foregoing certifications are being furnished solely pursuant to 18 U.S.C.  1350 and are not being filed as part of this report or as a separate disclosure document.
