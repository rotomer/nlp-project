Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
As previously disclosed in a Form 8-K filed on May 2, 2006, Maynard Webb, eBay Inc.'s Chief Operating Officer, plans to retire from the company in August 2006. On June 13, 2006, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of eBay Inc. approved the partial participation of Mr. Webb in the 2006 annual component of the eBay Incentive Plan. Under the terms approved by the Compensation Committee, eBay will pay to Mr. Webb 50% of the amount he would have received with respect to the 2006 annual component of the eBay Incentive Plan had he remained eligible to receive payment in 2007 with respect to the 2006 annual component, less any applicable withholding taxes.
