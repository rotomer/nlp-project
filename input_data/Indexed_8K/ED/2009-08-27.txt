Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On August 27, 2009, Consolidated Edison Company of New York, Inc. announced that its Board of Trustees elected Craig S. Ivey as President of the company. Mr. Ivey's election is effective November 1, 2009, upon the departure of the company's current President, Louis L. Rana, following 40 years of service. Mr. Ivey, 46, joins the company from Dominion Resources Services Company, where he was Senior Vice President, Transmission and Distribution since 2007, Senior Vice President, Distribution from 2006 to 2007, and Vice President, Electric Operations from 2001 to 2006.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, each registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: August 27, 2009
