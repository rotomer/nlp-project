Item 8.01 Other Events On June 28, 2005, The Estee Lauder Companies Inc. (the "Company") received a notice of exercise of the put right from the holder of all of the outstanding shares of the Series A Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock of the Company (the "Series A Preferred Stock"). Pursuant to the terms of the Certificate of Designation for the Series A Preferred Stock, the Company must purchase the 68,398 outstanding shares of Series A Preferred Stock within 120 days after receipt of such notice (i.e., on or before October 26, 2005) at a price per share of $1,000 (for a total of $68,398,000) plus any cumulative and unpaid dividends thereon.
