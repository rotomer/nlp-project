HOPKINTON, Mass.  February 27, 2006  EMC Corporation (NYSE:EMC), the world leader in information management and storage, today announced that it intends to redeem the $125 million 4.5% Senior Convertible Notes due April 1, 2007 it assumed in connection with the acquisition of Documentum, Inc. in December 2003. The notes will be redeemed on or after April 3, 2006 at a price equal to 100.9% of the face value of the notes, plus accrued but unpaid interest.
