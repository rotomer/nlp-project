Item 1.01.    Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
On March 29, 2006, pursuant to the EMC Corporation Executive Incentive Bonus Plan, the Compensation Committee of the EMC Corporation ("EMC") Board of Directors established the performance goals for Joseph M. Tucci's quarterly bonus opportunity of $90,000 for the second quarter of 2006. Actual bonus payments will be based on achievement of Mr. Tucci's goals and EMC's overall bonus funding level for that quarter. Mr. Tucci's goals include financial measures such as revenue and earnings targets, as well as non-financial measures such as product development and strategic initiatives.
