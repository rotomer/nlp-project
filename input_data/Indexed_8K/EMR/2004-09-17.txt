Item 5.02.    Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
        (b)           On September 17, 2004, Charles F. Knight announced his resignation as a member of the Board of Directors and all committees of the Board of which he was a member, as Chairman of the Board, as an officer and as an employee of Emerson Electric Co. David Farr, Emerson's chief executive officer, has been elected to the additional position of chairman of the board to fill the vacancy created by Mr. Knight's resignation.
