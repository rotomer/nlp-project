Emerson GAAP Underlying Orders  3 Month Summary
November 04 Order Comments:
Solid orders continued across the company with favorable currency exchange rates contributing approximately 2.5 to 3 percentage points of the total +5% to +10% increase.
Process Management experienced strong orders in measurement and analytical instruments, valves and power & water solutions, with strength in emerging markets.
Overall orders in Industrial Automation were strong led by continued capital spending and industrial demand in North America.
Orders in Network Power improved with strength in the systems business and strength in Asia along with solid demand in North America.
Climate Technologies orders continue to be impacted by slower growth in the U.S. residential air conditioning market and tougher prior year comparisons.
Orders in the Appliance and Tools segment stayed strong in the storage business and were mixed in the motors, appliance, and tools businesses.
