INFORMATION TO BE INCLUDED IN THE REPORT
On July 11, 2012, Ensco plc announced that James W. Swent III, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, was promoted to Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective immediately. In connection with his promotion, Mr. Swent's base salary will be increased to $525,000, and his target annual cash incentive award will be increased to 75% of base salary. Mr. Swent also will receive a restricted share award with a grant-date fair value of $190,000 and a performance unit award with a target value of $190,000.
