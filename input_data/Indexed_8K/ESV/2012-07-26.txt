Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition
On July 25, 2012, Ensco plc issued a press release announcing its second quarter 2012 results. Due to a clerical error, the press release reported total rig utilization for the quarter ended June 30, 2012 at 81% and total rig utilization for the quarter ended March 31, 2012 at 87%. Actual total rig utilization for the quarter ended June 30, 2012 was 85% and actual total rig utilization for the quarter ended March 31, 2012 was 83%.
The information furnished in this Item 2.02 shall not be deemed filed for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall such information be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing.
