Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
Effective October 21, 2004, the Board of Directors elected Mr. Daryl G. Brewster as a Director. Mr. Brewster will be a Class I member of the Board and will stand for election at the next Annual Shareholders Meeting in May of 2005. The Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Board of Directors is currently reviewing all committee assignments and in the near future the Committee will likely invite Mr. Brewster to serve on at least one of the Board's committees.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Company has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
