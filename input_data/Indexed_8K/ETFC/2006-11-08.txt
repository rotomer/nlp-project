Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
Effective November 7, 2006, the Board of Directors expanded the number of members of the Board to ten and elected Mr. R. Jarrett Lilien, the Company's President and Chief Operating Officer, as a Director. Mr. Lilien will be a Class II member of the Board and will stand for election at the next Annual Shareholders Meeting in May of 2007. Mr. Lilien will not serve on any Committee of the Board.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Company has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
