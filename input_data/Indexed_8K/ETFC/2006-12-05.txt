Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; The Compensatory Arrangements of Principal Officers.
Effective December 1, 2006, the Board of Directors elected Mr. Stephen H. Willard, a current member of the Board, as Vice Chairman, a position authorized in the Company's By-Laws. In this capacity, Mr. Willard will receive, in addition to his current compensation as Director, an annual retainer of $10,000 (identical to that paid to Committee Chairpersons) and $2,500 for each meeting of the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee attended, instead of the $2,000 paid as a regular member. This compensation structure will become effective January 1, 2007.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Company has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
