On March 10, 2010, at an investor conference, E*TRADE Financial Corporation (the "Company") gave an update on its CEO search. Robert Druskin, the Company's interim Chief Executive Officer, stated that the preferred candidate previously mentioned by the Company is no longer under consideration and that the Company's Board of Directors' search committee continues to move forward and is focused on identifying the very best person to lead the Company. Mr. Druskin also stated that he is actively involved in the business and remains fully committed to serving as interim Chief Executive Officer until the Company completes the process and a permanent CEO is in place.
