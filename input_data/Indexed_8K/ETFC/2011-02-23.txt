Citadel's Holdings of E*TRADE Common Stock and Convertible Debentures
Citadel and its affiliates will convert convertible debentures into 2,217,538 shares of common stock in order to sell such shares in the offering, assuming no exercise of the underwriter's option to purchase additional shares. Citadel and its affiliates have also committed to convert additional convertible debentures following the completion of the offering such that they will own 9.9% of E*TRADE's outstanding common stock or 19.7% of E*TRADE's common stock assuming conversion of debentures held by Citadel following completion of the offering.
