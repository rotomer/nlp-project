E*TRADE Financial Corporation (the "Company") is presenting today at the KBW Securities Brokerage & Market Structure Conference. A copy of the Company's presentation is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein. During the presentation, the Company will discuss its November 2012 month-to-date statistics for Daily Average Revenue Trades (DARTs) and customer net securities purchases. For the period through November 27, the Company reports DARTs of approximately 132,000, a six percent increase from October and a seven percent decrease from November 2011. The Company also reports that for the period through November 26, customers were net buyers of approximately $270 million in securities. The Company intends to issue its standard monthly activity report for the full period, including loan performance data for the October and November periods, on or about December 19, 2012.
The information appearing in this Item 7.01 shall not be deemed "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange act of 1934, or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section.
