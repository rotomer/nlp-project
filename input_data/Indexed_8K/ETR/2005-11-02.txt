Item 5.02 Election of Director
(Entergy Corporation)
Pursuant to Item 5.02(d)(3), on October 28, 2005, the Board of Directors of Entergy Corporation elected the new members of the Board of Directors to the following committees: Gary W. Edwards to the Corporate Governance Committee and the Personnel Committee; Stuart L. Levenick to the Audit Committee and the Finance Committee; and W. J. "Billy" Tauzin to the Corporate Governance Committee and the Finance Committee.
