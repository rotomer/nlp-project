Item 5.02(b)   Departure of Director
On January 27, 2006, Claiborne P. Deming and Kathleen A. Murphy notified the Entergy Corporation Board of Directors of their decision not to stand for re-election to the Board. Mr. Deming and Ms. Murphy will continue to serve as directors of Entergy Corporation until the 2006 annual meeting of stockholders, which is expected to be held on May 12, 2006.
