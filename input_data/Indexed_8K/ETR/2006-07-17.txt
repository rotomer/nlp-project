Item 5.02(b)   Departure of Director
On July 13, 2006, Robert v.d. Luft, the non-executive chairman of the Board of Directors of Entergy Corporation, announced his decision to retire effective August 1, 2006.  Effective upon Mr. Luft's retirement, J. Wayne Leonard, the Chief Executive Officer of Entergy Corporation, will become Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Entergy Corporation.
