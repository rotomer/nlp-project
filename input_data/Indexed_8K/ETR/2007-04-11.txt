Item 2.03 Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation.
As more fully-described in its report on Form 8-K filed on June 1, 2005, and in Note 4 to the financial statements and Item 9B in its annual report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2006, Entergy Corporation has two revolving credit facilities available to it. Entergy Corporation from time to time has borrowed under the facilities and has also from time to time issued letters of credit against the borrowing capacity of the facilities. On April 11, 2007, Entergy Corporation borrowed $380 million under one of the facilities. Item 8.01 Other Events.
As more fully-described in Management's Discussion and Analysis, Liquidity and Capital Resources, in Entergy Corporation's annual report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2006, Entergy's Non-Utility Nuclear business reached an agreement in July 2006 to purchase Consumers Energy Company's 798 MW Palisades nuclear energy plant located near South Haven, Michigan for $380 million. Entergy's Non-Utility Nuclear business completed the purchase of the plant on April 11, 2007.
