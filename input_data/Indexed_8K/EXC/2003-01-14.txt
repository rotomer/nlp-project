Item 9. Regulation FD Disclosure On January 15, 2002, John W. Rowe, Chairman and CEO, met with investors. During these meetings, Mr. Rowe confirmed: o Exelon's earnings guidance for 2002, which was previously disclosed in an 8-K dated October 21, 2002, of $4.65 to $4.80 per share, and o Exelon's earnings guidance for 2003, which was previously disclosed in an 8-K dated November 4, 2002, of $4.70 to $5.00 per share.
