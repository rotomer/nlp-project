UNITED STATES SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION Washington, DC 20549 FORM 8-K/A CURRENT REPORT Pursuant to Section 13 or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 April 26, 2004 (Date of earliest event reported)
Item 12. Results of Operations and Financial Condition On April 26, 2004, Exelon Corporation (Exelon) announced via Form 8-K Exelon's results for its first quarter ended March 31, 2004. The April 26 Form 8-K included within page 10 of the Earnings Release Attachments filed as Exhibit 99 a table under the heading "2004 Earnings Guidance - April through December" that presented the forward around-the-clock market prices for that period. The numbers reported in the April 26 Form 8-K were not updated from Exelon's January 28, 2004 earnings release. This Form 8-K/A has been filed to correct the projected around-the-clock market prices disclosed in the table. This correction does not affect any other part of the earnings release or its attachments. The updated around-the-clock market prices are as follows: 2004 Earnings Guidance - April through December Around-the-clock Market Prices ($/MWh) PJM $ 42.00 MAIN 31.00 NEPOOL 53.50 Gas Prices ($/Mmbtu) Henry Hub $ 6.00 This combined Form 8-K is being furnished separately by Exelon, Commonwealth Edison Company (ComEd), PECO Energy Company (PECO) and Exelon Generation Company, LLC (Generation) (Registrants).
