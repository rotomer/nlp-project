Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
As disclosed in the Current Report on Form 8-K filed on April 27, 2005, on April 26, 2005, the board of directors of Exelon Corporation elected Thomas J.
