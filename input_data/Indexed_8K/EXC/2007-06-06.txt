Section 7  Regulation FD
Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
On June 6-7, 2007, Exelon Corporation (Exelon) will meet with investors and participate in the Citigroup Power, Gas and Utilities Conference in Charleston, South Carolina.  During these discussions, Exelon will reaffirm its adjusted (non-GAAP) operating earnings guidance ranges for 2007 for Exelon, Exelon Generation Company, LLC (Generation), Commonwealth Edison Company (ComEd) and PECO Energy Company (PECO).  Exelon will use handouts consisting of materials previously filed on the Current Reports on Form 8-K on May 22, 2007 and May 29, 2007 in connection with the Edison Electric Institute Annual Finance Committee Meeting and the Deutsche Bank Energy and Utilities Conference, respectively.  The pages in the handouts which contain credit rating information have been updated to reflect the downgrade in ComEds short-term and long-term security ratings from Standard & Poors Corporation (S&P) on June 1, 2007.  ComEds S&P corporate credit rating, senior secured debt, senior unsecured debt and commercial paper ratings are BB, BBB-, B+ and B, respectively.
 
This combined Form 8-K is being furnished separately by Exelon, Generation, ComEd and PECO (Registrants).  Information contained herein relating to any individual registrant has been furnished by such registrant on its own behalf.  No registrant makes any representation as to information relating to any other registrant.
