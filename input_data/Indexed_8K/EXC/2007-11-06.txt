Section 8  Other Events
Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
As previously disclosed, on November 5-6, 2007, Exelon Corporation (Exelon) participated in the Edison Electric Institute Conference.  The presentation slides and handouts used at the conference were furnished in a Current Report on Form 8-K filed on November 5, 2007. During a webcast presentation on November 6, 2007, Exelon provided a preliminary 2008 adjusted (non-GAAP) operating earnings guidance range of $4.00 - $4.40/share. A qualitative description of the factors considered in adjusted (non-GAAP) operating earnings is contained in the appendix of the previously-furnished presentation slides and handouts.  Exelon stated that it would provide formal 2008 earnings guidance at Exelon's Investor Conference on December 19, 2007.
