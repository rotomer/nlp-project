Section 8  Other Events
Item 8.01. Other Events.
On October 29, 2012, Exelon Corporation (Exelon) announced that it has rescheduled its third quarter earnings call for Thursday, November 1 at 10:00 AM ET (9:00 AM CT) in order to focus on Hurricane Sandy and its potential impacts on the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic states. The call previously had been scheduled for Wednesday, October 31.
The call-in number for Exelon's third quarter earnings call in the U.S. and Canada is 800-690-3108, and the international call-in number is 973-935-8753. If requested, the conference ID number is 33703408. Media representatives are invited to participate on a listen-only basis. The call will be web-cast and archived on Exelon's Web site: .
