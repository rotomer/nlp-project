SEATTLE, WASHINGTON  January 25, 2011, Expeditors International of Washington, Inc. (NASDAQ:EXPD), the international freight forwarding and logistics company, said that on January 20, 2011, the company and its Hong Kong subsidiary, Expeditors Hong Kong Ltd. (together, "Expeditors") received an additional request for information issued by the European Commission ("EC") in connection with an ongoing investigation of freight forwarders. As previously indicated, Expeditors intends to respond to the EC's request.
