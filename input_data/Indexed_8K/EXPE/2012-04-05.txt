On April 3, 2012, the Board of Directors of Expedia, Inc. established the date of the Expedia 2012 annual meeting of stockholders. The annual meeting will be held on Tuesday, June 5, 2012. Any stockholders who wish to have a proposal considered for inclusion in Expedia's proxy materials for presentation at the 2012 annual meeting of stockholders must ensure that their proposal is received by Expedia no later than April 13, 2012 at its principal executive offices at 333 108th Avenue N.E., Bellevue, Washington 98004, Attention: Secretary. The proposal must be made in accordance with the provisions of Rule 14a-8 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended. Stockholders who intend to present a proposal at the 2012 annual meeting of stockholders without inclusion of the proposal in Expedia's proxy materials must provide notice of such proposal to Expedia at its principal executive offices, such notice to be received no later than April 13, 2012. In order to comply with Securities and Exchange Commission rules, Expedia intends to commence the printing of its proxy materials the week following the April 13 deadline for receipt of stockholder proposals. Expedia reserves the right to reject, rule out of order or take other appropriate action with respect to any proposal that does not comply with these and other applicable requirements.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
