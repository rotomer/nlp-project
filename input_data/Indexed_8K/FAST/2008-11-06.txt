Robert A. Hansen, who is a director of Fastenal Company (Fastenal), has informed us that he plans to retire from the Fastenal Board of Directors (Board) effective as of the end of the next Annual Meeting of Shareholders to be held in April 2009. Until his resignation, Mr. Hansen will continue to serve as a member of our Audit Committee and our Compensation Committee. Mr. Hansen, an associate professor of marketing and logistics management with the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota, has served on Fastenal's Board since 1999.
Mr. Robert Kierlin, our Chairman of the Board was quoted, "We would like to thank Mr. Hansen for his decade of service to Fastenal, in particular, his advice and guidance relating to our extensive training and leadership development programs".
Fastenal is currently identifying potential director candidates to fill the open seat on the Board and is also considering adding one additional member to the Board.
