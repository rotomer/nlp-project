On February 5, 2009, Henry K. McConnon, who is a director of Fastenal Company (Fastenal), has informed us that he plans to retire from the Fastenal Board of Directors (Board) effective immediately. Mr. McConnon, the President of Wise Eyes, Inc., an eyeglass retailer and wholesaler, has served on Fastenal's Board since our incorporation in 1968.
There were no disagreements between Mr. McConnon and Fastenal.
