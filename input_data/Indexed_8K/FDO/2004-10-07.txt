For Immediate Release
 
 
 
- Family Dollar Stores, Inc. (NYSE: FDO), a discount store chain operating 5,480 stores in 44 states, reported sales for the five-week period ended October 2, 2004, of approximately $510.6 million, or 10.1% above sales of $463.6 million for the similar period in the prior fiscal year.  Sales in existing stores for the five-week period ended October 2, 2004, increased approximately 1.5% above existing store sales for the similar period in the prior fiscal year, including an increase of approximately 4.5% in sales of hardlines and a decrease of approximately 8.6% in sales of softlines.
MATTHEWS, NC, October 7, 2004
 
The 5,480 stores in operation include 18 stores that were opened during the five-week period ended October 2, 2004.
 
The Company's current plan is for sales in existing stores in the four-week period ending October 30, 2004, to increase in the 1% to 3% range.
