Exhibit 99 Family Dollar Reports June Sales MATTHEWS, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--July 6, 2006--Family Dollar Stores, Inc. (NYSE:FDO) reported that net sales for the five weeks ended July 1, 2006, increased 9.5% to approximately $620.5 million from $566.9 million for the five weeks ended July 2, 2005. Comparable store sales for the five-week period increased approximately 3.6%. The Company had 6,095 stores as of July 1, 2006, including 26 new stores opened in the fiscal June period. About Family Dollar Stores, Inc. With more than 6,000 stores in a 44-state area ranging as far northwest as Idaho, northeast to Maine, southeast to Florida, and southwest to Arizona, Family Dollar is one of the fastest growing discount store chains in the United States. Family Dollar has provided value-conscious consumers with competitive prices at neighborhood stores for more than forty-six years. Offering a consistent selection of name-brand and good quality merchandise in an attractive and convenient shopping environment, the Company is focused on continuing to meet the needs of shoppers looking for excellent value.
