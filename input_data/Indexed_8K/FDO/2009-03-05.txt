Family Dollar Second Quarter Comparable Store Sales Increase 6.4%
Company Raises Guidance for Second Quarter Earnings
MATTHEWS, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--March 5, 2009--Family Dollar Stores, Inc. (NYSE: FDO) reported that net sales for the second quarter ended February 28, 2009, increased 8.7% to approximately $1.992 billion compared with $1.833 billion for the second quarter ended March 1, 2008. Sales in the quarter were driven by double-digit sales growth of consumable merchandise; sales of apparel were soft. Comparable store sales for the quarter increased 6.4%. The Company had 6,643 stores as of February 28, 2009, including 57 new stores opened during the second quarter.
Outlook
Reflecting sales results for the second quarter, the Company now expects earnings for the second quarter of fiscal 2009 will be between $0.59 and $0.61 per diluted share. The Company expects to report second quarter financial results on April 8, 2009, before the market opens.

About Family Dollar
Operating small store locations, Family Dollar is one of the fastest growing discount retail chains in the United States. Family Dollar offers a core assortment of name-brand and quality consumable merchandise supplemented by fashion and seasonal merchandise at everyday low prices.
Beginning with one store in 1959 in Charlotte, North Carolina, the Company currently operates more than 6,600 stores in 44 states. Family Dollar Stores, Inc., a Fortune 500 company, is based in Matthews, North Carolina, just outside of Charlotte and is a publicly held company with common stock traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol FDO.
