Exhibit 99.1
        MEMPHIS, Tenn., June 25, 2002FedEx Corp (NYSE: FDX) announced that it is comfortable with the current First Call consensus estimate for fiscal 2003 of $2.77, representing a 16% year-over-year earnings per share increase.
        "We have achieved record earnings and have doubled our net income in our most recent quarter," said Frederick W. Smith, chairman, president and chief executive officer. "FedEx has strong momentum going into fiscal 2003 and we are very optimistic about our ability to improve the return on invested capital, operating profit margins, earnings per share and to achieve strong cash flow."
