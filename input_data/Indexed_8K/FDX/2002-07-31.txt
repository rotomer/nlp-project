Item 9.    Regulation FD Disclosure.
        On July 22, 2002, Frederick W. Smith and Alan B. Graf, Jr., the principal executive officer and principal financial officer, respectively, of FedEx Corporation, each filed with the Commission the sworn written statements required by the Commission's June 27, 2002 Order Requiring the Filing of Sworn Statements Pursuant to Section 21(a)(1) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (File No. 4-460). A copy of the statements, along with the accompanying cover letter to the Commission, is attached as Exhibit 99.1.
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
