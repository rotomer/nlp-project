SECTION 5  CORPORATE GOVERNANCE AND MANAGEMENT
Effective February 28, 2010, Douglas G. Duncan will retire from his position as President and Chief Executive Officer of FedEx Freight Corporation, a wholly owned subsidiary of FedEx Corporation. Mr. Duncan's successor has not yet been appointed.
