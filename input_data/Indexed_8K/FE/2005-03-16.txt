Item 8.01 Other Events
As disclosed by FirstEnergy Corp. and its wholly owned subsidiary, Jersey Central Power & Light Company (Company), in their respective Forms 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2004, employees represented by System Council U-3 began a strike against the Company on December 8, 2004. The labor agreement between the Company and System Council U-3 originally expired on October 31, 2003 but was extended several times and ultimately expired on December 7, 2004.
On March 15, 2005, the Company announced that it was notified by IBEW System Council U-3 leadership that the stipulated settlement reached with the Company on March 12, 2005 was ratified by represented employees. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and incorporated by reference.

EXHIBIT 99.1
Morristown, NJ - Jersey Central Power & Light (JCP&L) announced today that it was notified by System Council U-3 leadership that the stipulated settlement reached with the company on Saturday, March 12, was ratified by represented employees. As a result, employees will return to work tomorrow.
JCP&L serves one million customers in 13 New Jersey counties.
