Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure
FirstEnergy is providing further clarity in connection with the Webcast of its presentation at the Edison Electric Institute Financial Conference on November 8, 2005. FirstEnergy reiterates that its estimated long-term earnings growth rate remains at 3-4%, its estimated dividend growth rate remains at 4-5% and its target dividend payout ratio remains at 50-60%.
