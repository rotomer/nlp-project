Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
On August 15, 2007, the Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a Confirmatory Order to FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company (FENOC), a wholly owned subsidiary of FirstEnergy Corp., regarding commitments made by FENOC in response to the NRCs Demand for Information (DFI). On May 14, 2007, the NRC issued a DFI to FENOC regarding the evaluation of two reports associated with the reactor pressure vessel head corrosion at the Davis-Besse Nuclear Power Station. FENOC responded to the DFI on June 13, 2007 and submitted supplemental information regarding its response to the NRC on July 16, 2007.
