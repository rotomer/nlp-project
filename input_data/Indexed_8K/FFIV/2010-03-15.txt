Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders
(a) On March 11, 2010, F5 Networks, Inc. (the "Company") held its annual meeting of shareholders.
(b) At the meeting, shareholders voted on the election of three Class II directors to hold office until the annual meeting of shareholders for fiscal year end 2012 and until their successors are elected and qualified and ratification of the selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the Company's independent auditor for fiscal year 2010. A total of 73,127,307 shares of the Company's common stock outstanding and entitled to vote were present at the meeting in person or by proxy. All three nominees for director were elected and the ratification of the selection of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the independent auditors for fiscal year 2010 was approved.
The voting results were as follows:
1. To elect three Class II directors to hold office until the annual meeting of shareholders for fiscal year end 2012 and until their successors are elected and qualified
