As the company continues with its restructuring and repositioning strategy, particularly the focus on Tennessee banking and capital markets, the Emerging National Businesses will be discontinued as a separate business area of the company. As a result, the company announced on July 24, 2008, that Sarah L. Meyerrose, President  Emerging National Businesses, will be leaving the company. She will no longer be an executive officer effective at the close of business July 31, 2008. Ms. Meyerrose is an executive officer named as such in the company's 2008 proxy statement.
