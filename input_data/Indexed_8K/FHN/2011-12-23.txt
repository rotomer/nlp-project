ITEM 8.01.      Other Events.
 
As previously reported on a Current Report filed on October 20, 2011, the Board of Directors (Board) of First Horizon National Corporation (the Company) elected Scott M. Niswonger to the Board on October 17, 2011. Mr. Niswonger also was elected to the Board of Directors of First Tennessee Bank National Association (the Bank), the Companys subsidiary. At the time of his election Mr. Niswonger was appointed to serve on the Trust Committee of the Banks Board. On December 23, 2011, Mr. Niswonger additionally was appointed to serve on the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committees of the Companys Board and of the Banks Board. As previously reported, Mr. Niswonger is expected to stand for election to the Companys Board at the 2012 annual meeting of shareholders.
