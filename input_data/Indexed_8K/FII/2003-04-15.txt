(NYSE: FII), one of the nation's largest investment management companies, today announced that Standard & Poor's will add the company to the S&P 500 Index after the close of trading on a date to be announced by Standard & Poor's. The full text of the Standard & Poor's announcement is available at www.standardandpoors.com. Federated will report full financial and operational results for Q1 2003 on April 23, 2003. In order to provide timely and relevant data to the market in the period between the announcement and Federated's planned earnings release date, the company also announced today that it expects earnings per diluted share (EPS) to be in the range of $0.42 - $0.44 for the three months ended March 31, 2003. Federated Investors, Inc. is one of the largest investment managers in the United States, managing approximately $196 billion in assets as of March 31, 2003.
