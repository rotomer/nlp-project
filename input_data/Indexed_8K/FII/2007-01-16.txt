ITEM 2.01 COMPLETION OF ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS.
On January 9, 2007, Federated Investors, Inc. (Federated) acquired the remaining 11 percent of outstanding equity interests in MDTA LLC (MDTA), the first 89 percent of which were acquired by Federated on July 14, 2006 pursuant to the terms of the Sale, Purchase and Put/Call Agreement between Federated, HBSS Acquisition Corporation, MDTA and the equity interest holders of MDTA (the Agreement). The remaining 11 percent of equity interests were held by various MDTA employees (the Sellers). On January 2, 2007, the Sellers exercised their rights under the put option described in the Agreement. Federated paid the Sellers approximately $8 million on January 9, 2007 in connection with the closing of this purchase.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
