Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
(a) Phillip B. Lassiter resigned from the Board of Directors of Fidelity National Information Services, Inc. ("FIS"), effective as of July 1, 2006.
(b) On July 1, 2006, Robert M. Clements was elected to the Board of Directors of FIS. Mr. Clements is the Chairman and CEO of EverBank Financial Corp., the holding company for EverBank. Mr. Clements joined EverBank in 1994 and has served as the President and CEO of EverBank Financial Corporation since its formation in 1997. Prior to joining EverBank, Mr. Clements was a Vice President of Merrill Lynch & Co., where he was a member of the firm's leveraged buyout group. Mr. Clements was previously elected to the Board of Directors of Fidelity National Title Group, Inc. ("FNT"), an affiliate of FIS, in April 2006, but will resign from the FNT Board to join the FIS Board. Mr. Clements was appointed to the audit committee of the Board of Directors of FIS.
