Item 2.03 Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant
As previously announced, on April 23, 2012, Fifth Third Bancorp ("Fifth Third") entered into an accelerated share repurchase transaction with Goldman, Sachs & Co. ("Goldman") pursuant to which Fifth Third would purchase approximately $75 million of its outstanding common stock. Fifth Third is repurchasing the shares of its common stock as part of its previously announced 30 million share repurchase program, which had approximately 19 million shares remaining.
On May 30, 2012, Fifth Third was notified by Goldman that effective Tuesday, May 29, 2012, they had finished purchasing shares under the April 23 agreement. A total of 4,838,710 shares were repurchased upon execution of the agreement, and an additional 631,986 were repurchased upon completion of the agreement. The additional shares are expected to settle on June 1, 2012. In total, 5,470,696 shares are being repurchased at an average price of $13.7094.
Upon completion of the accelerated share repurchase transaction, Fifth Third will have remaining authority to repurchase approximately 14 million shares under its previously announced share repurchase program.
Goldman and certain of its affiliates have performed, and in the future may perform, various financial advisory and other services for Fifth Third and Fifth Third's affiliates for which they have received, and may in the future receive, customary fees and expenses.
