Item 8.01.             Other Events.
 
On November 1, 2012, Fluor Corporation announced that the Board of Directors had declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.16 per share on the Company's common stock (the "Dividend"), payable on January 3, 2013 to the shareholders of record on December 3, 2012.  On November 28, 2012, the Board approved a revised payment date of December 26, 2012 (as opposed to January 3, 2013) for the Dividend, which will continue to be payable to the shareholders of record on December 3, 2012.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
