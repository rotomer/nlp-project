Item 5.02. Departure of Directors of Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
     On October 13, 2004, Richard J. Guiltinan, Jr. notified the Company of his plans to leave the Company. Mr. Guiltinan said that he intends to work closely with Mark A. Blinn, who was appointed the Company's Vice President and Chief Financial Officer last week, on an orderly transition. Mr. Blinn will also serve as the Company's Chief Accounting Officer until a successor to Mr. Guiltinan is named.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
Date: October 18, 2004
