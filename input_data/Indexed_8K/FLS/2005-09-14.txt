Item 1.02. Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.
As reported by Flowserve Corporation (the "Company") on Form 8-K filed August 18, 2005, the Company delivered a notice of redemption to the trustee for the Company's and Flowserve Finance B.V.'s 12-1/4% Senior Subordinated Notes due 2010 (the "Senior Subordinated Notes") pursuant to which the Company irrevocably called all outstanding Senior Subordinated Notes for redemption. On September 12, 2005, the Senior Subordinated Notes were redeemed in accordance with the notice of redemption. Approximately $188.5 million and 65 million of principal was outstanding under the Senior Subordinated Notes. The Senior Subordinated Note holders received 106.125% of the principal amount held, plus accrued interest to the redemption date. As of September 12, 2005, the indentures for the Senior Subordinated Notes, dated as of August 8, 2000 among the Company, certain of its subsidiaries and The Bank of New York, as trustee were terminated. The information set forth under Items 1.01 and 1.02 of the Company's Form 8-K filed August 18, 2005 regarding the redemption of the Senior Subordinated Notes is hereby incorporated by reference into this Item 1.02.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
