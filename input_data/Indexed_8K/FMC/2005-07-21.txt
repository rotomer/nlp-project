FMC Corporation  News Release
 
 
FMC Corporation Completes Redemption of Notes
 
 FMC Corporation (NYSE: FMC) announced today that it has completed the previously announced redemption of the entire $355 million aggregate principal amount of 10.25 percent Senior Secured Notes due 2009 (the "Notes"). Pursuant to the terms of the Notes and the related indenture, FMC paid a prepayment premium of $44 million. This amount, along with the write-off of unamortized financing costs, will result in recognition of debt extinguishment expense of approximately $56 million in the third quarter of 2005.
PHILADELPHIA, July 21, 2005
 
FMC Corporation is a diversified chemical company serving agricultural, industrial and consumer markets globally for more than a century with innovative solutions, applications and quality products. The company employs approximately 5,000 people throughout the world and operates its businesses in three segments: Agricultural Products, Specialty Chemicals and Industrial Chemicals.
