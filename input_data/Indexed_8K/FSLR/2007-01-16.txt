Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
     On January 9, 2007, First Solar, Inc. ("First Solar") announced that it had amended four of its existing long-term supply contracts. The terms of the amendments increase the total volume of modules to be sold by First Solar between 2009 and 2012 by an aggregate of 264MW.
     The amendments relate to the following long-term supply contracts:
     (i) Framework Agreement on the Sale and Purchase of Solar Modules dated April 10, 2006, between First Solar GmbH and Blitzstrom GmbH, which was amended by the parties thereto on December 21, 2006;
     (ii) Framework Agreement on the Sale and Purchase of Solar Modules dated April 5, 2006, between First Solar GmbH and Gehrlicher Umweltschonende Energiesysteme GmbH, which was amended by the parties thereto on December 22, 2006;
     (iii) Framework Agreement on the Sale and Purchase of Solar Modules dated April 9, 2006, among First Solar GmbH, Juwi Holding AG, JuWi Handels Verwaltungs GmbH & Co KG and juwi solar GmbH, which was amended by the parties thereto on December 22, 2006; and
     (iv) Framework Agreement on the Sale and Purchase of Solar Modules dated March 30, 2006, between First Solar GmbH and Phnix Sonnenstrom AG, which was amended by the parties thereto on December 21, 2006.
     A copy of the press release announcing the four amendments is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.

First Solar, Inc. (Nasdaq: FSLR) today announced that the Company has amended four of its existing long-term supply contracts to increase the total volume of modules to be sold between 2009 and 2012 by an aggregate of 264MW. "The additional volumes under the long-term supply contracts enable our customers to better plan and build their businesses and advance our goal of securing long-term revenue visibility," stated Michael J. Ahearn, Chief Executive Officer of the Company. "We continue to see solid demand and price elasticity as a low cost manufacturer in the industry."
PHOENIX, Ariz.  January 9, 2006 
First Solar, Inc. (Nasdaq: FSLR) manufactures solar modules with an advanced thin film semiconductor process that significantly lowers solar electricity costs. By enabling clean renewable electricity at affordable prices, First Solar provides an economic alternative to peak conventional electricity and the related fossil fuel dependence, greenhouse gas emissions and peak time grid constraints.
