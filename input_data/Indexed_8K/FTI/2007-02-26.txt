On February 13, 2007, FMC Technologies, Inc. (the "Company") issued a press release announcing its financial results for the fiscal quarter and year ended December 31, 2006. After the release was issued, the Company identified an adjustment that affected its unaudited December 31, 2006 balance sheet. The Company has updated its balance sheet to reflect proper classification of the funded status of its pension plans as of December 31, 2006. The adjustment, which reflects a $15.0 million increase in pension plan assets, increases assets by $4.6 million, decreases liabilities by $4.6 million and increases equity by $9.2 million as of December 31, 2006. A copy of the revised balance sheet is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and is incorporated herein by reference.
