(a) Amendment of Articles
On May 15, 2009, stockholders at the Company's annual meeting of stockholders voted in favor of the Board of Directors' proposal to amend, effective immediately, the Company's Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation to increase the number of authorized shares of common stock from 195 million shares to 300 million shares.
A copy of the amendment to the Company's Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation is filed as Exhibit 3.1 to this report and is incorporated herein by reference.
