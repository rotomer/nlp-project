Zarrella Assistant Vice President Vice President Corporate Communications Corporate Development 203.614.5042 203.614.5179 bsmith@czn.com mzarrell@czn.com Citizens Communications Completes Final Utility Sale Stamford, Conn., April 1, 2004 -- Citizens Communications (NYSE:CZN) today announced the completion of the sales of its Vermont Electric division. The total proceeds from these sales, including the proceeds from the sale of Citizens' Vermont electric transmission assets on December 2, 2003 were $21.4 million in cash, subject to post closing adjustments under the agreements. With the closing of these sales, Citizens has completed its utility divestiture program, generating $1.9 billion of proceeds, which exceeds the company's original estimate of $1.8 billion.
