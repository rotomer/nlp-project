The interest rate on the Notes is being reset pursuant to the contractual requirements of the Notes and will be effective on and after August 17, 2004 assuming a successful remarketing of the Notes on August 12, 2004. The securities affected by this announcement are as follows: * 6.75% Senior Notes due 2006 (CUSIP No. 17453BAC5); and * Equity Units (CUSIP No. 17453B200). Any holder that has sold its Equity Units on or prior to August 16, 2004 will not be entitled to receive excess proceeds, if any, from the remarketing, after deducting a remarketing fee to the remarketing agent. This press release does not constitute an offer to sell or the solicitation of an offer to buy any securities.
