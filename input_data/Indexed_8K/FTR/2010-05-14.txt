Frontier Communications Corporation (NYSE: FTR) reported today that its pending acquisition of Verizon Communications (NYSE: VZ) local wireline operations in West Virginia has been approved by the Public Service Commission of West Virginia (PSC), subject to certain conditions.  The acquisition has now received all necessary state approvals.  The parties are awaiting regulatory approval from the Federal Communications Commission (FCC).
 STAMFORD, Conn., May 13, 2010 
Todays approval is a welcome and important step. Our goal is to gain the approval of the FCC so that we can close the transaction and begin bringing its benefits to consumers and businesses, said Maggie Wilderotter, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Frontier.
West Virginia is the ninth and final state to approve the transaction, joining Arizona, Oregon,
California, Illinois, Nevada, Ohio,
South Carolina and Washington.
