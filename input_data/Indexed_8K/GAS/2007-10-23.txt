ITEM: Other Events
 
Item 8.01                       Other Events.
As of October 19, 2007, AGL Resources Inc. (the Company) entered into a Transfer Agent Services Agreement with Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. (Wells Fargo).  Pursuant to the terms of this agreement, which is effective October 29, 2007, Wells Fargo will serve as the Companys transfer agent and administrator for its direct stock purchase and dividend reinvestment plan (also referred to as ResourcesDirect) and employee stock purchase plan, replacing Computershare Investor Services.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
