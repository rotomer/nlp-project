ITEM: Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
On November 5, 2008 AGL Resources Inc. (AGL) announced that Kevin P. Madden will retire as Executive Vice President, External Affairs, effective as of March 1, 2009 (the Retirement Date).  Following the Retirement Date, it is expected that Mr. Madden will provide consulting services to AGL for a period of time. AGL and Mr. Madden  currently are negotiating the terms of a retirement and consulting agreement, which will be disclosed in a future filing on Form 8-K.
