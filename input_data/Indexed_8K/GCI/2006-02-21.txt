Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
This Current Report on Form 8-K/A is being filed by Gannett Co., Inc. (the "Company") to supplement the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K (the "Prior Report"), dated January 12, 2006 and filed on January 17, 2006. The Prior Report announced the election of Marjorie Magner to the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board"), effective February 1, 2006.
 
The Company is filing this Form 8-K/A to disclose that Ms. Magner has been named to the Audit Committee of the Board, effective February 21, 2006.
 
Pursuant to requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
