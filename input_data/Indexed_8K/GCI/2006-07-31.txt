Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
This Current Report on Form 8-K/A is being filed by Gannett Co., Inc. (the "Company") to supplement the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K (the "Prior Report"), dated March 27, 2006 and filed on March 27, 2006. The Prior Report announced the election of Arthur H. Harper to the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board"), effective March 31, 2006.
The Company is filing this Form 8-K/A to disclose that Mr. Harper has been named to the Nominating and Public Responsibility Committee of the Board, effective July 25, 2006.
Pursuant to requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
