Item 8.01 Other Events
On July 22, 2008, General Electric Company (GE) and Mubadala Development Company (Mubadala), headquartered in Abu Dhabi, UAE, announced a framework agreement on a global partnership encompassing a broad range of initiatives including commercial finance, clean energy research and development, aviation, industry and corporate learning.
 
The agreement provides for shared capital commitments to new joint ventures and investment funds.  In addition, Mubadala also plans over time to become a long-term GE shareholder. The expectation is that Mubadala will become one of GEs top ten institutional investors through the open market, as conditions allow.
