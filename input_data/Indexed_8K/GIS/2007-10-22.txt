On October 22, 2007, General Mills, Inc. (the "Company") terminated (effective October 25, 2007) its Credit Agreement, dated as of August 3, 2007 (the "Credit Agreement"), among the Company, the several financial institutions from time to time party to the agreement and JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., as Administrative Agent. The Credit Agreement provided $750 million of revolving credit to the Company and was scheduled to expire in December 2007. Several parties to the Credit Agreement are also parties to the Company's other credit facilities.
