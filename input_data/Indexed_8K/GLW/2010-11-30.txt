Item 8.01 Other Events.
On November 29, 2010, Corning Incorporateds (the "Company") wholly-owned subsidiaries, Corning Hungary Data Services Limited Liability Company and Corning International Corporation, received dividend payments from Samsung Corning Precision Materials Co., Ltd. ("SCP") on SCPs common stock, totaling 1.44 trillion Korean Won, having a U.S. Dollar equivalent of approximately $1.24 billion. The Companys 50% ownership in SCP is beneficially held by these two wholly-owned subsidiaries of the Company.
