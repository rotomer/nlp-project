Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
Corning Incorporated (the "Company") has confirmed that there were no injuries or damages at the Companys facilities in Japan as a result of Fridays earthquake. The earthquake occurred on March 11 at 3:14 p.m. local time. The Company has glass-making facilities in Shizuoka and Sakai City, Japan. Currently, the Companys glass-making operations in Japan are running normally. To date, the Company has not experienced any power outages, nor has it received notification of any power allocations. The Company continues to monitor the status of its customers and suppliers in Japan. This information is furnished pursuant to Item 7.01 and shall not be deemed to be "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that Section, and is not incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act regardless of any general incorporation language in such filing.
