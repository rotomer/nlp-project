Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers On November 23, 2005, Joseph M. DePinto resigned as President of GameStop Corp. (the "Company"), effective November 30, 2005, to become the President and Chief Executive Officer of 7-Eleven, Inc. Until the appointment of a successor, R. Richard Fontaine, the Company's Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, and Daniel A.
