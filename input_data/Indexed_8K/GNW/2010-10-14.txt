As previously reported, Pamela S. Schutz, Executive Vice President  Genworth, intends to retire from Genworth Financial, Inc. (the "Company") in February 2011. On October 13, 2010, the Company determined that in connection with Ms. Schutz's retirement, Patrick B. Kelleher, the Company's Senior Vice President  Chief Financial Officer, will replace Ms. Schutz as President and CEO of the Retirement and Protection segment and as an Executive Vice President of the Company. The Company will finalize the date of the transition prior to Ms. Schutz's retirement date. During this transition, Mr. Kelleher will continue to serve as the Company's Chief Financial Officer as the Company conducts a search for his replacement.
