Item 8.01 Other Events.
Genworth Financial, Inc. (the "Company") today announced that it has repaid $250 million of outstanding borrowings under its five-year revolving credit facilities, paying $125 million of outstanding borrowings under each of the two facilities. Following such repayments, the Company has approximately $240 million of borrowings outstanding under each of these facilities, totaling $480 million. These facilities expire in May and August of 2012.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
