On October 5, 2004, Google Inc. ("Google") made available on the "Investor Relations" section of its corporate web site (www.google.com) certain financial data for fiscal years 2001, 2002 and 2003 and quarterly financial data for the six quarters ended June 30, 2004 and for the first six months of fiscal 2004.
