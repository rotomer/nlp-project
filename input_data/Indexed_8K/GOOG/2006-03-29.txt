Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
The purpose of this Current Report on Form 8-K/A is to amend the Current Report on Form 8-K of Google Inc. (""), filed on December 22, 2005, as amended on March 16, 2006, which described the general terms of a letter agreement (the "") entered into between Time Warner Inc. (""), America Online, Inc. ("") and Google. The Letter Agreement sets forth the general terms upon which Google would, in exchange for $1 billion in cash, acquire a 5% equity interest in AOL through an investment in a wholly owned subsidiary of Time Warner that will own all of the outstanding equity interests in AOL. On March 24, 2006, the parties signed definitive agreements governing this $1 billion investment in AOL and Google expects that the investment will close in the second quarter of 2006.
