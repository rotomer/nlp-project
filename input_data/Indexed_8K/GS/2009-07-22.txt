Item 8.01 Other Events.
On July 22, 2009, The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. (Company) repurchased in full from the United States Department of the Treasury (Treasury) the warrant to purchase 12,205,045 shares of the Company's common stock (Warrant) that was issued to the Treasury pursuant to the Treasury's TARP Capital Purchase Program. The purchase price paid by the Company to the Treasury for the Warrant was $1.1 billion.
