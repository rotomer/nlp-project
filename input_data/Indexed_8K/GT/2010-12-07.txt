Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On October 7, 2010, The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company (the "Company") filed a Current Report on Form 8-K announcing the election of Peter S. Hellman to the Companys Board of Directors. At that time, Mr. Hellman had not yet been appointed to any committees of the Board of Directors. We are filing this Form 8-K/A to provide information on Mr. Hellmans committee assignments. On December 7, 2010, Mr. Hellman was named to the Audit Committee and the Committee on Corporate Responsibility and Compliance of the Board of Directors.
