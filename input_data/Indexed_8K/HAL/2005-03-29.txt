Item 8.01.
 
Other Events.
 
In March 2005, David J. Lesar, chairman, president and CEO, and Albert O. Cornelison, Jr., Executive Vice President & General Counsel, each of Halliburton Company, adopted pre-arranged trading plans intended to satisfy Rule 10b5-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended.
 
Any transactions under each plan will be disclosed through Form 4 filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The planned sales are intended for asset diversification and liquidity.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
