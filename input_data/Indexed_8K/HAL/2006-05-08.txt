Item 8.01.
 
Other Events.
 
Sales under the Rule 10b5-1 trading plan established in August 2005 by David J. Lesar, chairman, president and CEO of Halliburton Company, were completed in April 2006. In May 2006, Mr. Lesar adopted a new Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.
 
 
As with the completed plan, any transactions under the new plan will be disclosed through Form 4 filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The planned sales are intended for asset diversification and liquidity.
