Item 8.01.
 
Other Events.
 
In May 2006, Mr. Lesar, chairman, president and CEO of Halliburton Company established a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan which was to expire on May 4, 2007.  Mr. Lesar terminated such plan and established a new Rule 10b5-1 trading plan effective March 1, 2007.
 
 
Any transactions under the new plan will be disclosed through Form 4 filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The planned sales are intended for asset diversification and liquidity.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
