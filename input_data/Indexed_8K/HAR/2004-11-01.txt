Item 1.01.       Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On October 26, 2004, Harman Canada Co. (an indirect wholly owned subsidiary of Harman International), entered into a share purchase agreement to acquire all of the issued and outstanding shares of QNX Software Systems Ltd. ("QNX") for approximately US$138 million in cash plus the assumption of certain liabilities.  The acquisition is expected to close in the quarter ending December 31, 2004.  Harman International currently licenses certain software products from QNX.
