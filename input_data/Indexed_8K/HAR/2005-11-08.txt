Item 5.02.      Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors;                        Appointment of Principal Officers.
            In order to fill an existing vacancy on the Board, on November 2, 2005, the Board of Directors of Harman International Industries, Incorporated (the Company) elected Gina Harman to serve as a director until the annual meeting of stockholders to be held in 2006.
            Ms. Harman, age 56, is the President of the Companys Harman Consumer Group, a position she has held since 2002.  Ms. Harman was President of the North America operations of the Company's Consumer Group from 1998 to 2002 and has been with the Company since 1985.  Gina Harman is the daughter of Dr. Sidney Harman.  Dr. Harman is the Executive Chairman of the Company.
