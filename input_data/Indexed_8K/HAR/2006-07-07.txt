Item 2.05.     Costs Associated with Exit or Disposal Activities.
          On June 12, 2006, Harman International Industries, Incorporated (the Company) announced that it planned to take restructuring actions intended to increase efficiency in its manufacturing and engineering organizations.
          On June 30, 2006, the Company committed to a plan to implement this restructuring.  The restructuring will be achieved primarily through involuntary workforce reductions.
          The Company expects to incur total costs of approximately $16 million associated with the restructuring, a portion of which will be expensed in the quarter ended June 30, 2006.  Substantially all of such restructuring costs represent cash severance payments.
