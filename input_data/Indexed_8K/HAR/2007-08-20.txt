ITEM: Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
 
On August 13, 2007, Gina Harman resigned from her position as director of Harman International Industries, Incorporated (the Company), effective immediately.
 
On August 13, 2007, following Ms. Harmans resignation, the Board of Directors (the Board) of the Company appointed Dinesh Paliwal to serve as a director and fill the vacancy created by Ms. Harmans resignation.  As previously announced, the Board appointed Mr. Paliwal to the positions of President, Chief Executive Officer and Vice Chairman of the Company, effective July 1, 2007.  The terms of Mr. Paliwals letter agreement and other arrangements with the Company were disclosed on a Form 8-K of the Company filed on May 9, 2007.
