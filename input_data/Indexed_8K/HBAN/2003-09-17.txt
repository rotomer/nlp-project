ITEM 9. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE On September 16, 2003, Huntington Bancshares Incorporated's management made an investor presentation in New York City. The presentation coincided with the 20th anniversary of Huntington's listing on NASDAQ on September 20, 1983. The presentation was held at NASDAQ's MarketSite at 43rd and Broadway in New York. Due to space constraints, on-site attendance was by invitation, however, the entire event was accessible to the public via a live, listen-only Internet Webcast. The presentation began at noon (EDT) and concluded at 5:00 p.m. (EDT). A replay of the webcast, slides for viewing and printing, and a copy of the agenda are all accessible through the Investor Relations section of Huntington's Web site at www.huntington.com.
