Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On July 19, 2005, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Huntington Bancshares Incorporated approved, and the Board of Directors ratified, changes to the compensation rates payable to non-employee directors for services as a director. The annual retainer for all non-employee directors was increased by $8,000 to $35,000. The additional annual retainer payable to the Chair of the Audit Committee was increased by $2,500 to $10,000. A revised compensation schedule for non-employee directors is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference. In addition, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors approved stock option grants in the amount of 10,000 shares to each non-employee director, which is an increase of 2,500 shares over the annual option grant awarded to the non-employee directors for each of the past three years.
