About Hudson City
Hudson City Bancorp, Inc. is the parent company of Hudson City Savings Bank, a well-established community banking institution with a long-standing tradition of service excellence. Hudson City has assets exceeding $29.5 billion, making it the largest savings bank headquartered in New Jersey. Hudson City Savings Bank operates 94 full service branches throughout New Jersey, Suffolk and Richmond (Staten Island) Counties, New York. The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation insures Hudson City Savings' deposits.
