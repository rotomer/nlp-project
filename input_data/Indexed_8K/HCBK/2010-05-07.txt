FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  May 7, 2010
PARAMUS, N.J., May 7, 2010  Hudson City Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ: HCBK), announced today that it will participate in the 2010 Janney Capital Markets Bank and Thrift Conference on Thursday, May 13, 2010. Ronald E. Hermance, Jr., Hudson City's Chairman, President and CEO, will present at the conference at 1:05 PM Eastern Time.
The slide presentation will be available through Hudson City Bancorp, Inc.'s website, until June 30, 2010.
www.hcbk.com
Hudson City Bancorp, Inc. is the parent company of Hudson City Savings Bank, a well-established community banking institution with a long-standing tradition of service excellence. Hudson City is ranked in the top twenty five U.S. financial institutions by asset size and operates a total of 132 branch offices in the New York metropolitan area. The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation insures Hudson City Savings' deposits.
