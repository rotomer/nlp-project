Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement On February 1, 2006 the Compensation and Management Development Committee ("the Committee") of Amerada Hess Corporation ("the Company") approved target bonuses for the five most highly compensated executive officers of the Company for 2006. One-third of the bonus is payable based upon attainment of a specified level of a corporate performance measure, one-third is based upon the attainment of specified metrics for business unit performance, and one-third is discretionary based on individual performance. Payouts may range from 0% to 150% of each component of the target bonus, based on the percentage attainment of the predetermined corporate and business unit performance measures and, with respect to the individual performance component, the committee's determination of an appropriate amount.
