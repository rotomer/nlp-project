Item 5. Other Events. On December 1, 2003, The Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc. announced that it has agreed to acquire CNA Financial Corporation's group life and accident, short-term and long-term disability and certain specialty businesses, excluding group long term care. The definitive purchase price will be set at closing, but based on the surplus as of September 30, 2003 the acquisition will be made for approximately $500 million in cash.
