Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure. On June 23, 2005, The Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc. (the "Company") received a subpoena from the New York Attorney General's Office requesting information relating to purchases of the Company's variable annuity products, or exchanges of other products for the Company's variable annuity products, by New York residents who were 65 or older at the time of the purchase or exchange. The Company intends to cooperate fully with the New York Attorney General's Office in connection with the subpoena.
