Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
The Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc. ("The Hartford") announced today that a registration statement filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission by Hartford Series Fund, Inc. became effective on June 4, 2011. The fund registration statement relates to planned enhancements to The Hartford's Personal Retirement Manager suite of variable annuity products, which The Hartford expects to begin offering in the coming weeks.
The information furnished pursuant to this Item 7.01 shall not be deemed "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the "Exchange Act") or otherwise subject to the liabilities under that Section and shall not be deemed to be incorporated by reference into any filing of the Company under the Securities Act of 1933 or the Exchange Act.
