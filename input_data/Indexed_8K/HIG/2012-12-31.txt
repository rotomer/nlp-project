Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
On December 10, 2012, Hartford Life and Accident Insurance Company ("HLA"), an indirect subsidiary of The Hartford Financial Services Group, Inc. (the "Company"), received regulatory approval to reorganize its Mutual Funds business. This reorganization, which is expected to occur by December 31, 2012, will result in certain subsidiaries supporting the Mutual Funds business being distributed to Hartford Life, Inc., HLA's parent and a direct subsidiary of the Company, via a book value dividend.
