ITEM 8.01.  OTHER INFORMATION
On September 16, 2009, H. J. Heinz Company (the Company) completed the sale of its Kabobs frozen hors doeuvres business within the U.S. Foodservice segment. The Kabobs business had annual sales of approximately $17 million in Fiscal Year 2009. The disposal transaction included one factory in Lake City, Georgia.  As a result of this transaction, the Company will recognize a pretax loss of approximately $15 million ($10 million after-tax), which will be recorded in discontinued operations. The sale is not expected to significantly affect the Companys profit on a continuing operations basis going forward.
