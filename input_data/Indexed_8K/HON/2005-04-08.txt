On April 8, 2005, Marshall N. Carter advised the Company that, in light of his appointment as Chairman of the New York Stock Exchange, he will not stand for re-election to the Company's Board of Directors at the Annual Meeting of Shareowners to be held on April 25, 2005. Mr. Carter has been a member of the Company's Board of Directors since 1999.
 
The authorized number of directors will be reduced from 14 to 13, effective as of the date of cessation of Mr. Carter's service as a director.
