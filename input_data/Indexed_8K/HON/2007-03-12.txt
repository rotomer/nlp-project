MORRIS TOWNSHIP, NJ, March 12, 2007  Honeywell
(NYSE: HON)
today announced the commencement of the public offering of its $400 million 5.30% Senior Notes Due 2017 and its $600 million 5.70% Senior Notes Due 2037 (collectively, the "Notes"). Honeywell intends to use the proceeds of the offering for repayment of outstanding debt and for general corporate purposes.
     
The Notes will be senior unsecured and unsubordinated obligations of Honeywell and will rank equally with all of Honeywell's existing and future senior unsecured debt and senior to all Honeywell's subordinated debt.
     
Banc of America Securities LLC, Barclays Capital Inc., and Citigroup Corporate and Investment Banking are acting as book-runners (each a "Book-Runner") for the public offering of the Notes.
