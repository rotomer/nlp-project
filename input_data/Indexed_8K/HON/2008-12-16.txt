In light of his recent nomination for the position of U.S. Secretary of the Department of Veterans Affairs, General Eric K. Shinseki, U.S. Army (Retired), advised the Board of Directors of Honeywell International Inc. ("Honeywell") on December 12, 2008 that he was resigning from the Board of Directors, effective December 31, 2008. General Shinseki's resignation from Honeywell's Board of Directors does not involve any disagreement on any matter relating to Honeywell's operations, policies or practices.
     
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
