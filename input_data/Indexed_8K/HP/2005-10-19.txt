ITEM 502(b) DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS.
     In a letter to Walter H. Helmerich, III, Chairman of Helmerich & Payne, Inc. ("H&P"), Mr. Francis Rooney, a Director of H&P, respectfully resigned from the Board of Directors of H&P, effective October 17, 2005. Mr. Rooney was nominated by the President of the United States, and confirmed by the United States Senate, to serve as Ambassador to the Holy See. Mr. Rooney's duties will preclude his continued participation on the H&P Board of Directors.
