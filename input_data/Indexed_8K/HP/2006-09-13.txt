ITEM 1.01  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
(a)           On September 11, 2006, Helmerich & Payne, Inc. (the "Company") and the Company's Chairman of the Board, Mr. W. H. Helmerich, III, entered into a Second Amendment to Consulting Services Agreement (the "Second Amendment"), thereby amending that certain Consulting Services Agreement between the two parties dated March 30, 1990.  The Second Amendment increases Mr. Helmerich's annual pay to $170,300.  The Second Amendment becomes effective October 1, 2006 and does not otherwise amend the Consulting Services Agreement.
