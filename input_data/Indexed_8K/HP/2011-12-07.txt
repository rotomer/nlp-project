On December 6, 2011, the Company's Chairman, Mr. W. H. Helmerich, III, notified the Board of Directors of Helmerich & Payne, Inc. (the "Company") that he intends to serve the remainder of his current term and will retire from the Company's Board of Directors effective on the conclusion of the Company's March 7, 2012 annual meeting of stockholders.  Mr. Helmerich has served as Chairman of the Board since 1987 and a Director since 1949.  Mr. Helmerich's decision to retire is not a result of any disagreement with the Company or its management.  Mr. Hans Helmerich, President, Chief Executive Officer and current Director of the Company, will become the Chairman of the Board effective upon the retirement of Mr. W. H. Helmerich, III.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly authorized the undersigned to sign this report on its behalf.
