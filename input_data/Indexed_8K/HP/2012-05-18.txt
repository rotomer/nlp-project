ITEM 8.01  OTHER EVENTS
 
The Board of Directors of the Helmerich & Payne, Inc. (the "Company") previously authorized the repurchase of up to 4,000,000 shares of the Company's common stock pursuant to a stock repurchase program.  Under such a program, share purchases may be made from time to time in the open market or through privately negotiated transactions depending on market conditions, share price, trading volume and other factors, and such purchases, if any, will be made in accordance with applicable insider trading and other securities laws and regulations.  These repurchases may be commenced or suspended at any time or from time to time without prior notice.  No repurchases have been made to date.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly authorized the undersigned to sign this report on its behalf.
