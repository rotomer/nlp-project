Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
On September 15, 2005, H&R Block Services, Inc. ("Block Services"), a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company, entered into the Other Income License Agreement (Products and/or Services) dated September 15, 2005 with Wal-Mart Stores, Inc. ("Wal-Mart") (the "Wal-Mart Agreement").
 
The Wal-Mart Agreement provides for Block Services (or certain of its subsidiaries) to operate H&R Block tax preparation offices in various Wal-Mart stores. The Wal-Mart Agreement expires on May 30, 2007, although either party may terminate the Wal-Mart Agreement between April 16 and May 1 of each year.
