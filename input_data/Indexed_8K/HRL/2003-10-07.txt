SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION
 
 
 
 
Item 5. 
OTHER EVENTS AND REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
 
Union workers from five of the Company's production facilities voted to ratify a new four-year labor contract on September 30, 2003.
 
The United Food and Commercial Workers union represented approximately 3,000 workers from the following five Hormel production facilities: Algona, Iowa; Austin, Minnesota; Beloit, Wisconsin; Fremont, Nebraska; and Tucker, Georgia.  The previous labor contracts expired in early September of the current year.
