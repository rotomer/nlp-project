Section 2  FINANCIAL INFORMATION
 
Item 2.03
Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant
 
On November 22, 2011, Hormel Foods Corporation (the Company) entered into a First Amendment to the Credit Agreement dated as of May 25, 2010 by and among the Company and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, as Administrative Agent (the "Credit Agreement").  The amendment updates terms and conditions, extends the maturity of the agreement to November 22, 2016, adds CoBank, ACB as a new lender, updates commitment percentages and updates definitions throughout the agreement.  A copy of the amendment is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.
