Item 8.01 Other Events.
Effective October 2, 2009, Harris Corporation (the "Company") has revised its executive officer compensation policies such that the Company will no longer provide tax reimbursement or gross-up payments with respect to any perquisites provided to executive officers. Tax reimbursement or tax gross-up payments made pursuant to a plan, policy or arrangement applicable to a broad base of management employees of the Company, such as a relocation or expatriate tax equalization policy, are not affected by the revisions to the Companys executive officer compensation policies.
