(e) On July 23, 2012, Harris Corporation (the "Company") entered into an Amendment to Letter Agreement (the "Amendment") with Howard L. Lance that amends the Letter Agreement, dated October 8, 2012 (the "Letter Agreement"), between the Company and Mr. Lance. The Amendment amends the Letter Agreement by terminating early Mr. Lance's service as a Special Advisor to the Company and the advisory fees payable by the Company to Mr. Lance for such service, in each case as of July 15, 2012 rather than through December 31, 2012.
The summary description of the Amendment contained in this Current Report on Form 8-K is not complete and is qualified in its entirety by, and should be read in conjunction with, the complete text of the Amendment, which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference.
