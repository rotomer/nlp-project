Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
(b)           On February 27, 2007, David A. Jones, the chairman of Hospira's board of directors, informed Hospira that he will retire from the board effective upon the expiration of his term at the 2007 Annual Meeting of Shareholders (the "2007 Annual Meeting").
 
On March 5, 2007, Hospira's board elected Christopher B. Begley, its chief executive officer, as chairman of the board effective upon the 2007 Annual Meeting.   The board also elected Irving W. Bailey II as lead director effective upon the 2007 Annual Meeting.  The size of the board will be reduced from nine to eight members effective upon the 2007 Annual Meeting.
