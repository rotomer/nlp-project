Item 8.01
                                        
Other Events
 
Hospira, Inc. "("Hospira") has change in control agreements with its named executive officers, including its chief executive officer and chief financial officer, as well as Hospira's senior vice presidents. These change in control agreements (or forms thereof) for our named executive officers are filed as exhibits to Hospira's Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2009.  Each of these agreements has an initial term of three years and, at the end of each year, is automatically renewed for an additional three-year term unless Hospira gives the officer notice that the agreement will not be extended.  Hospira's Compensation Committee has determined that the terms of these change in control agreements will not be extended. Accordingly, these change in control agreements will expire pursuant to their terms as of December&nb sp;31, 2012.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
