HOST MARRIOTT CORPORATION CLOSES $350 MILLION PRIVATE
PLACEMENT OF SENIOR NOTES DUE 2012
 
BETHESDA, MD; August 4, 2004  Host Marriott Corporation (NYSE:HMT) announced today the closing of $350 million aggregate principal amount of Senior Notes (the "Notes") bearing interest at a rate of 7% per year, due in 2012, by Host Marriott, L.P., for whom the Company acts as sole general partner. The Notes were offered in a private placement to qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
 
The net proceeds of the offering were approximately $345 million, after deducting discounts, fees and expenses, and will be used to redeem a portion of Host Marriott L.P.'s existing 7
