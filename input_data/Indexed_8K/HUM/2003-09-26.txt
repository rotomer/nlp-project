Item 5.   
           Humana Military Healthcare Services was notified today by the Department of Defense that it was not successful in its bid for the TRICARE program's retail pharmacy contract.
          Under government contract procurement regulations, the Company has the right to appeal the award decision on the retail pharmacy contract.   The Company is currently evaluating this alternative.
          The Company is maintaining its guidance for 2003 and 2004 diluted earnings per common share, as stated in its second quarter 2003 earnings press release dated July 28, 2003.
          Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date:    September 26, 2003
