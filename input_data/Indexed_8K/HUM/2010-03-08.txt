Members of Humana Inc.'s senior management team are scheduled to meet with investors at various times between March 9, 2010 and March 24, 2010. During such meetings, Humana's management expects to reaffirm its guidance of $5.15 to $5.35 in diluted earnings per common share ("EPS") for the year ending December 31, 2010. This EPS guidance is consistent with that issued in Humana's press release dated February 1, 2010, and subsequently reiterated in an investor presentation posted to the Investor Relations section of its web site (www.humana.com) at the time of that presentation. The date and time of each presentation during the period discussed in this filing are available via the Investor Relations section of Humana's web site.
