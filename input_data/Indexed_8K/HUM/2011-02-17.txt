Item 7.01   Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
 
Members of Humana Inc.s senior management team are scheduled to meet with investors between February 18, 2011 and March 31, 2011. During such meetings, Humanas management expects to reaffirm its earnings per common share and membership guidance for the year ending December 31, 2011, previously issued on February 7, 2011. Certain of these meetings are formal presentations, and the date and time of each such presentation during the period discussed in this filing are available via the Investor Relations section of Humanas web site.
