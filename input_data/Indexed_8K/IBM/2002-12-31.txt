Attachment I SAN JOSE, CALIF. -- DECEMBER 31, 2002. IBM (NYSE: IBM) today announced it has completed an agreement to combine its hard disk drive operations with Hitachi's, forming a new company called Hitachi Global Storage Technologies. Under the agreement, Hitachi has purchased the majority of IBM's HDD-related assets for $2.05 billion. As planned Hitachi paid 70 percent of the purchase price to IBM today. Hitachi will pay the remainder to IBM over the next three years. # # # #
