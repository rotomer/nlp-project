Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure
On October 16, 2006, IntercontinentalExchange, Inc. ("ICE") was informed by Sandler O'Neill and Partners, L.P. ("Sandler") that, after the close of trading on the New York Stock Exchange, Sandler and UBS Investment Bank placed approximately five million shares of ICE's common stock. This transaction was entered into following the October 15, 2006 expiration of lock-up agreements with certain ICE shareholders. ICE did not participate in this transaction.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: October 16, 2006
