Item 7.01      REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
On March 12, 2007, the Board of Trade of the City of New York, Inc. ("NYBOT"), IntercontinentalExchange's wholly-owned subsidiary, released its audited Consolidated Financial Statements for the years ended December 31, 2006, 2005 and 2004 to its members. A copy of NYBOT's Consolidated Financial Statements can be accessed via ICE's website under the heading for "Markets" then "NYBOT." NYBOT's audited Consolidated Financial Statements will also be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission via an amendment to this Current Report on Form 8-K in the near future.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this Current Report on Form 8-K to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
