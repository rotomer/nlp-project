(b) Director Retirement.
     On March 13, 2008, Richard L. Sandor, Ph. D. retired from his position as a Director of IntercontinentalExchange, Inc. (the "Company") to pursue his primary business interest as the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of the Chicago Climate Exchange, Inc. and the Chairman of Climate Exchange PLC. There were no disagreements with the Company on any matters related to the Company's operations, policies or practices. Dr. Sandor was not a member of any committee of the Company's Board of Directors.
