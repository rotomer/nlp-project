(b)           On December 14, 2009, Richard R. Burt informed the Board of Directors of International Game Technology (the Company) that he will not seek re-election to the Companys Board of Directors at the end of his current term at the Companys annual meeting of shareholders in 2010, anticipated to take place on March 2, 2010.  In order to assure a smooth transition, Mr. Burt will continue to serve on the Board through such annual meeting.  Mr. Burts decision not to stand for re-election did not result from a disagreement with the Company on any matter relating to the Companys operations, policies or practices.
