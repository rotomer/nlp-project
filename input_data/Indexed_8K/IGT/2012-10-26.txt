IGT and Bally Technologies Settle Patent Litigation
 
 International Game Technology (NYSE: IGT) and Bally Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: BYI) announced today they have entered into a Settlement and License Agreement that will end their pending patent litigation in the case styled IGT v. Bally Gaming Int'l, Inc., Civil Action No. 1:06-cv-00282-SLR (D. Del.). As part of the settlement agreement, Bally will obtain a patent license to IGT's bonusing portfolio under confidential terms and will dismiss its counterclaims with prejudice.
