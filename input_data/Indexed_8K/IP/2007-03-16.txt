On March 12, 2007, International Paper Company's Board of Directors elected Lynn Laverty Elsenhans as a director. The effective date of her election is March 15, 2007. She has been appointed as a Class I director and, along with other Class I directors, has been nominated for election by shareholders at the Company's annual meeting on May 7, 2007. At its meeting in May 2007, the Board of Directors will assign Ms. Elsenhans to one or more committees of the Board.
