On January 13, 2010, International Paper Company (the "") entered into Amendment No. 3 (the "") to the Second Amended and Restated Credit and Security Agreement, dated as of March 13, 2008 (the ""), among Red Bird Receivables, LLC, as Borrower, the Company, as Servicer, the Conduits and Liquidity Banks from time to time parties thereto, and the agents parties thereto.
