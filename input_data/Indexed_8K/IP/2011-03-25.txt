On March 22, 2011, Maura A. Smith, 55, Senior Vice President, General Counsel, Corporate Secretary and Global Government Relations, notified International Paper Company (the "Company") that she plans to resign on April 30, 2011, after more than 8 years of service to the Company. She has been appointed Executive Vice President, Government Affairs, General Counsel and Corporate Secretary for PepsiCo, effective May 5, 2011.
Sharon R. Ryan, 52, Vice President, Chief Ethics and Compliance Officer, and Associate General Counsel, will be the Acting General Counsel and Corporate Secretary until a permanent successor is named. Ms. Ryan joined the Company in 1988, and has served in numerous legal roles during her career at the Company.
