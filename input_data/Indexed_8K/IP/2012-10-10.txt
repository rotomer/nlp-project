SECTION 5. CORPORATE GOVERNANCE AND MANAGEMENT.
 
As previously reported on a Current Report on Form 8-K filed by International Paper Company (the "Company") on June 12, 2012 (the "Original 8-K"), the Company's Board of Directors elected Ilene S. Gordon as a director effective October 1, 2012. The Company is filing this Current Report on Form 8-K/A to amend the Original 8-K to report that on October 9, 2012, Ms. Gordon was appointed to the Audit and Finance Committee and the Management Development and Compensation Committee of the Company's Board of Directors. All other information in the Original 8-K remains unchanged.
