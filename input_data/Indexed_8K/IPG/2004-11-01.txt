Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
On October 26, 2004, at a regularly scheduled meeting, the Board of Directors of The Interpublic Group of Companies, Inc. ("Interpublic") elected David M. Thomas as a non-management director. Mr. Thomas was also appointed as a member of the Audit Committee and the Corporate Governance Committee.
