FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
 
 
 
 
New York, NY  May 26, 2011  Interpublic Group (NYSE: IPG) today announced that on May 26, 2011, the companys Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend on IPG common stock of $0.06 per share, payable on June 24, 2011 to holders of record at the close of business on June 10, 2011.
