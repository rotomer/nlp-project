FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
 
New York, NY  August 17, 2011  Interpublic Group (NYSE: IPG) today announced that the companys Board of Directors has declared a quarterly dividend on IPG common stock of $0.06 per share, payable on September 23, 2011 to holders of record at the close of business on September 9, 2011.
