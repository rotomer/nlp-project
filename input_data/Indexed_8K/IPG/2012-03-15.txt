Item 8.01.  Other Events.
On March 14, 2012, Michael I. Roth, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of The Interpublic Group of Companies, Inc. (IPG), executed a 10b5-1 trading plan, pursuant to which he has elected to sell on April 2, 2012 a total of 324,341 shares, representing shares he currently owns from prior vested awards.  The trading plan will terminate upon completion of the sale.
Mr. Roth will use the proceeds of such sale for tax planning purposes.  After this sale, Mr. Roth will hold IPG shares valued in excess of 5 times his base salary, as determined under IPGs stock ownership guidelines using the closing price of IPG common stock on March 13, 2012.
