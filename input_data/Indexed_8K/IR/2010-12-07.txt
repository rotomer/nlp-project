Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On December 1, 2010, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Ingersoll-Rand plc approved a revision to the Annual Incentive Matrix program in which certain key employees, including the Named Executive Officers, participate. Operating Margin percentage is being introduced as a financial metric for the 2011 performance year. Available Cash Flow and Revenue Growth will also be financial metrics that along with individual performance determine payments under the program.
