Item 8.01. Other Events.
The Company will be added to the S&P 500 GICS (Global Industry Classification Standard) Industrials Economic Sector and the Diversified Support Services Sub-Industry Index.
On December 29, 2008, Standard & Poors Index Services announced that Iron Mountain Incorporated (the Company) will be added to their US S&P 500 Index (the S&P 500).  
The Company was formally included in the S&P 500 after the market closed on January 5, 2009.
