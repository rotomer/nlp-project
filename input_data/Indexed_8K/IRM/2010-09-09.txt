Item 5.02.
            
Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
On September 8, 2010 Harry Ebbighausen, Iron Mountain's President, North America, assumed day-to-day leadership responsibilities for Iron Mountain Digital.  Ramana Venkata, the former President of Iron Mountain Digital, will depart the company on September 30, 2010 following a transition period.  In addition to his role as President, North America, Mr. Ebbighausen is expected to remain as the interim leader of Iron Mountain Digital until a permanent leader is identified.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
