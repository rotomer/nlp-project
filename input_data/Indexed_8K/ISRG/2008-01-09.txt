Intuitive Surgical, Inc., (the "Company") is filing this Amendment No. 1 on Form 8-K/A to its Current Report on Form 8-K (the "Previously Filed Report") filed on December 10, 2007, to further note in the Previously Filed Report that on January 4, 2008, the Company entered into a non-exclusive license agreement with California Institute of Technology ("Cal Tech") under which the Company has been granted a non-exclusive license to Cal Tech's patent portfolio and Cal Tech has dismissed its lawsuit against the Company.
