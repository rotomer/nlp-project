Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
On May 6, 2005, Jon C. Kinney, age 62, announced that he will retire from his position as the Companys Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer effective August 6, 2005. Mr. Kinney has held this position since January 7, 1998, and has been an employee of ITW since 1972. Upon his retirement, Mr. Kinneys duties will be split among Ronald D. Kropp, the Companys Vice President and Controller, Financial Reporting and Eugene Osterkorn, the Companys Vice President and Controller, Operations.
