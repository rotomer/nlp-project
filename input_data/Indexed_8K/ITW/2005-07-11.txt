Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
On August 6, 2004, Illinois Tool Works Inc. (the "Company") announced that its Chairman and Chief Executive Office, W. James Farrell, anticipated retiring at the May 2006 Shareholders meeting. Subsequently, on July 8, 2005, Mr. Farrell confirmed in a letter to the Board of Directors that he now intends to step down as the Companys Chief Executive Officer at the Companys Board of Directors Meeting on August 5, 2005. A copy of that letter is filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this report. Mr. Farrell is expected to continue as the Chairman of the Companys Board of Directors until the May 2006 Shareholders Meeting.
