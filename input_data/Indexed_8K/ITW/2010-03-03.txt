Item 8.01 Other Events.
The form of award to be used in connection with a new performance-based cash award grant pursuant to the Illinois Tool Works Inc. 2006 Stock Incentive Plan is included as an exhibit to this report. The award is subject to attainment of a performance goal based 50% on revenue growth and 50% on average return on invested capital over a three-year performance period. If the grantee's employment terminates before the end of the performance period for any reason other than death, disability or retirement, the award is forfeited. If employment terminates due to death or disability, the grant becomes vested to the full extent of the performance achievement, and if termination is due to retirement after attaining age 62 and 10 years of service or age 65 and 5 years of service, the award will vest on a pro-rata basis for the portion of the period that the grantee was employed and based on the level of performance achieved.
