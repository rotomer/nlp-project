Robert J. Yerbury, Senior Managing Director and Head of UK Retail, has informed the Company that he intends to retire from his position as chief executive officer of Invesco Perpetual effective September 2008. Mr. Yerbury will continue to serve as Chief Investment Officer (CIO) of Invesco Perpetual and in his wider responsibilities guiding the companys and global trading initiative. Mr. Yerbury has been with Invesco Perpetual since 1983, becoming CIO in 1997 and adding the role of chief executive officer in 2004. James I. Robertson, Senior Managing Director and Head of Global Operations/IT, will assume the position of chief executive officer of Invesco Perpetual from Mr. Yerbury.
