Robert J. Yerbury, Senior Managing Director and Head of UK Retail, has informed the Company that he intends to retire from his position as chief executive officer of  Invesco Perpetual effective September 1, 2008. Mr. Yerbury will continue to serve as Chief Investment Officer (CIO) of Invesco Perpetual and in his wider responsibilities guiding the
Investors Forum
and global trading, but will no longer be deemed an executive officer of the Company under the rules of the Securities and Exchange Commission.  James I. Robertson, Senior Managing Director, will assume the position of chief executive officer of Invesco Perpetual from Mr. Yerbury.
