Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $418.8 billion.
Invesco is a leading independent global investment management company, dedicated to helping people worldwide build their financial security.  By delivering the combined power of our distinctive worldwide investment management capabilities, Invesco provides a comprehensive array of enduring investment solutions for retail, institutional and high net worth clients around the world.
