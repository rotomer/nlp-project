As previously mentioned on the first quarter 2010 earnings call, Invesco is now including an additional $38.1 billion of assets under management in our disclosures in an effort to align with industry standards and to present our business in a consistent and complete manner.  Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $456.9 billion. Preliminary average assets under management during April were $456.8 billion, a 1.6% increase from the first quarter 2010 of $449.6 billion.  Q4 2009 average assets were $438.4 billion.
