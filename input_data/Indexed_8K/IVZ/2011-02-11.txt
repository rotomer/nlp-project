Invesco Ltd. Announces January 31, 2011
Assets Under Management
Investor Relations Contact: Jordan Krugman       404-439-4605
Media Relations Contact:     Doug Kidd                404-479-2922
 
Atlanta, February 9, 2011 ---
Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $624.3 billion, an increase of 1.3% month over monthThe month over month increase in AUM was driven by long-term net inflows, positive market returns, and strengthening foreign currencies against the U.S. dollar.  The benefit from stronger foreign currency against the U.S. dollar resulted in an increase of $1.9 billon of AUM in the month. Total average assets for the quarter through January 31 were $620.5 billion and average assets e xcluding ETF, UIT and Passive for the quarter through January 31 were $536.7 billion.
