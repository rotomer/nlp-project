Invesco Ltd. Announces March 31, 2011
Assets Under Management
 
Investor Relations Contact: Jordan Krugman       404-439-4605
Media Relations Contact:     Doug Kidd                404-479-2922
 
 
Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $641.9 billion, an increase of 0.1% month over monthThe month over month increase in AUM was driven by positive net long-term flows and market returns offset by foreign exchange which reduced AUM by $0.9 billion during the month. Total average assets for the quarter through March 31 were $630.2 billion and average assets excluding ETF, UIT and Passive for the quarter through March 31 were $543.0 billion.
Atlanta, April 11, 2011 ---
