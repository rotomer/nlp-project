Invesco Ltd. Announces March 31, 2012
Assets Under Management
 
Invesco Relations Contact:  Jordan Krugman    404-439-4605
Media Relations Contact:    Doug Kidd            404-479-2922
 
 
 
 
 
Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $672.8 billion, an increase of 0.8% month over month. The increase was largely the result of positive market returns and long-term net inflows.  FX decreased AUM by $0.9 billion. Additionally, during the month of March the company recognized $1.2 billion of outflow in the Alternative asset class related to the previously disclosed liquidation of the U.S. Treasury component of the PPIP fund. Total average assets for the quarter through March 31 were $658.2 billion and average active assets for the quarter through March 31 were $552.4 billion.
Atlanta, April 11, 2012 ---
