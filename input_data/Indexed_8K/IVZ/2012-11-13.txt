Invesco Ltd. Announces October 31, 2012
Assets Under Management
 
 
 
Invesco Ltd. (NYSE: IVZ) today reported preliminary month-end assets under management of $677.4 billion, a decrease of 0.8% month over month. The decrease was due to unfavorable market returns, negative foreign exchange and total net outflows driven by $2.2 billion of outflow in the Invesco PowerShares QQQ.  FX decreased AUM $1.1 billion in the month.  During the month, the company had positive long-term net flows in active AUM.  Preliminary total average assets for the quarter through October 31 were $679.2 billion, and preliminary average active assets for the quarter through October 31 were $564.6 billion.
Atlanta, November 12, 2012 ---
