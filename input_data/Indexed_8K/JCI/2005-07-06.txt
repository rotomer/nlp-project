Item 8.01
Other Events
On June 30, 2005, Johnson Controls, Inc. ("Johnson Controls") and Delphi Corporation ("Delphi") agreed to the terms of the sale of Delphi's global battery business for approximately $202.5 million, subject to closing adjustments. The transaction closed on July 1, 2005. In conjunction with the acquisition, Johnson Controls has received a global long-term contract to supply General Motors with original equipment and original equipment service batteries.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
