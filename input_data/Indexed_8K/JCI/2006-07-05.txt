Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On June 30, 2006, Johnson Controls, Inc. (the "Company") and Giovanni Fiori, Executive Vice President, International of the Company, mutually agreed upon December 31, 2007 as Mr. Fioris retirement date in accordance with the terms of the letter agreement between the Company and Mr. Fiori, dated November 29, 2004 (the "Second Letter Agreement"), which related to the letter agreement, dated November 21, 2002, between the Company and Mr. Fiori (the "First Letter Agreement"), and Mr. Fioris executive employment agreement with the Company. The First Letter Agreement and the Second Letter Agreement were filed as Exhibit 10.U to the Companys Annual Report on Form 10-K for its fiscal year ended September 30, 2005, and the current form of employment agreement between the Company and all elected officers and certain key employees, including Mr. Fiori, was filed as Exhibit 10.K to the Companys Annual Report on Form 10-K for its fiscal year ended September 30, 2005.
