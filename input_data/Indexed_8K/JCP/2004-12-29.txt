Item 1.01 Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
On December 23, 2004, the Company and its subsidiary, J. C. Penney Corporation, Inc., executed a Third Amendment to Employment Agreement with Vanessa Castagna. The Third Amendment revises Ms. Castagna's Employment Agreement to provide (1) that the amount of Ms. Castagna's award under the J. C. Penney Corporation, Inc. Management Incentive Compensation program for the fiscal year ending January 29, 2005 be the actual award payable, and (2) that payments of salary to and including January 31, 2005 and the March 2005 Compensation Program payment shall not be subject to reduction for payments received by Ms. Castagna from another employer.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
 
Date: December 29, 2004
