Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On May 12, 2010, Juniper Networks, Inc. (the "Company") held its annual meeting of stockholders at which the Companys stockholders voted upon the following proposals described in the Companys 2010 proxy statement: (1) To elect Robert M. Calderoni, William F. Meehan and Pradeep Sindhu to serve as Class II directors for a term of three years; (2) to approve an amendment to the Juniper Networks, Inc. 2006 Equity Incentive Plan that increases the number of shares reserved for issuance thereunder by thirty million (30,000,000) shares; and (3) to ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the Companys independent auditors for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2010. All nominated directors were elected and the other proposals approved by the required stockholder vote. The final number of shares voted with respect to each proposal are as set forth below. (1) Proposal for election of Class II directors*: Robert M. Calderoni For: 248,177,357; Withheld: 188,813,412 William F. Meehan For: 431,493,220; Withheld: 5,497,549 Pradeep Sindhu For: 424,622,979; Withheld: 12,367,790 *results exclude 30,039,520 broker non-votes (2) Proposal to approve an amendment to the Juniper Networks, Inc. 2006 Equity Incentive Plan that increases the number of shares reserved for issuance thereunder by thirty million (30,000,000) shares*: For: 274,928,237; Against: 161,885,917; Abstain 176,615 *results exclude 30,039,520 broker non-votes (3) Proposal to ratify Ernst & Young LLP as auditors for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2010: For: 460,345,046; Against: 6,561,125; Abstain: 124,118
