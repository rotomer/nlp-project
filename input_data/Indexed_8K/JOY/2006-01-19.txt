ITEM 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
   On January 13, 2006 the Human Resources and Nominating Committee of the registrants Board of Directors established average return on equity over the three-year award cycle ending on the last day of Fiscal 2008 as the performance goal applicable to the performance shares granted to the registrants executive officers on November 14, 2005 pursuant to the Joy Global Inc. 2003 Stock Incentive Plan. The executive officers receiving grants on such date were John N. Hanson, Donald C. Roof, Michael W. Sutherlin, Mark E. Readinger, Dennis R. Winkleman and John J. Fons.
    
   
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
