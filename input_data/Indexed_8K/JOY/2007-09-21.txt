JOY GLOBAL INC. REAFFIRMS FISCAL 2007 GUIDANCE
 
Milwaukee, WI  September 20, 2007  Joy Global Inc. (NASDAQ: JOYG)
, a worldwide leader in high-productivity mining solutions, announced today that as part of the Analyst Day investor presentations in New York City, management has reaffirmed guidance for Fiscal 2007. The Company continues to expect revenues to be in the range of $2.50 to $2.55 billion with fully diluted earnings per share to be in the range of $2.65 to $2.70.
 
About the Company
 
Joy Global Inc. is a worldwide leader in manufacturing, servicing and distributing equipment for surface mining through P&H Mining Equipment and underground mining through Joy Mining Machinery.
