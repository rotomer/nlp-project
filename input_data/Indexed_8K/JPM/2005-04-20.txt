Item 8.01 Other Events.
JPMorgan Chase & Co. (the Registrant) announced on April 20, 2005, that William J. Moran, Executive Vice President and the firm's General Auditor, will be retiring effective June 30, 2005. Martha Gallo, an Executive Vice President of the Registrant, has been named to succeed Mr. Moran as General Auditor.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: April 20, 2005
