Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
(b) As previously announced on October 19, 2005, William B. Harrison, Jr. retired as Chief Executive Officer of Registrant, effective December 31, 2005. Mr. Harrison will continue as Chairman of the Board.
(c) As previously announced on October 19, 2005, James Dimon has succeeded Mr. Harrison as Chief Executive Officer, effective December 31, 2005. Mr. Dimon also retains the position of President. Mr. Dimon, 49, has been President and Chief Operating Officer of Registrant since July 2004. From March 2000 to July 2004, prior to its merger with Registrant, he had been Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Bank One Corporation.
