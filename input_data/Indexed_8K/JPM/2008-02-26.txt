Capital Gains Tax Treatment in the Pricing Supplement
We hereby consent to the filing of this opinion as an exhibit to the Registration Statement and to the references to us under the heading United States Federal Taxation in the Prospectus Supplement, under the heading Certain U.S. Federal Income Tax Consequences in the Product Supplement and under the heading Selected Purchase Considerations  .
