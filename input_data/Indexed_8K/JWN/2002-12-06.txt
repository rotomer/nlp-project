Item 8. Change in Fiscal Year. On November 26, 2002 our Board of Directors elected to change our fiscal year. Beginning February 1, 2003, our fiscal year-end will change from January 31 to the Saturday closest to January 31. In the future, each fiscal year will consist of four 13-week quarters, with an extra week added to the fourth quarter every five to six years. Our current and previous fiscal year-ending dates will remain unchanged at January 31, 2003 and 2002. We will include the one-day transition period in our first quarterly report on Form 10-Q of fiscal 2003.
