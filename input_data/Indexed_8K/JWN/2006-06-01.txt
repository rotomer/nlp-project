EXHIBIT INDEX EXHIBIT NUMBER DESCRIPTION 99.1 Nordstrom May 2006 Preliminary Sales Release, dated June 1, 2006.

For Immediate Release - --------------------- June 1, 2006 NORDSTROM SAME-STORE SALES FOR MAY INCREASE 7.8 PERCENT SEATTLE - June 1, 2006 - Nordstrom, Inc. (NYSE: JWN) today reported preliminary sales of $600.8 million for the four-week period ending May 27, 2006, an increase of 10.1 percent compared to sales of $545.6 million for the four-week period ending May 28, 2005. Same-store sales increased 7.8 percent. Preliminary year-to-date sales of $2.39 billion increased 8.5 percent compared to sales of $2.20 billion in 2005. Year-to-date same-store sales increased 6.0 percent. SALES RECORDING To hear Nordstrom's prerecorded May sales message, please dial (402) 220- 6036. This recording will be available for one week. Sales Release Thurs., Aug. 31, 2006 Nordstrom, Inc. is one of the nation's leading fashion specialty retailers, with 156 US stores located in 27 states. Founded in 1901 as a shoe store in Seattle, today Nordstrom operates 99 full-line stores, 49 Nordstrom Racks, five Faconnable boutiques in the United States, one free-standing shoe store, and two clearance stores. Nordstrom also operates 34 Faconnable boutiques in Europe. In addition, Nordstrom serves customers through its online presence at http://www.nordstrom.com and through its catalogs.
