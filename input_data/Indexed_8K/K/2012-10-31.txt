Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
(d) On October 26, 2012, the Board of Directors of Kellogg Company (the "Company") elected Mary A. Laschinger as a director. Ms. Laschinger's initial term expires at the 2013 annual meeting of shareowners. The Board also appointed Ms. Laschinger to the Manufacturing and Social Responsibility and Public Policy committees. Ms. Laschinger is Senior Vice President, International Paper Company, and President of xpedx, Inc., IP's North America distribution business.
Ms. Laschinger's compensation will be similar to other non-employee directors of the Company, which is described in the Company's proxy statement filed with the SEC on March 5, 2012.
