Section 5  Corporate Governance and Management
     On December 13, 2005, Lee G. Irving, KeyCorp's Chief Accounting Officer, announced his intention to retire from KeyCorp effective at the close of business on March 31, 2006.
     KeyCorp has appointed Robert L. Morris as his successor to fill the position of Chief Accounting Officer, commencing April 1, 2006. Mr. Morris is presently serving as KeyCorp's Corporate Controller, a position he has held since December 2000. From 1999 until December 2000, Mr. Morris was Senior Vice President of Financial Operations Integration. Prior to 1999 he held various other positions within KeyCorp's accounting and auditing areas. Mr. Morris, age 53, has been with KeyCorp since 1989.
