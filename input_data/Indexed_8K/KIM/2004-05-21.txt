Item 5. Other Events and Regulation FD Disclosure. On May 20, 2004, the Board of Directors of Kimco Realty Corporation (the "Company") met and resolved to increase the ownership limit under the Company's Charter with respect to the Company's common stock from 2% to 9.8%.
