Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
     On October 18, 2004, as a result of the Omnibus Stock Plan being approved at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of KLA-Tencor Corporation (the "Company") granted Ken Schroeder, the Company's President, Chief Executive Officer and Director, 100,000 shares of restricted stock of the Company. The entire grant will become exercisable on the later of July 1, 2007 or one year after Mr. Schroeder's retirement from the position of Chief Executive Officer. All vesting is subject to Mr. Schroeder continuing to render services to the Company through the vesting date.
