On September 15, 2005, the Board of Directors of Kimberly-Clark Corporation (the "Corporation") appointed Mr. Abelardo E. Bru to the Management Development and Compensation Committee and the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Corporation's Board of Directors. As previously reported on the Current Report on Form 8-K filed on June 17, 2005, Mr. Bru was elected to the Corporation's Board of Directors effective September 1, 2005.
