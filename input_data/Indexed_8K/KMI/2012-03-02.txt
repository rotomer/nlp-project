On March 2, 2012, Kinder Morgan, Inc. ("Kinder Morgan") held a special meeting of its stockholders in order to vote upon a proposal (the "share and warrant issuance proposal") to approve the issuance of shares of Kinder Morgan Class P common stock and warrants to purchase shares of Kinder Morgan Class P common stock to be issued as part of the merger consideration, as well as the issuance of shares of Kinder Morgan Class P common stock to be issued upon exercise of such warrants, in each case, in connection with an Agreement and Plan of Merger, pursuant to which El Paso Corporation will become a wholly owned subsidiary of Kinder Morgan.
As of January 20, 2012, the record date for determining the Kinder Morgan stockholders entitled to vote on the share and warrant issuance proposal presented at the special meeting, there were 170,921,140 shares of Kinder Morgan Class P common stock and 535,972,387 shares of Kinder Morgan Class A common stock entitled to vote at the special meeting. At the special meeting, holders of 21,528,167 shares of Kinder Morgan Class P common stock and holders of 535,925,720 shares of Kinder Morgan Class A common stock entitled to vote were represented in person or by proxy, constituting a quorum.
