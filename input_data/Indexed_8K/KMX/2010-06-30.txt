Item 5.07.                                Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On June 28, 2010, CarMax, Inc. (the "Company") held its 2010 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. The following actions were taken:
1.           The shareholders re-elected Ronald E. Blaylock, Keith D. Browning, and Thomas G. Stemberg to the Board, each for a three-year term expiring at the 2013 Annual Meeting of Shareholders pursuant to the vote set forth below.
There were 17,118,020 broker non-votes for each director.
2.           The shareholders ratified the selection of KPMG LLP as the Companys independent registered public accounting firm for fiscal year 2011 by a vote of 198,940,887 shares for, 1,177,491 shares against, and 51,112 shares abstaining.
 
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
