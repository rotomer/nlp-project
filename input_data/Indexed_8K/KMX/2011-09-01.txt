On August 1, 2011, CarMax Auto Funding LLC (a subsidiary of CarMax, Inc.) offered to rescind purchases of the asset-backed notes initially sold by CarMax Auto Owner Trust 2011-1 (the "Notes") in March 2011. The rescission offer period expired on August 31, 2011. None of the Notes were tendered in connection with the rescission offer. Accordingly, CarMax Auto Funding LLC has not repurchased any of the Notes.
