Exhibit 99.1
National & Retail Trades and First Call
For release: June 3, 2004 at 8:30 AM (EST)
MENOMONEE FALLS, WI, -- (Business Wire)  June 3, 2004 -- Kohl's Corporation (NYSE: KSS) reported today that sales for the four-week period ended May 29, 2004 increased 20.2 percent over the four-week period ended May 31, 2003. On a comparable store basis, sales increased 5.0 percent.
For the 17 weeks ended May 29, 2004, total sales were up 14.3 percent over the 17 weeks ended May 31, 2003.  On a comparable store basis, sales for the 17-week period increased 1.3 percent.
As of May 29, 2004, the Company operated 589 stores in 38 states, compared with 492 stores in 34 states at the same time last year.
