Exhibit 99.1
National & Retail Trades and First Call
For release: August 31, 2006 at 8:30 AM (EDT)
MENOMONEE FALLS, WI, -- (Business Wire)  August 31, 2006 -- Kohl's Corporation (NYSE: KSS) reported today that sales for the four-week period ended August 26, 2006 increased 13.1 percent over the four-week period ended August 27, 2005. On a comparable store basis, sales increased 5.2 percent.  
For the 30 weeks ended August 26, 2006, total sales were up 14.7 percent over the 30 weeks ended August 27, 2005.  On a comparable store basis, sales for the 30 weeks increased 6.0 percent.
On August 26, 2006, the Company operated 749 stores in 43 states, compared with 674 stores in 40 states at the same time last year.
