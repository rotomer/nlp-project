Exhibit 99.1
National & Retail Trades and First Call
For release: March 8, 2007 at 8:30 AM (EST)
MENOMONEE FALLS, WI, -- (Business Wire)  March 8, 2007 -- Kohl's Corporation (NYSE: KSS) reported today that sales for the four-week period ended March 3, 2007 increased 12.8 percent over the four-week period ended February 25, 2006. On a comparable store basis, sales increased 4.4 percent.  
On March 3, 2007, the Company operated 817 stores in 45 states, compared to 732 in 41 states at the same time last year.  
The Company celebrates the grand opening of seven new stores today. In April, the Company plans to open ten additional stores, including its initial entry into Idaho. The balance of its planned 110-115 stores for 2007 are expected to open in the third fiscal quarter.
