Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On September 12, 2011, Paul R. Hauser, the Company's Senior Vice President, President - Residential Furnishings Segment and a named executive officer, notified the Company of his decision to retire effective February 18, 2012.
As part of the Company's succession planning, Perry E. Davis, currently the Company's Vice President, President - Bedding Group, will assume Mr. Hauser's duties and be promoted to Senior Vice President and President of the Company's Residential Furnishings Segment also effective February 18, 2012. Mr. Davis, 52, was appointed Vice President, President - Bedding Group in 2006 and has served the Company in various other capacities since 1981.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
