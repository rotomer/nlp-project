ITEM 9. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE. The December 10, 2002 WALL STREET JOURNAL carried an article about a type of arrangement under which a not-for-profit organization gives a home buyer the money with which to make the down payment for a home purchase, and the home seller then makes a contribution to the not-for-profit organization equal to the amount given to the home buyer. Only 3% to 5% of Lennar Corporation's home sales involve arrangements of this type.
