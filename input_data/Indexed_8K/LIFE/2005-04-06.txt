Section 8  Other Events
 
Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
Dr. Jay M. Short, the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Diversa Corporation, and a director of three additional publicly traded companies, including Invitrogen Corporation, has committed to retire from the Board of Directors of one of the publicly traded companies (other than Invitrogen) within the next six months. He announced his intent to serve on the board of directors of no more than three publicly traded companies so long as he remains the Chief Executive Officer of Diversa Corporation.
