Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On November 16, 2006, Lockheed Martin Corporation announced that Michael F. Camardo, Executive Vice President - Lockheed Martin Information & Technology Services, will retire effective April 1, 2007. A successor will be named in the near future.
Mr. Camardo joined the Corporation in 1964 as a Financial Analyst for the then-RCA Service Company. Having served in increasingly responsible positions with RCA, he rose to become Division Vice President of RCA Government Services. After RCA merged with General Electric Corporation, he was named President of the unit under its new name, GE Government Services. He was named President of Martin Marietta Services Group upon completion of the merger with GE Aerospace in April 1993, and became President of Lockheed Martin Services Group when Lockheed merged with Martin Marietta two years later. He was named to his current position in 1999.
