Item 5
Statistical Report for the quarter ended June 30, 2002.
 
Lincoln Financial Group
This document is dated October 2, 2002. It may not be accurate after such date and LNC does not undertake to update or keep it accurate after such date.
The Lincoln Statistical Report included herein is a revised report as of June 30, 2002. The key revisions from the Lincoln Statistical Report as of June 30, 2002 filed on Form 8-K on August 5, 2002 are the following:
 
* Effective March 7, 2002, the Annuities segment became known as Lincoln Retirement.
** Upon the closing of Swiss Re's acquisition of LNC's reinsurance business on December 7, 2001, all historical financial data for the former Reinsurance segment was moved into "Other Operations." In addition, ongoing amortization of the deferred gain on indemnity reinsurance is shown in "Other Operations."
*** The cumulative effect of accounting changes relates to the adjustment recorded upon adoption of FAS #133 in the 1st qtr. 2001.
