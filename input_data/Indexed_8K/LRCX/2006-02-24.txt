Item 5.02. Election of Director
     On January 6, 2006, the Company filed a Form 8-K disclosing the appointment of Catherine P. Lego to fill a newly-created directorship on the Board. Ms. Lego was not immediately named to any Board committees. Such Form 8-K filing is amended to disclose that on February 17, 2006, the Board named Ms. Lego to serve on its audit committee.
