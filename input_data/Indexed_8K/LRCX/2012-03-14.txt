On March 12, 2012, in accordance with the customary practice of Lam Research Corporation (the "Company"), the Company and Martin B. Anstice entered into an amendment to Mr. Anstice's indemnification agreement with the Company, dated as of November 7, 2008, to extend the indemnification and term provisions of the indemnification agreement to cover Mr. Anstice's service as a director of the Company in addition to his service as an officer of the Company. The amendment is effective as of February 6, 2012, the date of Mr. Anstice's appointment as a director of the Company.
Except as specifically modified by the amendment, all terms and provisions of Mr. Anstice's indemnification agreement (the form of which is attached to the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K dated November 13, 2008 as Exhibit 10.148) remain unmodified and in full force and effect.
