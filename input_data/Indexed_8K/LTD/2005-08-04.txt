Columbus, Ohio (August 4, 2005)  Limited Brands (NYSE: LTD) reported a comparable store sales increase of 1% for the four weeks ended July 30, 2005 compared to the four weeks ended July 31, 2004. Net sales were $596.2 million compared to net sales of $575.3 million last year.
 
The Company reported flat comparable store sales for the second quarter ended July 30, 2005. Net sales for the second quarter were $2.291 billion, an increase of 4% compared to sales of $2.211 billion last year.
 
Comparable store sales decreased 2% for the year-to-date period and net sales were $4.266 billion compared to sales of $4.189 billion last year.
