Columbus, Ohio, May 6, 2009  Limited Brands, Inc. (NYSE: LTD) reported a comparable store sales increase of 4 percent for the four weeks ended May 1, 2010, compared to the four weeks ended May 2, 2009. The company reported net sales of $584.6 million for the four weeks ended May 1, 2010, compared to net sales of $531.2 million last year.
The company reported a comparable store sales increase of 10 percent for the 13 weeks ended May 1, 2010, compared to the 13 weeks ended May 2, 2009. The company reported net sales of $1.932 billion for the 13 weeks ended May 1, 2010, compared to sales of $1.725 billion last year.
