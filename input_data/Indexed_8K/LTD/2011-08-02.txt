Item 8.01 Other Events
Limited Brands, Inc. ("Limited Brands") hereby reports that it contributed 7.2 million shares of common stock of Express, Inc. ("Express") to The Limited Brands Foundation (the "Foundation") on July 28, 2011. The contribution was made to fund certain preexisting obligations, and anticipated future activities, of the Foundation in a manner that is tax efficient for Limited Brands. The contribution, which included all remaining shares of Express owned by Limited Brands, is consistent with Limited Brands' strategic focus on intimate apparel, beauty and personal care.
