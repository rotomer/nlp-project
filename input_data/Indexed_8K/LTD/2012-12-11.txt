LIMITED BRANDS ANNOUNCES $3 PER SHARE SPECIAL DIVIDEND
Columbus, Ohio, Dec. 10, 2012 - As part of its ongoing commitment to return value to shareholders, Limited Brands (NYSE: LTD) announced today that its Board of Directors has declared a special dividend of $3 per share. The special dividend will be paid on Dec. 26, 2012, to shareholders of record at the close of business on Dec. 20, 2012.
ABOUT LIMITED BRANDS:
Limited Brands, through Victoria's Secret, Pink, Bath & Body Works, La Senza and Henri Bendel, is an international company. The company operates 2,632 specialty stores in the United States and its brands are sold in more than 700 company-operated and franchised additional locations world-wide.
