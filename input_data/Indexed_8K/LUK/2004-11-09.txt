Exhibit 99.1 November 9, 2004 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE Contact: Laura Ulbrandt (212) 460-1900 LEUCADIA NATIONAL CORPORATION ANNOUNCES NINE MONTH 2004 RESULTS Leucadia National Corporation (LUK - NYSE and PCX) today announced its operating results for the nine month period ended September 30, 2004. Net income was $96,587,000 or $1.35 per diluted common share for the nine month period ended September 30, 2004 compared to net income of $57,721,000 or $.96 per diluted common share for the nine month period ended September 30, 2003. Net income per share included $.03 of losses and $.09 of income from discontinued operations for the nine month 2004 and 2003 periods, respectively. For more information on the Company's results of operations for 2004, please see the Company's Form 10-Q for the nine months ended September 30, 2004, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission today.
