EXHIBIT 99.1 March 14, 2005 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - --------------------- Contact: Laura Ulbrandt (212) 460-1900 LEUCADIA NATIONAL CORPORATION ANNOUNCES 2004 RESULTS Leucadia National Corporation (LUK - NYSE and PCX) today announced its operating results for the year ended December 31, 2004. Net income was $145,500,000 or $1.34 per diluted common share for the year ended December 31, 2004 compared to $97,054,000 or $1.05 per diluted common share for the year ended December 31, 2003. Net income for 2004 included $6,456,000 of losses from discontinued operations, including gain on disposal, net of taxes or $.06 per diluted common share, while net income for 2003 included $11,179,000 of income from discontinued operations, including gain on disposal, net of taxes or $.12 per diluted common share. For more information on the Company's results of operations for 2004, please see the Company's Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2004, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission today.
