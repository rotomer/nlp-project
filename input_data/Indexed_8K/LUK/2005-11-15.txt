Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. On November 14, 2005, Square 711 Developer, LLC ("Square 711"), a subsidiary of Leucadia National Corporation (the "Company"), entered into an agreement with Walton Acquisition Holdings V, L.L.C. ("Walton") to sell to Walton its interest in 8 acres of unimproved land in Washington, D.C. for aggregate cash consideration of $122,000,000. Closing of the transaction is subject to the completion by Walton of due diligence by December 14, 2005. After satisfaction of mortgage indebtedness on the property of $32,000,000 and other payments due at closing, the Company expects to receive net cash proceeds of approximately $75,000,000, and record a pre-tax gain of approximately $48,000,000, which will be reflected in discontinued operations. 2
