Exhibit 99.1 February 28, 2007 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE Contact: Laura Ulbrandt (212) 460-1900 LEUCADIA NATIONAL CORPORATION ANNOUNCES 2006 RESULTS Leucadia National Corporation (LUK - NYSE) today announced its operating results for the year ended December 31, 2006. Net income was $189,399,000 or $.85 per diluted common share for the year ended December 31, 2006 compared to net income of $1,636,041,000 or $7.14 per diluted common share for the year ended December 31, 2005. As previously disclosed, the 2005 results included an adjustment that reduced the deferred tax asset valuation allowance and credited income tax expense by $1,135,100,000. Net income also included income of $59,630,000 or $.25 per diluted common share for 2006 and $415,701,000 or $1.80 per diluted common share for 2005 related to discontinued operations, including gain on disposal. For more information on the Company's results of operations for 2006, please see the Company's Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2006, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission today.
