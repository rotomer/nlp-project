Item 3.02. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.
 
From November 7, 2008 through November 14, 2008, Leucadia National Corporation (the Company) issued an aggregate of 4,924,221 common shares, par value $1.00 per share (Common Shares) upon conversion of an aggregate of $113,093,000 principal amount of the Companys 3-3/4% Convertible Senior Subordinated Notes due 2014 (Notes), as set forth below, pursuant to privately negotiated transactions to induce conversion. The Notes were convertible at a rate of 43.5414 Common Shares per $1,000 principal amount of the Notes. The Common Shares were issued without registration pursuant to Section 3(a)(9) under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
