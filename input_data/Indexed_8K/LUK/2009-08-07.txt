August 7, 2009 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE Contact: Laura Ulbrandt (212) 460-1900 LEUCADIA NATIONAL CORPORATION ANNOUNCES SIX MONTH 2009 RESULTS Leucadia National Corporation (LUK - NYSE) today announced its operating results for the six month period ended June 30, 2009. Net income attributable to Leucadia National Corporation common shareholders for the six month periods ended June 30, 2009 and 2008 was $271,016,000 ($1.11 per diluted common share) and $90,954,000 ($.39 per diluted common share), respectively. For more information on the Company's results of operations for the first half of 2009, please see the Company's Form 10-Q for the six months ended June 30, 2009, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission today.
