April 8, 2011
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Contact:  Laura Ulbrandt  (212) 460-1900
New York, New York, April 8, 2011 -- Leucadia National Corporation (NYSE: LUK) announced today that it has agreed to purchase $125 million of the common stock of Jefferies Group, Inc. (NYSE: JEF) as part of a $500 million common stock offering announced by Jefferies on April 7, 2011.
Immediately prior to this offering, Leucadia owned approximately 27.83% of Jefferies.  After giving effect to the offering and Leucadias purchase of shares, Leucadia will now own approximately 27.54% of Jefferies common stock.
