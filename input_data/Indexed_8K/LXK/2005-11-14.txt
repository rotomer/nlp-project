Regulation FD Disclosure.
Lexmark International, Inc. (Lexmark) will conduct its annual briefing for financial analysts and investors at its worldwide headquarters in Lexington, Kentucky on Tuesday, November 15, 2005.
Speakers whose comments will be webcast include:
The program will focus to a significant extent on Lexmarks unique strengths and capabilities in delivering customized solutions and services to enterprise customers and will include product demonstrations which will not be webcast.
