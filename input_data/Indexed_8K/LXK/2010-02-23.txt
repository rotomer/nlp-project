On February 17, 2010, Mr. James F. Hardymon, age 75, announced his intention not to seek reelection as a member of the Board of Directors, and will retire after twelve years of service on the Board, effective as of the Annual Meeting of Stockholders on April 22, 2010, which is the last day of his current three-year term. Additionally, after nineteen years of service as a member of the Board, Mr. Marvin L. Mann, age 76, has decided to retire as a member of the Board, effective as of the date of the Companys Annual Meeting of Stockholders on April 22, 2010. The Company expresses its gratitude to Messrs. Hardymon and Mann for their many years of dedicated service and invaluable counsel to the Company.
