Item 8.01.    Other Events
 
On August 3, 2006, the Board of Directors of Marriott International, Inc. (the "Company") increased by 40 million shares the authorization to repurchase the Company's Class A Common Stock. When combined with the remaining balance in prior share repurchase authorizations, the Company is authorized to purchase approximately 51 million shares. Shares may be purchased in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
