EXHIBIT 99 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE CONTACT: - --------------------- SAMUEL CYPERT 313-792-6646 MASCO CORPORATION UPDATES EARNINGS GUIDANCE Taylor, MI (June 19, 2003) -- Masco Corporation (NYSE: MAS) Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Richard A. Manoogian, speaking at the Company's annual Investors Conference today, made the following comments regarding the Company's current sales and earnings outlook: "The strong sales we achieved in April have continued into May as we continue to gain market share in a number of our product lines. We expect second quarter sales to be up at least 18 to 20 percent from last year's level with full year sales increasing approximately 15 percent to over $10.8 billion. Based on present business trends, as previously forecast, the Company continues to believe that second quarter earnings should approximate $.43 to $.45 per common share, with full year earnings in a range of $1.65 to $1.70 per common share."
