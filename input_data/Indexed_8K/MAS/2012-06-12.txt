Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On June 6, 2012, Masco Corporation (the "Company") announced that, in connection with his upcoming retirement, Charles F. Greenwood is stepping down as the Company's Vice President  Human Resources. Mr. Jai Shah, 46, has been appointed to serve as the Company's Vice President  Chief Human Resource Officer, effective immediately. Mr. Shah has been employed with the Company since 2003, most recently serving as the Company's Vice President  Finance, Retail/Wholesale Platform.
Mr. Greenwood will continue with the Company until his retirement next year to help ensure a smooth transition as Mr. Shah assumes his new role.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
 
June 12, 2012
