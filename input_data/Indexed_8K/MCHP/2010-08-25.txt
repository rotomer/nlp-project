Item 5.07.                      Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
 
At the Microchip Technology annual meeting of stockholders held on August 20, 2010, the stockholders elected each of the following individuals to serve on the Board of Directors until the next annual meeting of stockholders, or until his successor is duly elected and qualified.
 
 
 
In addition, the following proposal was voted on and approved at the annual meeting.
 
 
Proposal to ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the Company's independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2011.
