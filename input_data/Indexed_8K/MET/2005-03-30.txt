For Immediate Release One MetLife Plaza 27-01 Queens Plaza North [MetLife Logo] Long Island City, NY 11101 Contacts: For Media: John Calagna (212) 578-6252 For Investors: Tracey Dedrick (212) 578-5140 METLIFE ANNOUNCES TERMINATION OF HSR WAITING PERIOD FOR TRAVELERS LIFE & ANNUITY ACQUISITION NEW YORK, March 30, 2005 - MetLife, Inc. (NYSE: MET) announced today the termination of the 30-day waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976 with respect to MetLife's proposed acquisition of Citigroup Inc.'s Travelers Life & Annuity, and substantially all of Citigroup's international insurance businesses. The transaction was announced on January 31, 2005. MetLife anticipates that the transaction, which is still subject to certain conditions, including other regulatory approvals, will close this summer. MetLife, Inc., through its subsidiaries and affiliates, is a leading provider of insurance and other financial services to individual and institutional customers. The MetLife companies serve individuals in approximately 13 million households in the U.S. and provide benefits to 37 million employees and family members through their plan sponsors. Outside the U.S., the MetLife companies serve approximately 9 million customers through direct insurance operations in Argentina, Brazil, Chile, China, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Mexico, South Korea, Taiwan and Uruguay. For more information about MetLife, please visit the company's Web site at www.metlife.com.
