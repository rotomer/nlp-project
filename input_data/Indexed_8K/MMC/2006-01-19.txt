Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
 
As announced in the Current Report on Form 8-K filed by Marsh & McLennan Companies, Inc. ("MMC") on December 22, 2005, MMC's board of directors, at a meeting held on December 16, 2005, elected Leslie M. ("Bud") Baker, Jr. and Marc D. Oken to the board of directors, effective January 18, 2006. At its meeting on January 18, 2006, MMC's board of directors appointed Messrs. Baker and Oken as members of the Audit Committee of the board of directors, effective immediately.
