Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
(b)
 
On March 1, 2007, Lewis W. Bernard advised the board of directors of Marsh & McLennan Companies, Inc. ("MMC") that he will not stand for reelection as a director when his current term expires at MMC's annual meeting of stockholders in May 2007. Mr. Bernard, 65, will continue to serve as a director and the chair of the board's compensation committee for the remainder of his current term.
 
Stephen R. Hardis, MMC's non-executive chairman, said: "The board of directors will miss Lewis Bernard. He has served MMC and its stockholders with great distinction for nearly 15 years. The board thanks Lewis for his contributions, and looks forward to his continued counsel from now through May."
