    On February 4, 2008, Marsh & McLennan Companies, Inc. ("
MMC
") filed a Form 8-K under Item 5.02 disclosing that MMC's Board of Directors (the "Board") had appointed Mr. Brian Duperreault as MMC's president and chief executive officer and had elected Mr. Duperreault as a director of MMC. At the time of that filing, Mr. Duperreault had not been appointed to any committees of the Board.
 
On March 20, 2008, the Board appointed Mr. Duperreault to the Executive Committee and the Finance Committee of the Board, effective immediately.
 
Also on March 20, 2008, the Board appointed Bruce P. Nolop to the Finance Committee of the Board, effective immediately.
