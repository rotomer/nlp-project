Item 5.03.  Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year
The Board of Directors of 3M Company amended paragraph 39 of the Company's Bylaws, effective November 13, 2006, to eliminate the supermajority vote requirements. Prior to this amendment, no provisions of the Bylaws could be adopted, amended or repealed by stockholders, nor could paragraphs 12, 13 or 39 be amended or repealed without an affirmative vote of no less than eighty percent (80%) voting power of all of the Company's outstanding shares of capital stock. An amended paragraph 39 of the Bylaws is attached hereto as Exhibit 3(ii).
