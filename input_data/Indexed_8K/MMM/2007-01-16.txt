Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
Item 5.02.  
(b) On January 10, 2007, director Kevin W. Sharer notified George W. Buckley, the Company's Chairman of the Board, President, and Chief Executive Officer, that he does not wish to stand for re-election to the Board at the Company's annual meeting of shareholders in May for personal reasons. Mr. Sharer will continue as a member of the Board of Directors and as a member of the Compensation Committee and of the Finance Committee until the annual meeting on May 8, 2007. There are no disagreements between the Company and Mr. Sharer.
