On May 4, 2007, Robert B. Mahoney gave notice of his resignation as Executive Vice President of Molex Incorporated and President of Molex's Asia Pacific South region effective as of July 1, 2007. Mr. Mahoney will remain at the Company through October 31, 2007 to assist in a transition.
Mr. Mahoney will be leaving Molex to become Chief Executive Officer of Hi-P International Limited, a company headquartered in Singapore and traded on the Singapore Exchange. Hi-P is a leading supplier of electromechanical modules to the telecommunications, consumer electronics and computing industries. Molex owns approximately 20% of Hi-P and believes that Mr. Mahoney's new position at Hi-P will greatly benefit both companies.
