     On July 21, 2009, Kazumasa Kusaka, who has served as a member of the Board of Directors of Molex Incorporated (the "Company") since May 2007, notified us that he is resigning from the Board, effective July 31, 2009. Mr. Kusaka is resigning due to a change in his principal occupation. Upon Mr. Kusaka's resignation, the Board, in accordance with the Company's Bylaws, will fix the size of the Board at 12 and the number of directors in Class II at three.
