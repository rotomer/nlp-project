Item 8.01. Other Events.
 
On February 1, 2005, Douglas A. Pertz, a director of the registrant, entered into a sales plan (the "Plan") intended to comply with the requirements of Rule 10b5-1(c)(1)(i)(B) under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended. Pursuant to the Plan, Mr. Pertz has instructed Goldman, Sachs & Co., as broker, to sell up to 2,806,945 shares of the registrant's common stock, par value $0.01 per share, representing approximately 0.74% of the registrant's outstanding common stock, beneficially owned by Mr. Pertz pursuant to limit orders at specified prices through January 31, 2006. Mr. Pertz and Goldman, Sachs & Co. may amend or terminate the Plan, if the registrant does not object to the amendment or termination, at a time when Mr. Pertz is not aware of material nonpublic information concerning the registrant or its securities.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
