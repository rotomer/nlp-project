The following information is being "furnished" in accordance with General Instruction B.2. of Form 8-K and shall not be deemed "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall it be deemed to be incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "Securities Act"), or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing:
Mosaic Global Holdings Inc., a wholly-owned subsidiary of The Mosaic Company, is redeeming all remaining outstanding $3,525,000 aggregate principal amount of its 10.875% Senior Notes due 2013 (the "Notes") on August 1, 2008 pursuant to the terms of the Indenture dated as of August 1, 2003 relating to the Notes. The Notes are being redeemed at a price of $1,054.38 per $1,000 principal amount. Accrued and unpaid interest of $54.375 per $1,000 in principal amount of the Notes to the redemption date will be paid to the holders of the redeemed notes registered on the regular record date of July 15, 2008.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
