On April 15, 2009, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of The Mosaic Company ("Mosaic") elected David T. Seaton to serve as a director of Mosaic for a term expiring at the annual meeting of shareholders in 2010 or until his successor is elected and qualified. Mr. Seaton was also appointed to the Audit Committee of the Board.
Mr. Seaton is entitled to compensation for his service as a director of Mosaic pursuant to the policy adopted by the Board for all non-employee directors, including but not limited to a prorated portion, valued at $42,500, of the 2008 annual grant of restricted stock units. The date of grant of such restricted stock units is April 16, 2009.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
