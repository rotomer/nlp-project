Transactions with Cargill, Incorporated
We were formed on October 22, 2004 through the business combination of IMC Global Inc., or IMC, and the fertilizer businesses of Cargill, Incorporated. Cargill, Incorporated and its subsidiaries ("Cargill") owned approximately 64.3% of our outstanding common stock as of July 1, 2009. Seven of our thirteen directors, including the Chairman of our Board of Directors and our Chief Executive Officer and President, were designated by Cargill to serve in their respective capacities for us.
On July 1, 2009, Mosaic de Argentina S.A. executed an offer to sell MAP and MES fertilizers to Cargill S.A.C.I. Product is sold at prices negotiated at the time of sale. This offer is effective until August 31, 2010.
This offer was approved by our Cargill Relationship Committee, an internal management committee comprised of senior managers of the Company, pursuant to authority delegated to it under our Related Person Transactions Approval Policy.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
