Transactions with Cargill, Incorporated
We were formed on October 22, 2004 through the business combination of IMC Global Inc., and the fertilizer businesses of Cargill, Incorporated. Cargill, Incorporated and its subsidiaries ("Cargill") owned approximately 64.05 % of our outstanding common stock as of January 13, 2011.
On January 14, 2010, Mosaic de Argentina Sociedad Anonima and Mosaic Fertilizantes do Brasil Ltda. renewed an agreement to sell fertilizer and feed products to Cargill Bolivia S.A., Cargill's subsidiary in Bolivia. The renewal is effective through December 22, 2011.
On January 14, 2010, Mosaic de Argentina Sociedad Anonima and Mosaic Fertilizantes do Brasil Ltda. renewed an agreement to sell fertilizer and feed products to Cargill Agropecuaria S.A.C.I., Cargill's subsidiary in Argentina. The renewal is effective through December 22, 2011.
Both renewals were approved by our Cargill Relationship Committee, an internal management committee comprised of senior managers of the Company, pursuant to authority delegated to it under our Related Person Transactions Approval Policy.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
