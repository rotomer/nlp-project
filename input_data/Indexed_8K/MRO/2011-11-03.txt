Item 2.01  Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
On November 1, 2011, we completed the acquisition with Hilcorp Resources Holdings, LP to purchase its assets in the Eagle Ford shale formation in Texas for $3.6 billion.  Hilcorp Resources Holdings, LP is a partnership between affiliates of Hilcorp Energy Company and Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. LP.  The assets include approximately 143,000 net acres primarily in Atascosa, Karnes, Gonzales and DeWitt counties in Texas.
