Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
On June 2, 2008, Standard & Poors (S&P) announced that it had lowered its counterparty credit ratings on Morgan Stanley to A+/A-1 from AA-/A-1+ and that it had lowered its counterparty credit ratings on two other major financial services firms.  The outlook assigned to Morgan Stanley by S&P remains negative.
