(c) Effective May 23, 2005, J. Scott Di Valerio was promoted to the position of Corporate Vice President, Finance and Administration and Chief Accounting Officer of Microsoft Corporation (the "Company"). Mr. Di Valerio, age 42, has served as a Corporate Vice President and Corporate Controller of the Company since April 2003. Before joining the Company, Mr. Di Valerio was the vice president of corporate controllership at the Walt Disney Company from January 2001 to April 2003. Before joining Disney, Mr. Di Valerio was the chief financial officer of Mindwave Software Inc. from May 2000 to October 2000. Prior to going to Mindwave, Mr. Di Valerio spent 15 years with PricewaterhouseCoopers. Mr. Di Valerio received his bachelor's degree in business administration at the University of San Diego and is a CPA.
 
Mr. Di Valerio is an at-will employee with the Company. No employment agreement exists between Mr. Di Valerio the Company.
