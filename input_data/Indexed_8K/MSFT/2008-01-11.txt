On January 10, 2008, Microsoft Corp. announced the retirement and transition plan for Jeffrey S. Raikes, president of the Microsoft Business Division. The Company also announced it has hired Stephen Elop, formerly chief operating officer of Juniper Networks, Inc., to succeed Mr. Raikes as president of the Microsoft Business Division beginning late January 2008. Mr. Raikes will continue to serve through September 2008 as a member of the Company's senior leadership team.
