Item 5.    Other Events
        On September 19, 2003, Motorola, Inc. announced that Christopher B. Galvin, chairman and chief executive officer, has informed the Board of Directors of his decision to retire. Mr. Galvin has agreed to remain as chairman and CEO until his successor is named and will work closely with the board's search committee to identify a replacement.
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
