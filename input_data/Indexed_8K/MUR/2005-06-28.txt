This Form 8-K/A amends the Form 8-K filed earlier on June 27, 2005 to correct the revised number of shares registered under Form S-8, Reg. No. 333-119733 to 10,000,000 shares.
 
 
Item 8.01. Other Events
 
As a result of the Company's 2 for 1 stock split on June 3, 2005, the number of shares of the Company's common stock, par value $1.00 per share, registered on Form S-8, Reg. No. 333-119733, was proportionately increased to 10,000,000 shares.
 
 
Pursuant to the Requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this Report to be signed on its behalf by the Undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
