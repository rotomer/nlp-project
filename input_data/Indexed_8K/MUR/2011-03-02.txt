On March 1, 2011, Steven A. Coss, Executive Vice President and General Counsel of Murphy Oil Corporation, retired from the Company. Mr. Coss had served the Company in various capacities for more than 41 years.
Effective with the retirement of Mr. Coss, Walter K. Compton has been appointed Senior Vice President and General Counsel for the Company. Mr. Compton joined the Company in 1988 after obtaining a bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Southern Methodist University and a law degree from the University of Arkansas. Mr. Compton was Vice President, Law from February 2009 to February 2011, and had been Secretary from December 1996 to February 2011.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: March 2, 2011
