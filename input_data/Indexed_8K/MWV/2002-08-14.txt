Item 9. Regulation FD Disclosure
On August 14, 2002, the Principal Executive Officer, John A. Luke, Jr., and the Principal Financial Officer, Karen R. Osar, of MeadWestvaco Corporation (the "Registrant") submitted to the United States Securities and Exchange Commission sworn statements pursuant to Securities and Exchange Commission Order No. 4-460 (June 27, 2002). The filing of these statements with this Form 8-K should not be considered as implying that the Registrant considers these documents to be material in nature.
          A copy of each statement is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and 99.2, respectively.
