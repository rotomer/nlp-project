On April 26, 2005, the stockholders of MeadWestvaco Corporation approved the MeadWestvaco Corporation 2005 Performance Incentive Plan. The section of the definitive proxy statement entitled "3. Proposal for Adoption of MeadWestvaco Corporation 2005 Performance Incentive Plan" is incorporated by reference herein and a copy of the MeadWestvaco Corporation 2005 Performance Incentive Plan is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1 and incorporated by reference herein.
