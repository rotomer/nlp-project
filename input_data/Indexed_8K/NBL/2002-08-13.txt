Item 9. Regulation FD Disclosure.
        On August 13, 2002, Noble Energy, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), filed its quarterly report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended June 30, 2002, with the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC"). Accompanying such report as correspondence to the SEC were certifications of the Chief Executive Officer and the Chief Financial Officer of the Company submitted pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 (18 U.S.C. Section 1350). A copy of each of these certifications is attached as an exhibit hereto.
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
