Dustin A. Hatley, age 45, was appointed as Vice President, Controller and Chief Accounting Officer of Noble Energy, Inc. (the "Company") on February 1, 2012. He previously served as the Company's Corporate Controller from February 2011 and served in other progressive positions of responsibility after joining the Company in March 2005. Prior to joining the Company, Mr. Hatley served as the Chief Accountant and Financial Reporting Manager for ENI Petroleum Co., Inc. in Houston, Texas. Before joining ENI Petroleum, he spent approximately fourteen years in the energy industry while working in Ernst & Young's audit practice or working directly for energy industry service companies. Mr. Hatley is a Certified Public Accountant and holds bachelors and masters degrees in Accounting from the University of Houston Clear Lake.
Mr. Frederick B. Bruning resigned as Chief Accounting Officer effective on February 1, 2012, but will remain with the Company as Vice President until his retirement in March 2012.
