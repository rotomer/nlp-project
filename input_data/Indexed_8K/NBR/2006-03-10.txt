Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
By letter dated and received by the Company March 6, 2006, James C. Flores notified the Company of his resignation from our Board of Directors effective immediately. Prior to his resignation, Mr. Flores served as Chairman of the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors. Mr. Flores also served on the Governance and Nominating Committee and Audit Committee. Mr. Flores did not indicate that he resigned because of a disagreement with the Company over its operations, policies or practices.
