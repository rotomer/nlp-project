Item 8.01  Other Events
Reference is made to Item 3. Legal Proceedings in the Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2003 for FPL Group, Inc. (FPL Group) and Florida Power & Light Company (FPL) and Part II, Item 1. Legal Proceedings in the Quarterly Reports on Form 10-Q for each of the periods ended March 31, 2004 and September 30, 2004 for FPL Group and FPL.
On November 23, 2004, the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Florida, in which the Oorbeek and Berman, Klein and Phillips shareholder derivative suits were pending, issued its Order and Final Judgment of Dismissal approving the Stipulation and Agreement of Settlement of these lawsuits.  The court's order became final on December 29, 2004.
