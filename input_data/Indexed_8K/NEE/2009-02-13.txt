Item 8.01  Other Events
On February 13, 2009, the board of directors of FPL Group, Inc. (FPL Group) declared a regular quarterly common stock dividend of $0.4725 per share, which raises the annual dividend to $1.89 per share.  This represents an increase of approximately six percent from FPL Group's previous quarterly common stock dividend.  The dividend is payable March 16, 2009, to shareholders of record on February 27, 2009.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
