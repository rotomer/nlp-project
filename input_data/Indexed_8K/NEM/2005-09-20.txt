On September 16, 2005, Newmont Mining Corporation of Canada Limited agreed to sell 14,457,441 shares of Kinross Gold Corporation for approximately Canadian $130 million. Newmont Mining Corporation of Canada Limited is a subsidiary of Newmont Mining Corporation (the "Company"). The Company expects to recognize a pre-tax gain of approximately US $19 million on a consolidated basis in the third quarter of 2005 as a result of the sale. The shares had been classified as an available-for-sale marketable security in the Company's financial statements.
