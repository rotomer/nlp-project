On November 21, 2005, Newmont Capital Pty Ltd agreed to sell 81.5 million shares of Oxiana Limited for approximately Australian $106 million (approximately US $78 million). Newmont Capital Pty Ltd is a subsidiary of Newmont Mining Corporation (the "Company"). The Company expects to recognize a pre-tax gain of approximately US $17 million on a consolidated basis in the fourth quarter of 2005 as a result of the sale. The shares had been classified as an available-for-sale marketable security in the Company's financial statements.
