On December 19, 2005, Minera Yanacocha, SRL ("Yanacocha") filed with the Peruvian securities regulatory authority (CONASEV), for its review and approval, a prospectus to register a $200 million bond program in the Peruvian public market. Newmont Mining Corporation holds a 51.35% interest in Yanacocha. The registration would make the bond program available for Yanacocha to make up to $200 million in issuances over a two-year period. Any funds generated from the bond program may be used by Yanacocha for capital expenditures and its general corporate purposes.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
