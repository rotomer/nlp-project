Explanatory Note
This Form 8-K/A amends the current report on Form 8-K filed by Newmont Mining Corporation on June 8, 2007 to provide additional information that was not determined or available at the time the original report was filed.
 
As previously reported in a current report on Form 8-K filed on June 8, 2007, on June 4, 2007, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of Newmont Mining Corporation (the "Company") elected Richard T. O'Brien and Joseph A. Carrabba to fill the newly created vacancies on the Board. At the time the current report on Form 8-K was originally filed, it had not been determined on which committees of the Board of Directors Mr. O'Brien or Mr. Carrabba would serve. On July 18, 2007, Mr. O'Brien was appointed to serve on the Executive-Finance Committee and Mr. Carrabba was appointed to serve on the Operations and Safety Committee.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
