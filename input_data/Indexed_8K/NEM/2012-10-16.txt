Newmont Announces Attributable Third Quarter Gold and Copper Production and Sales
This release reflects Newmont's preliminary third quarter operating results and are subject to change when the Company files its third quarter Form 10-Q on Thursday, November 1st.
DENVER, Oct. 16, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Newmont Mining Corporation (NYSE: NEM) ("Newmont" or the "Company") today reported preliminary third quarter attributable gold and copper production of 1.24 million ounces and 35 million pounds, respectively. Preliminary attributable gold and copper sales were 1.21 million ounces and 38 million pounds, respectively, at average realized prices of approximately $1,659 per ounce of gold and $3.54 per pound of copperThe London PM Fix average gold price for the quarter was $1,652 per ounce. Third quarter production was impacted by lower mill availability and recoveries at Boddington, and lower ore tonnes and grade mined at Tanami in Australia.
