May 5, 2010
 
Newfield recently added additional crude oil hedges. The Company added hedges on 3,000 BOPD from June 2010 - December 2012.  The hedges for June - December 2010 are swaps at $89.05 per barrel.  The hedges for 2011 are three-way collars at an average of $65/$85/$105.50 per barrel.  The hedges for 2012 are three-way collars at an average of $65/$85/$114.73 per barrel.
