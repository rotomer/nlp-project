ITEM: Changes in registrant's certifying accountant
 
 
Item 4. Changes in Registrant's Certifying Accountants.
     On May 24, 2004, the Audit Committee of the Board of Directors of NRG Energy, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), engaged KPMG LLP to serve as the Company's independent auditor beginning immediately. KPMG's engagement with the Company commences with the review of the Company's second quarter 2004 10-Q filing.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Dated: May 25, 2004
