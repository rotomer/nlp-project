NEWS
RELEASE
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
NRG Energy, Inc. (NYSE: NRG) repaid $400 million of the Company's Term Loan B facility on December 29, 2006, thereby completing the debt reduction portion of its previously announced capital allocation program. NRG used cash on hand to fund the repayment.
PRINCETON, NJ; January 8, 2007 
NRG Energy, Inc. owns and operates a diverse portfolio of power-generating facilities, primarily in Texas and the Northeast, South Central and Western regions of the United States. Its operations include baseload, intermediate, peaking, and cogeneration facilities and thermal energy production. NRG also has ownership interests in generating facilities in Australia, Germany and Brazil.
