FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE July 22, 2003 Norfolk Southern Increases Quarterly Dividend NORFOLK, VA - Norfolk Southern Corporation (NYSE: NSC) today announced an increase in the quarterly dividend to 8 cents per share on its common stock, payable on September 10 to stockholders of record on August 1. "Norfolk Southern has a long history of emphasizing a return to shareholders through the payment of dividends," said Chairman, President and CEO David R. Goode.
