NORFOLK
SOUTHERN INCREASES QUARTERLY DIVIDEND
 
NORFOLK, VA -- Norfolk Southern Corporation (NYSE: NSC) today announced an increase in the quarterly dividend to 16 cents per share on its common stock, payable on March 10 to stockholders of record on Feb. 3.
