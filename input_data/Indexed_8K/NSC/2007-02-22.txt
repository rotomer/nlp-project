Item 7.01.       Regulation FD Disclosure
            This Current Report on Form 8-K is being furnished to confirm that the Registrant will revise the carloading data for the week ended February 17, 2007. Originated general merchandise traffic was understated by approximately 3,400 carloads due to a technical error.  Corrected volume data will be available on the Association of American Railroads's website as well as on the Registrant's website at
www.nscorp.com
