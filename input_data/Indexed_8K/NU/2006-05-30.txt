Section 5 -
Corporate Governance and Management
Item 5.02 -
Departure of Principal Officer; Appointment of Principal Officer
On May 30, 2006, John P. Stack, Vice President-Accounting and Controller of Northeast Utilities, left the company. David R. McHale, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, is assuming the duties of principal accounting officer of Northeast Utilities until a replacement is named for Mr. Stack.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
