Item 5.02.   Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On July 1, 2010, Nucor Corporation (the "Corporation") announced that D. Michael Parrish will retire from his position as Executive Vice President of Bar Products of the Corporation, effective August 31, 2010. Mr. Parrish, age 57, joined the Corporation in 1975 and has been an Executive Vice President since 1998. His decision to retire is not a result of any disagreement with the Corporation or its management.
James R. Darsey will be promoted to Executive Vice President of Bar Products upon Mr. Parrish's retirement. He began his career with the Corporation in 1979 and was promoted to the position of President of the Corporation's Vulcraft/Verco Group in 2007.
A copy of the news release announcing the events described above is attached as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated in this report by reference.
