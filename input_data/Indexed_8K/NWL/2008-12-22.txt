(b) On December 17, 2008, James J. Roberts resigned as Executive Group President, Office Products and Cleaning, Organization & Dcor of Newell Rubbermaid Inc. (the "Company"). Mr. Roberts responsibilities will be handled on an interim basis by the Companys President and Chief Executive Officer Mark Ketchum. A copy of the news release issued by the Company on December 17, 2008, describing this personnel change is included as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.

 Newell Rubbermaid (NYSE: NWL) today announced that James J. Roberts has resigned as executive group president of the Office Products and Cleaning, Organization & Dcor segments, by mutual agreement with the company. Roberts joined Newell Rubbermaid in April 2001. His responsibilities will be handled on an interim basis by President and Chief Executive Officer Mark Ketchum.
ATLANTA, December 18, 2008
"We thank Jim for his contributions to Newell Rubbermaid and wish him well in his new endeavors," Ketchum said.
Newell Rubbermaid Inc., an S&P 500 company, is a global marketer of consumer and commercial products with sales of over $6 billion and a strong portfolio of brands, including Rubbermaid, Sharpie, Graco, Calphalon, Irwin, Lenox, Levolor, Paper Mate, Dymo, Waterman, Parker, Goody, Bernzomatic and Amerock. The company is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga.
