" News Corporation (the "Company") (NYSE: NWS, NWSA; ASX: NWS, NWSLV) issued a press release today announcing that the Company's Board of Directors has approved the adoption of the News Corporation 2005 Long-Term Management Incentive Plan (the "2005 Plan").
