Item 8.01.    Other Events
 
        On December 19, 2006, News Corporation acquired NBC Universal, Inc.'s 25% stakes in NGC Network International LLC ("NGC International") and NGC Network (UK) Limited ("NGC UK"), two joint ventures which produce and distribute the National Geographic Channel in various international markets.
        The transaction increases News Corporation's interest in NGC International to 75%, with National Geographic Television ("NGT") holding the remaining interest.   NGC UK is now owned by News Corporation (25%), NGT (25%) and British Sky Broadcasting Group plc (50%).
