Item 8.01.    Other Events
 
On January 31, 2007, News Corporation (the "Company") and VeriSign, Inc. ("VeriSign") finalized the formation of a joint venture whereby the Company acquired a controlling interest in VeriSign's wholly-owned Jamba subsidiary, and Jamba was combined with the Company's Fox Mobile Entertainment assets to form a leading global provider of mobile entertainment.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
