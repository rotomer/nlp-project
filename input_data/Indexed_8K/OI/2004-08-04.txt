ITEMS 9 AND 12. REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
        During the week beginning August 2, 2004, management of Owens-Illinois, Inc. scheduled meetings with several equity analysts to discuss the changes in the Company's business portfolio in light of the June 21, 2004, acquisition of BSN Glasspack and the planned divestiture of the Company's blow molded plastics business announced July 28, 2004. The Company's presentation at the meetings, entitled "Presentation to Equity AnalystsAugust 2004," has been made available at the Owens-Illinois webt site,
www.o-i.com
, in the Investor Relations section under "Annual Reports and Presentations."
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
