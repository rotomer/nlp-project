TOLEDO, Ohio, Nov. 15, 2004  Owens-Illinois, Inc. (NYSE: OI) ("") announced today that it commenced a cash tender offer for any and all of its outstanding 7.15% Senior Notes due 2005 (the "") in the aggregate principal amount of $350 million (the "") and that BSN Glasspack S.A., an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Owens-Illinois (""), commenced a cash tender offer for any and all of the approximately 140 million euros of outstanding 10-1/4% Senior Subordinated Notes due 2009 of BSN Financing Co. S.A. (the "") and any and all of the approximately 160 million euros of outstanding 9-1/4% Senior Subordinated Notes due 2009 of BSN Glasspack Obligation S.A. (the "") (collectively, the "").  In connection with the Owens-Illinois Offer and BSN Offers, Owens-Illinois and BSN are soliciting respective holders to consent to proposed amendments to the indentures governing the respective notes, which will eliminate certain covenants and events of default.  Holders tendering their notes will be required to consent to the proposed amendments.  Holders may not tender their notes without delivering consents or deliver consents without tendering their notes.

The Owens-Illinois Offer will expire at 12:01 a.m., New York City time, on Tuesday, December 14, 2004, unless extended or earlier terminated (the "").  Holders who validly tender their 7.15% Notes and validly deliver their consents prior to the Owens-Illinois Offer Expiration Time will be entitled to receive $1,003.55, payable in cash, for each $1,000 of 7.15% Notes accepted for payment.  Holders who validly tender their 7.15% Notes and validly deliver their consents prior to 5:00 p.m., New York City time, on Monday, November 29, 2004, unless extended or earlier terminated, will be entitled to receive $1,018.55, payable in cash, for each $1,000 principal amount of 7.15% Notes accepted for payment, which amount includes a consent payment of $15.00 per $1,000 of 7.15% Notes accepted for payment.  All holders tendering before the Owens-Illinois Offer Expiration Time will be paid accrued and unpaid interest on the principal amount tendered to, but excluding, the date such notes are accepted for payment.

Owens-Illinois may amend, extend or, subject to certain conditions, terminate the Owens-Illinois Offer.
