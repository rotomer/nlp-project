Item 8.01
Other Events
 
On November 16, 2005, ONEOK, Inc. (the "Company") completed the remarketing of its Senior Notes due 2008 (the "Senior Notes"), which were originally issued in January 2003 as a component of the Company's corporate units.
 
The Senior Notes were remarketed pursuant to a Remarketing Agreement Supplement dated November 8, 2005 among the Company, UBS Securities LLC and SunTrust Bank (which incorporates by reference the terms and conditions of a Remarketing Agreement dated January 28, 2003, among the Company, UBS Warburg LLC, Banc of America Securities LLC, J.P. Morgan Securities, Inc. and SunTrust Bank).
 
The Senior Notes were remarketed under the Company's Registration Statement on Form S-3, as amended (SEC Registration No. 333-102105), including the Company's Prospectus, dated January 23, 2003, as supplemented by a Prospectus Supplement dated November 10, 2005.
 
The attached Exhibits to this Form 8-K contain documents relating to this transaction.
