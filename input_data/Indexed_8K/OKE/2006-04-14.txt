TULSA, Okla. -- April 13, 2006 -- ONEOK, Inc. (NYSE:OKE) and Northern Border Partners, L.P. (NYSE: NBP) will release their first-quarter 2006 earnings on May 2, 2006.
A joint conference call will be held the following day on May 3, 2006, at 11 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time (10 a.m. Central Daylight Time). The call will also be carried live on ONEOK's and Northern Border Partners' Web sites.
ONEOK's and Northern Border Partners' senior management teams will participate in the call and webcast.
