ONEOK to Participate in Barclays Capital CEO Energy Conference
 
 TULSA, OklaAug. 31, 2009 -- ONEOK, Inc. (NYSE: OKE) will present at the Barclays Capital CEO Energy Conference on Thursday, Sept. 10, 2009, in New York City, at 9:45 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time (8:45 a.m. Central Daylight Time).
   
. 
 
 John W. Gibson, ONEOK chief executive officer, will present.

www.oneok.com
 
   
 The company will also post the presentation on its Web site that morning, beginning at 8 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time (7 a.m. Central Daylight Time).
   
 
ONEOK, Inc. (NYSE: OKE) is a diversified energy company. We are the general partner and own 45.1 percent of ONEOK Partners, L.P. (NYSE: OKS), one of the largest publicly traded master limited partnerships, which is a leader in the gathering, processing, storage and transportation of natural gas in the U.S. and owns one of the nations premier natural gas liquids (NGL) systems, connecting NGL supply in the Mid-Continent and Rocky Mountain regions with key market centers. ONEOK is among the largest natural gas distributors in the United States, serving more than two million customers in Oklahoma, Kansas and Texas. Our energy services operation focuses primarily on marketing natural gas and related services throughout the U.S. ONEOK is a Fortune 500 company.
