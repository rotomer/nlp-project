Section 8  Other Events
 
Item 8.01 Other Events
 
On March 20, 2007, Charles H. O'Reilly, Jr, Vice-Chairman of the Board of Directors of O'Reilly Automotive, Inc. (the "Company"), as trustee of a trust for the benefit of his child, established a plan in accordance with Rule 10b5-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, for the trading of the Company's common stock.   The plan provides for the sales of specified share amounts at specific market prices, subject to specified limitations. The plan was established for the purpose of diversifying the trust's portfolio. The plan was established during the Company's trading window and at a time when Mr. O'Reilly was not in possession of material, non-public information about the Company. Mr. O'Reilly has informed the Company that he will publicly disclose, as required by federal securities laws, any stock sales made under this plan.
