SPRINGFIELD, Mo., April 1, 2011 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- O'Reilly Automotive, Inc. (the "Company") (Nasdaq:ORLY), a leading retailer in the automotive aftermarket industry, announces the release date for its first quarter 2011 earnings as Wednesday, April 27, 2011, with a conference call to follow on Thursday, April 28, 2011.
The Company's first quarter 2011 earnings will be released after 5:30 p.m. central time on Wednesday, April 27, 2011, and can be viewed, at that time, on the Company's website at www.oreillyauto.com by clicking on "Investor Relations" and then "News Room".

Interested analysts are invited to join the call. The dial-in number for the call is (706) 679-5789 and the conference call identification number is 52475849. A replay of the call will also be available on the Company's website following the conference call.
