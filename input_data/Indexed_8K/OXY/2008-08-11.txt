Item 8.01.              Other Events.
 
On August 11, 2008, Occidental filed a post-effective amendment to its shelf registration statement (No. 333-152875), to limit the shelf to only senior debt securities, which it may offer from time to time. The Company has terminated its medium-term note program and has no current plans to make any offerings of senior debt securities or any other debt or equity securities.
