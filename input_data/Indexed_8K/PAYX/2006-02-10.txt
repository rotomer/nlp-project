ITEM 1.01. ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT.
     The Board of Directors of Paychex, Inc. (the "Company") approved an increase in the annual base salary for Jonathan Judge, President and Chief Executive Officer, of $50,000, or 6.25%, to $850,000 annually. The Board of Directors reaffirmed that Mr. Judge may receive an annual bonus up to 100% of his annual base salary for the Company's fiscal year ending May 31, 2006 and the Board approved an increase in the annual bonus to 120% of annual base salary for the Company's fiscal year ending May 31, 2007.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized.
