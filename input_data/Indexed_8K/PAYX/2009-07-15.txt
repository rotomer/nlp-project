Additional information regarding compensation awarded to certain of the Company's executive officers for the year ended May 31, 2009 will be provided in the Company's proxy statement for its 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders, which is expected to be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission in September 2009.
On July 9, 2009, the Company's Board accepted Phillip Horsley's resignation from the Board effective on October 12, 2009, the evening prior to the expected date of the Company's Annual Meeting of Stockholders. There were no disagreements between Mr. Horsley and the Company relating to the Company's operations, policies, or practices involved in Mr. Horsley's decision not to stand for re-election as a director.
Mr. Horsley has been invaluable to the Company during his nearly 30-year association with the Company. The Board thanks Mr. Horsley for his contributions and wishes him much success in his future endeavors.
