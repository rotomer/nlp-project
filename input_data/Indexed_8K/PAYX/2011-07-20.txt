Effective immediately, Michael Gioja has been named Senior Vice President of Product Development and Information Technology for Paychex, Inc. (the "Company"). Mr. Gioja has been with the Company since November 2008 as Vice President of Product Management, subsequently adding development to his responsibilities. He now assumes responsibility for all of the Company's strategic technology initiatives.
Michael McCarthy, Vice President of Major Market Services Sales, announced his retirement from the Company effective August 1, 2011. Christian Timol, an 18-year veteran of the Company, will assume the role of Vice President of Major Market Services Sales. Mr. Timol has held a number of senior management positions within the sales organization during his tenure with the Company, most recently as a Zone Sales Manager.
