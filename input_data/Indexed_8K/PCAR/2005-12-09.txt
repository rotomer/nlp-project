Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
     
 
PACCAR Inc (the registrant) is filing this amendment to its Current Report on Form 8-Kfiled on September 19, 2005, which reported the naming of Alison J. Carnwath to the Board of Directors, effective October 3, 2005. The information requested in Item 5.02(d)(3) was not available at that time.
 
On December 6, 2005, the Board of Directors of the registrant appointed Ms. Carnwath to the Compensation Committee effective January 1, 2006.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
