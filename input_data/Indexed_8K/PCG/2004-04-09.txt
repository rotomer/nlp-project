Pacific Gas and Electric Companys Chapter 11 Proceeding
          
On April 9, 2004, the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California (District Court) denied a motion to stay the implementation of Pacific Gas and Electric Companys plan of reorganization (Plan) on the previously announced effective date of April 12, 2004.  The request for a stay had been filed by two California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) commissioners who filed an appeal of the order issued by the
U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of California confirming the Plan
.
