Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
As previously reported in a Current Report on Form 8-K dated December 20, 2006, on
December 20, 2006, the Boards of Directors of PG&E Corporation and Pacific Gas and Electric Company (Utility)
elected Richard A. Meserve to serve as a director of PG&E Corporation and the Utility, effective immediately. On February 21, 2007, the PG&E Corporation Board of Directors appointed Dr. Meserve as a member of the Public Policy Committee of the PG&E Corporation Board of Directors effective upon the adjournment of the meeting of the Board held on February 21, 2007.
              
Dated: February 23, 2007
