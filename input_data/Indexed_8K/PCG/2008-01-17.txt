Item 5.02.  Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
On January 11, 2008, David A. Coulter, a member of the Boards of Directors of PG&E Corporation and its subsidiary, Pacific Gas and Electric Company (Utility), provided notice that he will be retiring from his position as a director after more than 10 years of service by not standing for re-election as a director at PG&E Corporations and the Utilitys joint annual meeting of shareholders to be held on May 14, 2008.  In providing notice, Mr. Coulter cited his increasing responsibilities as Managing Director and Senior Advisor of Warburg Pincus LLC, a global private equity firm.
Mr. Coulter has been a director of PG&E Corporation and the Utility since 1996.  He is currently a member of the Executive Committee of each of the Boards of Directors of PG&E Corporation and the Utility.  He also is a member of the Compensation Committee of the PG&E Corporation Board of Directors and serves as Chair of the Finance Committee of the PG&E Corporation Board of Directors.
