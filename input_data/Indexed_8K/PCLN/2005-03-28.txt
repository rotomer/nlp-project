Item 7.01.
             
Regulation FD Disclosure
 
On March 28, 2005, priceline.com Incorporated announced that it had completed the initial development phase of its retail hotel service and begun testing various merchandising presentations of the new service on its website, www.priceline.com.  Priceline.com also announced that it has begun to test-run a new television advertising campaign featuring William Shatner on certain cable networks.  The advertising campaign highlights the new service launch and emphasizes the broad range of choices that are now available to priceline.com's customers.  The advertising campaign is expected to be widely broadcast on cable television outlets beginning the week of April 11.
 
 
               
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
 
 
Date:  March 28, 2005
