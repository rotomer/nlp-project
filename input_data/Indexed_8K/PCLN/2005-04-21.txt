Item 1.01.
Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
                                         
 
On April 19, 2005, the Board of Directors of priceline.com Incorporated authorized the following annual compensation for service as the chairperson of priceline.com's Board of Directors: an annual fee of $25,000 and an annual grant of 8,000 stock options, which stock options will vest over a three-year period.  The compensation is in addition to the compensation received for service as a director.  Ralph Bahna, the chairman of priceline.com's Board of Directors, received a grant of 8,000 stock options on April 19, 2005 and will be paid an annual fee of $25,000, retroactive to January 1, 2005, for his service as Chairman of priceline.com's Board of Directors.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
