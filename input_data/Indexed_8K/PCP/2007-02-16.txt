On February 15, 2007, the Board of Directors of Precision Castparts Corp. (the "Company") elected Ulrich (Rick) Schmidt as a member of the Company's Board of Directors. Mr. Schmidt was appointed to serve on the Audit Committee of the Company's Board of Directors. In accordance with Oregon law, Mr. Schmidt will stand for election by the Company's shareholders at the 2007 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Mr. Schmidt will receive compensation for his services in accordance with the Company's non-employee director compensation program, which is described in the Current Report on Form 8-K filed by the Company on November 16, 2006.
