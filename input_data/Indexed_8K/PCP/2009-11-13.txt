(d) On November 11, 2009, the Board of Directors of Precision Castparts Corp. (the "Company") elected Richard L. Wambold as a director. Mr. Wambold was appointed to serve on the Nominating & Corporate Governance Committee. In accordance with Oregon law, Mr. Wambold will stand for election by the Company's shareholders at the 2010 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Mr. Wambold will receive compensation for his services in accordance with the Company's non-employee director compensation program, which is described in the Company's July 1, 2009 Proxy Statement.
