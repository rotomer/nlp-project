(b)(c) On June 17, 2008, the board of directors of Patterson Companies, Inc. (the "Company") elected Daniel H. Peckskamp to the position of Vice President, Operations and Shared Services. Prior to joining the Company, Mr. Peckskamp was with ADC Telecommunications, Inc. in a variety of positions since 1985, most recently Vice President of Global Operations. Mr. Peckskamp replaces Gary D. Johnson, the Company's former Vice President, Operations, who resigned from his position effective June 17, 2008, in contemplation of his retirement. Mr. Johnson will continue to work with the Company as a consultant through the end of calendar 2008.
