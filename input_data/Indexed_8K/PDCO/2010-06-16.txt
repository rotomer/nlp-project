(d) On June 15, 2010, the Board of Directors of Patterson Companies, Inc. (the "Company") elected Scott P. Anderson as a member of the Board of Directors. Mr. Anderson, who has served as the Company's President and Chief Executive Officer since April 25, 2010, will join the Board effective immediately. Mr. Anderson had held the position of President of the Company's subsidiary Patterson Dental Supply, Inc., since June 2006 and previously held the positions of Vice President, Sales and Vice President, Marketing of Patterson Dental Supply, Inc. At the Annual Meeting in 2010, Mr. Anderson will be a nominee for election as Director for a term expiring at the Annual Meeting in 2012.
