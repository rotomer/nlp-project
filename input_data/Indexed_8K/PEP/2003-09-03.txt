PEPSICO CONFIRMS EARLIER GUIDANCE
PURCHASE, N.Y. Sept. 3, 2003  PepsiCo today confirmed earlier statements that, for full year 2003, it expects to deliver reported EPS of $2.16 to $2.19, which includes about 3 cents of merger costs. The company also confirmed earlier guidance that it expects to generate cash from operations, after capital spending, of $3 billion. This assumes $1.5 billion of capital spending and $400-500 million of pension contribution.
This confirmation was provided in advance of a presentation to investors by Indra K. Nooyi, PepsiCo President and CFO, at the Prudential Back-to-School Consumer Conference in Boston on September 3 at 9:45 a.m. Eastern time. A live webcast of the presentation will be accessible through PepsiCo's website at and will be available for replay at the site for a period of 90 days.
