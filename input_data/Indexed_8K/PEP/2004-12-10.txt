        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.

Exhibit 99.1
 PepsiCo management today stated that, for the four weeks ended November 28, the Company's Frito-Lay North America division saw positive net price realization. The Company also said it expects to see positive net price realization for the fourth quarter, approximately in line with the first three quarters of the year.
PURCHASE, NY (December 10, 2004)
The comments were made in response to questions regarding syndicated category data that were released today.
