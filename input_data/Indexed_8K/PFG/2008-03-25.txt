Barry Griswell, chairman and chief executive officer of Principal Financial Group, Inc. and its subsidiary Principal Life Insurance Company, plans to retire from the companies on December 31, 2008. Principal Financial Group, Inc. and Principal Life Insurance Company announced on February 26, 2008 that Larry D.
