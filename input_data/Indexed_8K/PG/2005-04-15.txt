THE PROCTER & GAMBLE COMPANY NEWS RELEASE One P&G Plaza Cincinnati, OH 45202 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE DE SOLE RESIGNS FROM P&G BOARD TO PURSUE NEW VENTURE ---------------------------------------------------- CINCINNATI, April 15, 2005 - The Procter & Gamble Company [NYSE:PG] announced today that it has accepted the resignation of Domenico De Sole, 61, as a director of the company, effective immediately. "I have greatly valued my work as a P&G director over the past four years," said Mr. De Sole. "I am stepping off the board so I can focus additional time on a variety of businesses, including my new venture with Tom Ford and Estee Lauder. It has been an honor to serve this great company, and I remain confident about its future success." "Domenico De Sole has made important contributions to the company and to the work of P&G's board, for which we are thankful," said A.G. Lafley, P&G chairman, president and chief executive. "We have benefited greatly from his wisdom and experience and wish him continued success."
