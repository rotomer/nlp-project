ITEM 7.01 REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
     On October 9, 2007, The Procter & Gamble Company (the "Company") announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of thirty-five cents ($0.35) per share on the Common Stock and on the Series A and Series B ESOP Convertible Class A Preferred Stock of the Company, payable on or after November 15, 2007 to shareholders of record at the close of business on October 19, 2006. The Company is furnishing this 8-K pursuant to Item 7.01, "Regulation FD Disclosure."
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this Report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
99.     News Release by The Procter & Gamble Company dated October 9, 2007.

 
Exhibit 99
     CINCINNATI, Oct. 9, 2007  The Board of Directors of The Procter & Gamble Company (NYSE:PG) declared a quarterly dividend of thirty-five cents ($.35) per share on the Common Stock and on the Series A and Series B ESOP Convertible Class A Preferred Stock of the company, payable on or after November 15, 2007 to shareholders of record at the close of business on October 19, 2007.
     The company has been paying dividends without interruption since incorporation in 1890.
