ITEM 7.01    REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE;
 
On October 9, 2007, The Procter & Gamble Company furnished a Form 8-K announcing the Companys quarterly dividend. The text of that 8-K incorrectly identified the record date as October 19, 2006. The correct record date is October 19, 2007. The Company is furnishing this amended 8-K pursuant to Item 7.01, "Regulation FD Disclosure."

99.    News Release by The Procter & Gamble Company dated October 9, 2007.
 

 
                                                                                                          
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
CINCINNATI, Oct. 9, 2007 - The Board of Directors of The Procter & Gamble Company (NYSE:PG) declared a
quarterly dividend of thirty-five cents ($.35) per share on the Common Stock and on the Series A and Series B ESOP Convertible Class A Preferred Stock of the company, payable on or after November 15, 2007 to shareholders of record at the close of business on October 19, 2007.
The company has been paying dividends without interruption since incorporation in 1890.
