ITEM 5.02(b) & (c)   DEPARTURE OF CERTAIN OFFICERS; APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN
OFFICERS
On February 13, 2008, The Procter & Gamble Company (the Company) announced that James J. Johnson, Chief Legal Officer and Secretary, will retire effective June 1, 2008. The Company also announced that Steven W. Jemison (56), currently Deputy General Counsel, has been elected to succeed Mr. Johnson as Chief Legal Officer and Secretary effective March 1, 2008. 
During the past five years, Mr. Jemison held the following positions at the Company:  Deputy General Counsel (July 2006 - present), Secretary & Associate General Counsel (July 2005- June 2006), and General Counsel  Central & Eastern Europe, Middle East and Africa (February 2003- June 2005).
The Company is filing this 8-K pursuant to Item 5.02(b) and (c), "Departure of Certain Officers; Appointment of Certain Officers."
