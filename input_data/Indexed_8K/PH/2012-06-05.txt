(b) On June 4, 2012, Robert P. Barker, a named executive officer of Parker-Hannifin Corporation (the "Company"), notified the Company that he intends to retire. Mr. Barker served as Executive Vice President, Operating Officer and President  Aerospace Group at the time he notified the Company of his retirement. Mr. Barker has agreed to remain with the Company until January 31, 2013 to assist with his successor's transition. Effective July 1, 2012, the Board of Directors of the Company elected Mr. Barker to serve, during such transition period, as an executive officer of the Company with the title Executive Vice President and Operating Officer.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
