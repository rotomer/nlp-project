Item 5.03  Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year
On May 15, 2007, we filed with the State Department of Assessments and Taxation of Maryland (i) Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 510,000 Shares of 8.00% Series I Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock, (ii) Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 800,000 Shares of 7.95% Series J Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock and (iii) Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 800,000 Shares of 7.95% Series K Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock, without further designation or any preferences or relative, participating, optional, conversion or other rights appertaining thereto, or voting powers, restrictions, limitations as to dividends, qualifications, terms or conditions of redemption, other than those, if any, applicable to shares of our preferred stock generally. The shares of 8.00% Series I Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock, 7.95% Series J Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock and 7.95% Series K Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock had been designated as such in anticipation of the possible exchange of the Series I Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units, Series J Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units and Series K Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units, respectively. The Series I Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units were repurchased by our subsidiary, AMB Property II, L.P., and the Series J Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units and Series K Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Limited Partnership Units were redeemed by our subsidiary, AMB Property, L.P. A copy of the Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 510,000 Shares of 8.00% Series I Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock, the Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 800,000 Shares of 7.95% Series J Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock and the Articles Supplementary Redesignating and Reclassifying 800,000 Shares of 7.95% Series K Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock as Preferred Stock are attached to this report as Exhibits 3.1, 3.2 and 3.3, respectively, and are incorporated by reference in this report.
