On January 18, 2010, Philip Morris International Inc. (the "Company") announced that Charles R. Wall, Vice-Chairman and General Counsel of the Company will step down as General Counsel on March 1, 2010, and will retire as Vice Chairman on June 30, 2010, his scheduled retirement date. Mr. Wall will be succeeded as General Counsel by David Bernick, a senior litigation partner with the law firm of Kirkland & Ellis LLP.
