On January 26, 2010, Philip Morris International Inc. (the "Company"), announced that Jean-Claude Kunz, President EEMA Region & PMI Duty Free, will retire on June 30, 2010. Mr. Kunz will be succeeded by Miroslaw Zielinski, who is currently serving as the Company's President, Latin America & Canada. Mr. Zielinski will be succeeded as President, Latin America & Canada by James R. Mortensen, who is currently serving as the Company's Managing Director Mexico, Ecuador & Peru.
