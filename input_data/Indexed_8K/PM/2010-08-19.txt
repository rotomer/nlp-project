(e) For reasons of security and personal safety, Philip Morris International Inc. (the "Company") requires its Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Louis C. Camilleri, to use corporate aircraft for all air travel (whether personal or business).
On August 18, 2010, Mr. Camilleri and PMI Global Services Inc., a subsidiary of the Company, entered into a time sharing agreement (the "Agreement") that will enable Mr. Camilleri to reimburse the Company for his personal use of corporate aircraft. Mr. Camilleri will lease the corporate aircraft from PMI Global Services Inc. for any personal use and will pay the expenses for such personal flights pursuant to the terms of the Agreement. The Agreement may be terminated by either party upon 30 days written notice.
Mr. Camilleri has agreed to reimburse the Company for his personal usage of corporate aircraft under the terms of the Agreement to the extent that the aggregated incremental cost of such usage (as determined by the Company pursuant to Regulation S-K, Item 402) exceeds $200,000 per fiscal year.
The Agreement is attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
