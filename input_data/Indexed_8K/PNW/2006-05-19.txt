Item 1.02. Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.
     On May 16, 2006, Pinnacle West Capital Corporation ("Pinnacle West") terminated the unsecured revolving credit facility entered into on April 21, 2006 with Union Bank of California, N.A., as described in the Form 8-K Report filed on April 24, 2006. On May 15, 2006, an amendment to Pinnacle West's Amended and Restated Credit Agreement, dated as of December 9, 2005 (the "Credit Agreement") became effective reflecting the recent Federal Energy Regulatory Commission actions relating to Pinnacle West and allowing Pinnacle West to meet the Credit Agreement's other borrowing conditions.
