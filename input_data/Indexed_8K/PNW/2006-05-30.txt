8.01 Other Events.
     On May 30, 2006, Arizona Public Service Company ("APS") entered into oral arrangements with members of APS' Nuclear Oversight Committee ("NOC") pursuant to which each will provide nuclear advisory services to APS on an as-requested basis, subject to availability. APS will compensate the members at the rate of $1,600 per day, plus expenses, for these services. William L. Stewart is a member of the NOC and serves on the board of directors of Pinnacle West Capital Corporation, APS' corporate parent.
