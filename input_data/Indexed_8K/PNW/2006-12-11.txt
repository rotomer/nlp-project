Item 8.01. Other Events.
Arizona Public Service Company ("APS") General Rate Case  Power Supply Adjustor ("PSA")
     On December 8, 2006, the Arizona Corporation Commission approved APS' request to continue the interim PSA adjustor until rates become effective as a result of APS' pending general rate case. For additional information about the general rate case, the PSA, and the interim PSA adjustor, see "APS General Rate Case" in Note 5 of Notes to Condensed Consolidated Financial Statements in the Pinnacle West Capital Corporation/APS Report on Form 10-Q for the fiscal quarter ended September 30, 2006.
