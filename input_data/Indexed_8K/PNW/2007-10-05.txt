Item 8.01. Other Events
ACC Staff Report on Palo Verde Unit 1 Outages
     As previously reported, Arizona Public Service Company ("") recorded $79 million of deferrals under APS' power supply adjustor ("") resulting from replacement power costs associated with unplanned outages and reduced power operations at the Palo Verde Nuclear Generating Station (the ""). Virtually all of these deferrals were associated with vibration issues in Unit 1. In May 2006, the Arizona Corporation Commission (the "") directed the ACC staff to conduct a "prudence audit" of outages resulting in the 2006 Deferrals. See "PSA Deferrals Related to Palo Verde Outages" in Note 5 of Notes to Condensed Consolidated Financial Statements in the Pinnacle West Capital Corporation/APS Report on Form 10-Q for the fiscal quarter ended June 30, 2007.
