Pricing of Common Stock Offering
     On April 8, 2010, Pinnacle West Capital Corporation ("PNW") issued a press release related to the pricing of its common stock offering, which is attached to this report as Exhibit 99.1.

Exhibit 99.1
PHOENIX  Pinnacle West Capital Corporation (NYSE: PNW) announced today the pricing of its previously announced public offering of six million shares of common stock at $38.00 per share.
These securities will be issued under a shelf registration statement previously filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The transaction is expected to close on April 14, 2010, subject to customary closing conditions.
In connection with the offering, the Company has granted the underwriters an option for a period of 30 days to purchase up to an additional 900,000 shares of the Company's common stock.
Barclays Capital, Credit Suisse, BofA Merrill Lynch, and Wells Fargo Securities are acting as joint book-running managers for the transaction. Investors can obtain a copy of the final prospectus supplement relating to the offering from:
Pinnacle West is a Phoenix-based company. Through its subsidiaries, the Company generates, sells and delivers electricity and sells energy-related products and services to retail and wholesale customers in the western United States.
