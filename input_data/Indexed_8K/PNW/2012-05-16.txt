ACC Decision in APS General Retail Rate Case
 
On May 15, 2012, the Arizona Corporation Commission approved the regulatory settlement agreement proposed by Arizona Public Service Company ("APS") and other parties in APS's pending general retail rate case without material modifications.
 
For additional information, see "Retail Rate Case Filing with the Arizona Corporation Commission" in Note 3 of Notes to Condensed Consolidated Financial Statements in the Pinnacle West Capital Corporation/APS Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended March 31, 2012.
