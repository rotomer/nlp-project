Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
At its meeting on September 14, 2004, the Board of Directors of Prudential Financial, Inc. (the "Company") determined that it will submit a proposal for shareholder approval at the Company's 2005 annual meeting to amend the Company's certificate of incorporation to provide for the annual election of all directors. Under the Company's certificate of incorporation, at least 50% of the shares entitled to vote must vote on such a proposal and 80% of the votes cast must support the proposal.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: September 16, 2004
