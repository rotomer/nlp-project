Item 2.03 Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant.
 
On September 30, 2005, the registrant and certain of its subsidiaries entered into a five-year unsecured credit facility (the "Credit Facility") with Citicorp USA, Inc., as administrative agent, and certain banks named therein, as lenders thereunder, in an aggregate amount of up to $1,500,000,000. The Credit Facility replaces the registrant's exiting five-year credit facility. The registrant intends to use any borrowings under the Credit Facility for the general corporate purposes of the registrant and its subsidiaries (including in connection with the commercial paper program of the registrant and certain of its subsidiaries). The Credit Facility is subject to early termination by the registrant upon satisfaction of customary notice and other requirements. Borrowings under the Credit Facility are subject to acceleration upon the occurrence of customary events of default.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: October 4, 2005
