PRESS RELEASE
04-04 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Quanta Services, Inc. (NYSE: PWR) today announced that after review of operating results, it has revised its loss per share estimate for the first quarter ended March 31, 2004. The first quarter was impacted by adverse weather conditions, project delays and increased costs. The first quarter loss is now expected to range from $0.10 to $0.11 per share, down from prior guidance that ranged between a $0.04 to $0.07 loss per share. Actual first quarter results will be released during the week of May 3, 2004.
