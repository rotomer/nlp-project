Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.
     Effective in the quarter ended September 30, 2009, Quanta Services, Inc. began reporting its operating results under four reportable segments: (1) Electric Power Infrastructure Services, (2) Natural Gas and Pipeline Infrastructure Services, (3) Telecommunications Infrastructure Services and (4) Fiber Optic Licensing. The information contained in Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K provides quarterly segment data for each of the quarters in 2008 and 2009 in accordance with this updated reportable segment structure and is being provided supplementally for comparability purposes.
     The information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including the exhibit furnished pursuant to Item 9.01, shall not be deemed "filed" for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, or otherwise subject to the liabilities under that Section. Furthermore, the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including the exhibit furnished pursuant to Item 9.01, shall not be deemed to be incorporated by reference into the filings of the Company under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
