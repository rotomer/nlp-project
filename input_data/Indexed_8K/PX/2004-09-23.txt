ITEM 5.02(d) Election of Directors
 
As reported on Form 8K dated August 24, 2004, Ira D. Hall, president and chief executive officer of Utendahl Capital Management, L. P., was elected to the board of directors of Praxair, Inc. (NYSE: PX), effective September 1, 2004. This Form 8K/A amends the current report on Form 8K dated August 24, 2004 to report that, at a meeting of the board of directors of Praxair, Inc. held on September 20, 2004, Mr. Hall was appointed to Praxair's Governance and Nominating Committee and its Finance and Pension Committee.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
