Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
 
Praxair, Inc.'s Compensation and Management Development Committee has established 2005 performance goals for determining performance-based annual variable compensation cash awards to be paid to executive officers and other high level executives under the 2002 Praxair, Inc. Variable Compensation Plan (Filed as Exhibit 10.03 to the Company's 2001 Annual Report on Form 10-K). These goals are principally sales revenues, net income after tax and cash flow, as well as non-financial goals in the areas of safety performance, audit and compliance, Six Sigma, strategic positioning of the business for long term performance, consideration of performance relative to peer companies, and people development.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
