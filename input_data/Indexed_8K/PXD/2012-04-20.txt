Pioneer Natural Resources Company (the Company) is furnishing the following update regarding its operations in the Eagle Ford Shale play in South Texas. The Companys average daily net production from the Eagle Ford Shale play was approximately 22.5 thousand barrels oil equivalent per day (MBOEPD) for the first quarter of 2012. The Companys current daily net production from the Eagle Ford Shale play is approximately 23.5 MBOEPD.
