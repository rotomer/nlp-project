On October 1, 2012, Pioneer Natural Resources Company (the Company) will post an Investor Presentation, titled Investor Presentation - October 2012 on the Companys website, www.pxd.com. A copy of the presentation can be reviewed at the website by first selecting Investors, then Investor Presentations. The presentation will include an update of the Company's open commodity derivative positions as of September 28, 2012 on slides 43, 44 and 45. For oil, the update reflects additional swaps and the conversion of certain three-way collars to swaps for 2013, and additional three-way collars for 2014 and 2015. For natural gas liquids, three-way collars were added for 2013 and 2014. For gas, swaps were added for 2014 and three-way collars were added for 2015. A portion of the gas swaps added for 2014 include positions for Pioneer Southwest Energy Partners L.P.
