Item 5.02 - Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
Effective January 1, 2005, David Russell has been named Vice President, Drilling of Rowan Companies, Inc. with responsibility for worldwide drilling operations and will report directly to Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, D. F. McNease. Mr. Russell, who is 43, has been with the company for 21 years and has served as Vice President of Rowan Drilling Company, Inc., a subsidiary of Rowan Companies, Inc. for more than the past five years.
 
Additionally, Henry O. Boswell, a director of Rowan Companies, Inc. since 1988, retired effective January 10, 2005.
