Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
On March 19, 2007, the Company filed a Current Report on Form 8-K (which report is hereby incorporated by reference) reporting short-term incentive awards for 2006, base salary adjustments and short term incentive compensation award targets for 2007. Due to an administrative error, the new base salary for Mr. McNease, the Companys Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer, was reported as $750,000. His actual base salary effective April 1, 2007 is $725,000.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
