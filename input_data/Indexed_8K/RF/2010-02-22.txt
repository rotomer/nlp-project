On February 22, 2010, Regions Financial Corporation ("Regions") announced that it has named David Turner, 46, as Senior Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective as of close of business February 22, 2010. Mr. Turner will succeed Irene Esteves, who is stepping down as Chief Financial Officer of Regions as of that time and is terminating her employment with Regions. Ms. Esteves' departure is not the result of any disagreement regarding the financial statements of Regions. Mr. Turner will serve as a member of Regions' Executive Council and will report to President and Chief Operating Officer O.B. Grayson Hall, Jr. Mr. Turner has been employed by Regions as Executive Vice President and Director of Audit since May 2, 2005. Prior to joining Regions, Mr. Turner was an audit partner at both KPMG LLP and Arthur Andersen.
