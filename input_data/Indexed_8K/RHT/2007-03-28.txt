Effective as of March 22, 2007, Gabriela Gonzalez, Controller and principal accounting officer of Red Hat, Inc. (the "Company"), resigned from the Company voluntarily. Ms. Gonzalez indicated that her resignation was not due to a disagreement with the Company.
Charles E. Peters, Jr., the Company's Chief Financial Officer, has assumed the additional responsibilities of interim Controller and principal accounting officer. Mr. Peters will serve in that capacity until a successor is named for Ms. Gonzalez.
