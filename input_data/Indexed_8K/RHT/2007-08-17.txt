(b) Departure of Director.
On August 16, 2007, Eugene J. McDonald retired from the Board of Directors of Red Hat, Inc. (the "Company"), effective as of the election of the Company's Class II directors at the Company's 2007 Annual Meeting of Stockholders. Having reached the mandatory retirement age for directors under the Company's Corporate Governance Guidelines, Mr. McDonald, whose term as a Class II Director of the Company expired on August 16, 2007, was not eligible to stand for reelection. Mr. McDonald had served as a director since August of 2000.
