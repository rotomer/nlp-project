Item 8.01 Other Events
On December 21, 2012, pursuant to an Agreement and Plan of Merger dated December 19, 2012, by and among Red Hat, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Red Hat"), ManageIQ, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("ManageIQ"), Salta Acquisition Corporation, a Delaware corporation and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Red Hat ("Salta"), and Shareholder Representative Services LLC, the representative of the holders of ManageIQ equity interests, Red Hat completed its previously announced acquisition of ManageIQ through a merger of Salta with and into ManageIQ. ManageIQ develops, distributes and provides support for enterprise cloud management and automation software.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
