Item 8.01        Other Events
On February 20, 2008, Roper acquired through merger The CBORD Group, Inc., a provider of card systems and integrated security solutions to higher education, healthcare and other markets in a transaction valued at $367 million. The acquisition was funded from a combination of cash on hand and borrowings under our previously disclosed 2003 Amended and Restated Credit Agreement.
