On May 22, 2012, Mr. Donald H. Seiler resigned as a member of the Board of Directors of Ross Stores, Inc. (the "Company") and from the Audit Committee and the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Company's Board of Directors, effective as of the close of business June 1, 2012.
