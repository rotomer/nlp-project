(c) Appointment of Certain Officers
     On September 10, 2009, the Board of Directors of Range Resources Corporation approved the appointment of Dori A. Ginn, age 51, as Principle Accounting Officer and Vice President - Controller. Prior to her appointment, Ms. Ginn had served as Vice President  Controller for Range Resources Corporation. Since joining Range in 2001, she has held various positions within Range including Controller and Financial Reporting Manager. Prior to joining Range, Ms. Ginn held various accounting positions with Doskocil Manufacturing Company and Texas Oil and Gas Corporation. Ms. Ginn received a Bachelor of Business Administration in Accounting from the University of Texas at Arlington. She is a Certified Public Accountant.
