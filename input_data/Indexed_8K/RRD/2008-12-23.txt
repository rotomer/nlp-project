Item 8.01.    Other Events
 
On December 22, 2008, the Board of Directors of R.R. Donnelley & Sons Company (the "Company") amended the RR Donnelley Savings Plan to suspend Company matching and equalization contributions with respect to compensation paid on or after January 1, 2009, except where such contributions are required by the terms of collective bargaining or similar agreements.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
