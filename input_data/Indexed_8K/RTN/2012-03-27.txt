Item 5.02.  Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
 
Raytheon Company (the Company) is filing this amendment to its Current Report on Form 8-K filed on January 27, 2012, which reported the election of James E. Cartwright as an independent director to the Companys Board of Directors (the Board).  At the time of his election, the Board had not decided on which of the Boards standing committees Mr. Cartwright would serve.
On March 21, 2012, the Board voted to appoint Mr. Cartwright to the Boards Audit Committee, Public Affairs Committee and Special Activities Committee, effective immediately as of such date.
