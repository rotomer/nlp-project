- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers. This current report on Form 8-K/A updates information provided on a Form 8-K dated August 7, 2007 in which Sprint Nextel Corporation reported the appointments of Larry C. Glasscock and Rodney O'Neal to its Board of Directors. On November 5, 2007, the Board appointed Mr. Glasscock to serve on both the Audit and Finance Committees and Mr. O'Neal to serve on both the Human Capital & Compensation and Nominating & Corporate Governance Committees. At the same time, the Board appointed Linda Koch Lorimer to the Human Capital & Compensation Committee, Ms. Lorimer stepped down from the Audit Committee and Irvine O. Hockaday, Jr. stepped down from the Finance Committee.
