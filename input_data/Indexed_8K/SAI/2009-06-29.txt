The Annual Meeting of Stockholders of SAIC, Inc. was held on June 19, 2009. All of the directors nominated by management in SAIC's 2009 Proxy Statement were elected and no solicitation in opposition to management's nominees was made. In addition, the stockholders approved the proposal to automatically convert each share of SAIC's class A preferred stock into one share of common stock and ratified the appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as SAIC's independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending January 31, 2010.
