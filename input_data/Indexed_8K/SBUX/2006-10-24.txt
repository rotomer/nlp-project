Item 8.01. Other Events.
     On October 24, 2006, Starbucks Corporation announced that it has acquired ownership of High Grown Investment Group (Hong Kong) Limited, which in turn is the majority owner of Beijing Mei Da Coffee Co. Ltd., the operator of 61 Starbucks retail stores in Beijing and Tianjin, People's Republic of China, and an authorized licensee of Starbucks Coffee International. Financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
