Item 1.01 ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT On Monday, December 13, 2004, SCANA Corporation (SCANA) and H. Thomas Arthur, general counsel of SCANA, South Carolina Electric & Gas Company (SCE&G) and Public Service Company of North Carolina, Incorporated, and a named executive officer of SCANA and SCE&G, announced Mr. Arthur's plans to retire on or about January 3, 2005. In connection with Mr. Arthur's retirement, SCANA has agreed to provide for the continuation of certain customary executive benefits, such as reimbursement of financial counseling costs and executive physical examinations, for the next two years. SCANA is pursuing a consulting arrangement with Mr. Arthur which will provide for professional legal and regulatory advisory services at market rates over a one-to-two year period. Mr. Arthur's services would assist SCANA with the previously announced merger of its two natural gas transmission companies into a new interstate pipeline company in order to comply with certain regulatory requirements of FERC Order 2004.
