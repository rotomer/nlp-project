Item 5.02.    Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
 
On February 21, 2008, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of The Charles Schwab Corporation approved performance criteria under the Corporate Executive Bonus Plan (CEBP) for 2008. These performance criteria include revenue growth and pre-tax profit margin. All executive bonuses under the CEBP for 2008 will be based on overall corporate performance with respect to these criteria.
