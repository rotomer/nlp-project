Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
Item 1.01
               
 
At a meeting of the Board of Directors (the "Board") of Sealed Air Corporation (the "Corporation") held on December 15, 2005, the Board set the amount of the Annual Retainer (as defined in the Corporation's 2002 Stock Plan for Non-Employee Directors) to be paid to non-employee directors of the Corporation who are elected at the 2006 Annual Meeting of Stockholders at $75,000.  The annual retainer that was earned by a non-employee director of the Corporation who was elected at the Corporation's 2002 through 2005 annual meetings was $60,000.  The other compensation arrangements for non-employee directors of the Corporation remain as previously reported in the Corporation's proxy statement for its 2005 annual meeting.  A copy of the Board's adopting resolution is included as Exhibit 10 to this report and is incorporated herein by reference.
