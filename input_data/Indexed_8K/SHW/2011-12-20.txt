On October 31, 2011, Gary E. McCullough resigned from his position as President and Chief Executive Officer, and as a member of the Board of Directors, of Career Education Corporation. Mr. McCullough also tendered his resignation from his position as a member of the Board of Directors of The Sherwin-Williams Company ("Sherwin-Williams") in accordance with Sherwin-Williams' Corporate Governance Guidelines, which require a director to tender his or her resignation upon a significant change in the director's employment. On December 14, 2011, the Board of Directors of Sherwin-Williams accepted Mr. McCullough's resignation, effective immediately. Mr. McCullough did not resign on account of any disagreement on any matter relating to Sherwin-Williams' operations, policies or practices.
