Item 1.01.     Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
          On December 16, 2005, The J. M. Smucker Company (the "Company") entered into a consulting agreement with Mr. Richard G. Jirsa, a Vice President of the Company who will retire on December 30, 2005.  Under this agreement, Mr. Jirsa will provide consulting services related to accounting and finance matters.  The consulting agreement is effective January 1, 2006 and provides for a term of two years (unless earlier terminated in accordance with its terms).  Mr. Jirsa will receive a consulting fee of $100,000.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
