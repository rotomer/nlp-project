Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
On March 29, 2005, Schlumberger Limited ("Schlumberger") sold its facility in Montrouge, France to a third party for 173.5 million Euros (approximately US$227 million). Schlumberger expects to record a gain related to this sale in the first quarter of 2005. The sale of the Montrouge facility completes the restructuring and refocusing of Schlumberger's business announced two years ago.
