Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
SLM Corporation (the "Company") held its 2012 Annual Meeting of Shareholders on May 24, 2012. At the meeting, the following proposals were submitted to a vote of our shareholders, with the voting results indicated below:
. Our shareholders elected the following 15 directors to hold office until the 2013 annual meeting of shareholders and until their successors have been duly elected or appointed.
Proposal 1  Election of Directors
 
. Our shareholders approved the 2012 Omnibus Incentive Plan.
Proposal 2  Approval of SLM Corporation 2012 Omnibus Incentive Plan
 
. Our shareholders approved the Amended and Restated Employee Stock Purchase Plan.
Proposal 3  Approval of the Amended and Restated SLM Corporation Employee Stock Purchase Plan
 
. Our shareholders approved, by an advisory vote, the compensation of our named executive officers.
Proposal 4  Advisory Vote on Executive Compensation
 
. Our shareholders ratified the appointment of KPMG LLP as our independent registered public accounting firm for the year ending December 31, 2012.
Proposal 5  Ratification of the Appointment of KPMG LLP
 
Item 8.01 Other Events.
On May 24, 2012, our board of directors approved an additional $400 million to be utilized in the Company's ongoing share repurchase program previously announced in January 2012.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
