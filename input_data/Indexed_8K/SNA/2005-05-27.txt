Item 5.02
Appointment and Departure of Principal Officers
        On May 23, 2005, Constance R. Johnsen, 48, was appointed to the position of Chief Accounting Officer for Snap-on Incorporated. Mrs. Johnsen is also a Vice President of Snap-on Incorporated and the Controller. She has held both positions since October 2003. From July 2000 to October 2003 she was Director, Corporate Financial Reporting for Snap-on Incorporated and from January 2000 to July 2000 she was Senior Director, Accounting, Consolidations and Reporting for CNH Global N.V., a manufacturer of agricultural and construction equipment.
        On May 23, 2005, Blaine A. Metzger was appointed senior vice president  finance and accounting for Snap-on Tools Company LLC. On that same day he relinquished his position as Snap-on Incorporated's Vice President  Finance, Treasurer and Chief Accounting Officer.
