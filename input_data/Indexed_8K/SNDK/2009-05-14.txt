Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
SanDiskCorporation announced today that, as previously disclosed in its proxy statement for its 2009 Annual Meeting, it intends to adopt a majority vote standard for uncontested director elections if its stockholders approve certain amendments to its certificate of incorporation to permit the adoption of a majority vote standard.  If such certificate of incorporation amendments are approved at the 2009 Annual Meeting, SanDisk intends to adopt a majority vote standard through amendments to its bylaws and corporate governance principles no later than August 4, 2009.
