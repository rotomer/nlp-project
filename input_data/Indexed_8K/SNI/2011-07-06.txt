On June 30, 2011, Scripps Networks Interactive, Inc. (the "Company") entered into Amendment No. 2 (the "Amendment") to its Five-Year Competitive Advance and Revolving Credit Facility Agreement dated June 30, 2008 (the "Revolver").
Among other things, the Amendment extends the expiration date of the Revolver to June 30, 2014 and modifies the rates and fees that will be charged to the Company.
Except as expressly amended, the form of the Revolver remains unchanged. The foregoing description of the Amendment does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment, a copy of which is filed herewith as Exhibit 10.1.
