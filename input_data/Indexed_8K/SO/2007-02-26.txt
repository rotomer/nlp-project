On February 20, 2007, the Board of Directors of The Southern Company ("Southern Company") elected H. William Habermeyer, Jr. as a new director of Southern Company, effective March 1, 2007. Mr. Habermeyer also was elected as a member of the Nuclear Committee of the Board of Directors effective March 1, 2007.
From 2000 until his retirement in May 2006, Mr. Habermeyer served as President and Chief Executive Officer of Florida Power Corporation (d/b/a Progress Energy Florida, Inc.), a subsidiary of Progress Energy, Inc. ("Progress Energy"). Mr. Habermeyer joined Progress Energy in 1993 and served in various leadership roles including Vice President of Nuclear Services and Environmental Support, Vice President of Nuclear Engineering and Vice President of Progress Energy's Western Region.
