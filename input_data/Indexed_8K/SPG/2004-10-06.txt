Item 3.02   Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities
 
On October 1, 2004 Simon Property Group, Inc. ("Simon Property") issued an aggregate of 1,156,039 shares of its Series D 8% Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock, par value $.0001 per share (the "Series D Preferred Stock") to a total of thirty-two persons.  The consideration for the issuance of the Series D Preferred Stock was the exchange of an equal number of 8% Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Units of Simon Property's subsidiary, Simon Property Group, L.P.  The issuance of the Series D Preferred Stock was exempt from registration under Section 4(2) of the Securities Act of 1933 as a transaction not involving a public offering of securities.  The Series D Preferred Stock represents "restricted securities" within the meaning of Rule 144.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
