Simon Property Group, Inc. (the "Company") hereby amends its Current Report on Form 8-K dated filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on October 20, 2004 (the "Current Report") to amend Item 9.01 to include required financial statements and pro forma financial information.  At the time of filing of the Current Report disclosing the acquisition of Chelsea Property Group, Inc. ("Chelsea") by the Company, the Company indicated that it would file the necessary financial information not later than 71 days after the date on which the Current Report was required to be filed.  Item 9.01 of the Current Report is herby amended and restated as follows:
