Item 8.01               Other Events
 
On May 11, 2005, the Board of Directors of Simon Property Group, Inc. ("Simon")
authorized a common stock repurchase program under which Simon may purchase up to $250 million of its common stock over the next twelve months as market conditions warrant.   The shares may be repurchased in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions.
