Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
On November 11, 2009, our Board of Directors, upon the recommendation of the Board's Nominating and Governance Committee, elected Jim Reid-Anderson as a director and appointed him to the Board's Compensation Committee.
Mr. Reid-Anderson, 50, is a healthcare adviser to the managing board of Siemens AG, a worldwide manufacturer and supplier of electronics and electrical engineering in the industrial, energy and healthcare sectors. From May through November 2008 he was a member of Siemens AG's managing board and chief executive officer of Siemens Healthcare Sector, and from November 2007 through April 2008 he was the chief executive officer of Siemens Healthcare Diagnostics. From September 2000 to November 2007 Mr. Reid-Anderson served as chairman of the board, president and chief executive officer of Dade Behring Holdings, Inc., which he joined in August 1996.
