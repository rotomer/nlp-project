On April 7, 2008, in connection with her appointment as Executive Vice President and Chief Risk Officer of State Street Corporation, Maureen J. Miskovic resigned as a member of State Street's Board of Directors effective April 7, 2008.
 
In light of her resignation as a director, as described above in Item 5.02, Ms. Miskovic, who is listed as one of the director nominees in the proxy statement for our 2008 annual meeting, will not be standing for re-election. Recognizing Ms. Miskovic's resignation, on April 7, 2008, State Street's Board of Directors set the number of directors constituting the Board at 12 and reduced the number of directors to be elected at the meeting from 13 to 12.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date: April 7, 2008
