Item 5.    Other Events and Required FD Disclosure
 
On July 2, 2003, Bill Bradley, former U.S. Senator and current Managing Director of Allen & Company LLC, was named to Seagate Technology's board of directors. Seagate Technology's board of directors is fixed at 11 members pursuant to the terms of a shareholders agreement among Seagate Technology, New SAC, and certain other persons. Accordingly, on the same date, Seagate Technology accepted the resignation of David J. Roux from the board of directors. From 1997 through 1999, Senator Bradley was a Senior Advisor and Vice Chairman of the International Council of JP Morgan & Co., Inc. Senator Bradley served in the U.S. Senate from 1979 through 1997, representing the State of New Jersey.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
