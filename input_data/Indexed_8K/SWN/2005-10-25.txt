NEWS RELEASE
Houston, Texas  October 25, 2005Southwestern Energy Company (NYSE: SWN) today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a two-for-one stock split.  The Board of Directors established November 3, 2005 as the record date and November 17, 2005 as the date upon which the additional shares will be distributed to shareholders.  As of September 30, 2005, Southwestern had approximately 84.2 million shares of common stock outstanding.  Southwestern's transfer agent, Equiserve Trust Company, N.A., will distribute the new shares.
"Our Board of Directors has determined that a further split of the company's stock is appropriate at this time in light of our continuing strong operational performance," stated Harold M. Korell, President and Chief Executive Officer of Southwestern.
Southwestern Energy Company is an integrated natural gas company whose wholly-owned subsidiaries are engaged in oil and gas exploration and production, natural gas gathering, transmission, and marketing, and natural gas distribution. Additional information on the company can be found on the Internet at
