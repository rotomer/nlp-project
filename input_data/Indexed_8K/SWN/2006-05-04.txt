SECTION 1. Registrant's Business and Operations.
 
Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On May 1, 2006, Southwestern Energy Company (the "Company") entered into a stock purchase agreement ("Stock Purchase Agreement") with Atlas Pipeline Partners, L.P. ("Atlas") pursuant to which Atlas would acquire the Company's subsidiary, Mid-Continent Arkansas Pipeline, LLC (formerly Southwestern Energy Pipeline Company) ("MCAP"), which holds a 25% interest in the NOARK Pipeline System, Limited Partnership ("NOARK"), for $69.0 million. Atlas' subsidiary holds the remaining 75% interest in NOARK.  NOARK owns the Ozark Gas Transmission System, which the Company utilizes to transport natural gas.
A copy of the Stock Purchase Agreement was filed as Exhibit 10.1 to the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K dated May 4, 2006, and is incorporated herein by reference.
