On June 15, 2006, Safeway Inc. ("Safeway") entered into the First Amendment to Credit Agreement (the "First Amendment") by and among Safeway and Canada Safeway Limited ("CSL"), as borrowers, Deutsche Bank AG New York Branch, as administrative agent, Banc of America Securities LLC and J.P. Morgan Securities Inc., as joint lead arrangers, Bank of America, N.A., JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association, Citicorp USA, Inc. and BNP Paribas, as co-syndication agents, U.S. Bank National Association, as documentation agent, and the lenders that are party to the First Amendment. Said parties entered the First Amendment for purposes of extending the termination date of the Credit Agreement (which provides for a revolving credit facility in the aggregate amount of $1.6 billion) for an additional year to June 1, 2011.
A copy of the First Amendment is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
