On February 19, 2009, Douglas J. Mackenzie, a Safeway Board member, gave notice that he has decided not to stand for re-election to Safeway's Board of Directors at our next annual meeting of stockholders. Accordingly, Mr. Mackenzie's service as a Safeway director will end effective May 13, 2009. Mr. Mackenzie will continue to serve as a director of Safeway's subsidiary, Blackhawk Network Holdings, Inc.
