ITEM 3.02: UNREGISTERED SALES OF EQUITY SECURITIES.
     Between August 10, 2004 and November 4, 2004, Symantec issued 35,140,167 shares of its common stock, $0.01 par value per share, upon conversion of $599,844,000 in principal amount of Symantec's 3% convertible subordinated notes due November 1, 2006. The conversion price of the notes was $17.07 per share. The conversion of the notes was exempt from the registration requirements of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, pursuant to Section 3(a)(9) under the Act. Symantec filed a registration statement on Form S-3 for the resale of the notes and the shares of common stock issuable upon conversion of the notes on January 22, 2002.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
