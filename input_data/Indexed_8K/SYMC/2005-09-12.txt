ITEM 5.02. Departure of directors or principal officers; election of directors; appointment of principal officers.
     On September 10, 2005, John G. Schwarz, President of Symantec Corporation ("Symantec"), submitted his resignation as a principal officer of Symantec, effective immediately, to pursue a role as chief executive officer of another technology company.
     Gary L. Bloom, Symantec's Vice Chairman of the Board and President, will assume responsibility for Symantec's six business units. In addition, Mr. Bloom will continue in his leadership role over Symantec's corporate development organization.
     Thomas W. Kendra has been promoted to Executive Vice President responsible for Worldwide Sales and Services.
