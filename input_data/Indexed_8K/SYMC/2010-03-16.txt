Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On March 13, 2010, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of Symantec Corporation (the "Company") effected the following changes to its committee memberships, with such changes to be effective as of the start of the Company's fiscal year beginning on April 3, 2010: (1) William T. Coleman shall cease to be a member of the Compensation Committee and shall become a member of the Audit Committee; and (2) Stephen M. Bennett, who was appointed to the Board effective February 8, 2010, shall become a member of the Compensation Committee.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
