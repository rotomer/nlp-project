Item
 
5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
This amended Form 8-K is being filed to report that on January 26, 2007, the Board of Directors of the Company appointed Reuben V. Anderson to the Finance/Pension Committee and the Public Policy and Environmental Affairs Committee, James H. Blanchard to the Corporate Development Committee and the Human Resources Committee, and James P. Kelly to the Audit Committee and the Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee. A Form 8-K was filed on December 29, 2006 to report that each of Messrs. Anderson, Blanchard and Kelly were elected to the Board of Directors on December 29, 2006.
