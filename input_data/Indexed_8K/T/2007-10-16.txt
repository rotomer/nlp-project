On October 11, 2007, AT&T Inc. announced the retirement of Stan Sigman as President and Chief Executive Officer-AT&T Mobility. Ralph de la Vega replaced Mr. Sigman, and John Stankey assumed responsibility for regional telecom operations, previously headed by Mr. de la Vega. Mr. Sigman will remain with AT&T through the end of the year.
