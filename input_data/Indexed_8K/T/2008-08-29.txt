Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
 
On August 29, 2008, AT&T Inc. ("AT&T"), gave notice to terminate in whole amounts committed by the lenders under its $3 billion revolving credit agreement dated April 11, 2008 (the Agreement).   Pursuant to Section 2.04 of the Agreement, the Agreement will terminate effective September 4, 2008.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
