Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
On November 7, 2008, the registrant AT&T Inc. ("AT&T") announced plans to acquire Centennial Communications Corp. (Centennial), a regional provider of wireless and wired communications services, for $944 million in cash.  Under the terms of the agreement, Centennial shareholders would receive $8.50 per share for a total equity price of $944 million.  Including net debt, the total transaction would be valued at approximately $2.8 billion.  The acquisition is subject to regulatory approval, the approval of Centennial's shareholders and other customary closing conditions.  AT&T expects to obtain approvals by the end of the second quarter of 2009.
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
