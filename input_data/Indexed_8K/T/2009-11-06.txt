Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
 
On November 6, 2009, AT&T Inc. ("AT&T") announced the completion of the acquisition of Centennial Communications Corp.("Centennial"), a regional provider of wireless and wired communications services. Centennial shareholders received approximately $945 million in cash in exchange for their shares. AT&T has called for redemption all of Centennial's outstanding long-term debt with principal amount of approximately $1.4 billion and has terminated Centennial's existing credit facility of approximately $500 million.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
