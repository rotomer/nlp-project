Item 8.01  Other Events.
On January 29, 2010, AT&T Inc. (AT&T) announced that approximately 29,500 employees represented by Communications Workers of America District 3 failed to ratify the tentative labor agreement announced on December 14, 2009.  The company will continue to work to reach an agreement.
