Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
 
On December 6, 2010, AT&T received commitments from a syndicate of banks to replace the companys existing five-year $9.465 billion revolving credit facility with a four-year $5 billion revolving credit facility and a 364-day $3 billion revolving credit facility.  Both new facilities are expected to contain terms substantially similar to those of the existing facility.  AT&T and the committed banks expect both facilities to become effective on December 20, 2010 upon satisfaction of customary closing conditions.
