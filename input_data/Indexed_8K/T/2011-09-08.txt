Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
 
On September 7, 2011, AT&T Mobility LLC (formerly known as Cingular Wireless LLC), a subsidiary of the registrant AT&T Inc. ("AT&T"), provided irrevocable notice to The Bank of New York Mellon Trust Company, N.A., as trustee (the Trustee), to redeem all the outstanding 6.5% Notes due December 15, 2011 with CUSIP number 17248RAF3, in accordance with the terms of the Indenture and Supplemental Indentures with the Trustee.  The redemption date will be October 7, 2011, and the redemption amount will be determined on October 4, 2011.
 
On September 8, 2011, AT&T Corp., a subsidiary of AT&T, provided irrevocable notice to the Trustee to redeem all the outstanding 7.3% Senior Notes due November 15, 2011 with CUSIP number 001957BC2, in accordance with the terms of the Indenture with the Trustee.   The redemption date will be October 11, 2011 and the redemption amount will be determined on October 5, 2011.
 
AT&T expects the aggregate cash payment to be approximately $2.328 billion, including principal of approximately $2.25 billion, accrued interest of approximately $60 million, and a call premium of approximately $18 million.  AT&T expects this redemption to result in a pre-tax gain of approximately $1 million to AT&Ts fourth-quarter 2011 financial results.  The redemption of this debt is expected to be
funded by cash on hand.
