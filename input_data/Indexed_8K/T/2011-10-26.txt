Item 8.01  Other Events.
On October 26, 2011, AT&T Inc. (formerly known as SBC Communications Inc.) provided irrevocable notice to The Bank of New York Mellon, as trustee (the Trustee), to redeem all the outstanding 5.875% Notes due February 1, 2012 with CUSIP number 78387GAH6, in accordance with the terms of the Indenture with the Trustee. The redemption date will be November 28, 2011, and the redemption amount will be determined on November 22, 2011.
AT&T expects the aggregate cash payment to be approximately $1.029 billion, including principal of approximately $1.0 billion, accrued interest of approximately $19 million, and a call premium of approximately $10 million. The redemption of this debt is expected to be funded by cash on hand.
