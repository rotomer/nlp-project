Item 8.01  Other Events.
On November 28, 2011, New Cingular Wireless Services, Inc. (formerly known as AT&T Wireless Services, Inc.), a subsidiary of the registrant AT&T Inc. (AT&T), provided irrevocable notice to U.S. Bank National Association, as trustee (the Trustee), to redeem all the outstanding 8.125% Notes due May 1, 2012 with CUSIP number 00209AAG1, in accordance with the terms of the Indenture with the Trustee. The redemption date will be December 28, 2011, and the redemption amount will be determined on December 22, 2011.
AT&T expects the aggregate cash payment to be approximately $2.078 billion, including principal of $2.0 billion, accrued interest of $25 million, and a call premium of $53 million. The redemption of this debt is expected to be funded by cash on hand.
