Item 8.01  Other Events.
On February 29, 2012, AT&T Inc. (formerly known as SBC Communications Inc.) provided irrevocable notice to The Bank of New York Mellon, as trustee (the Trustee), to redeem all the outstanding 5.875% Notes due August 15, 2012 with CUSIP number 78387GAK9, in accordance with the terms of the Indenture with the Trustee. The redemption date will be March 30, 2012, and the redemption amount will be determined on March 27, 2012.
 
AT&T expects the aggregate cash payment to be approximately $1.028 billion, including principal of approximately $1.0 billion, accrued interest of approximately $7 million, and a call premium of approximately $21 million. The redemption of this debt is expected to be funded by cash on hand.
