ITEM 8.01. OTHER EVENTS
        On September 29, 2004, the Office of Fair Trading in the U.K. referred an agreement between Coors Brewers Limited (CBL), the Company's operating subsidiary in the United Kingdom, and Service Dispense Equipment Ltd. (SDE) to Britain's Competition Commission.
        The agreement between CBL and SDE is to outsource the management and servicing of CBL's on-trade dispense equipment. Coors previously announced this agreement in a release filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on August 3, 2004.
        The Competition Commission is expected to report by March 15, 2005.
        Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
