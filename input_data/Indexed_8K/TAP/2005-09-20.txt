Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
In light of recent reports in the press and elsewhere, Molson Coors Brewing Company reconfirmed today that it is continuing to explore a full range of options for its Brazilian business unit, Cervejarias Kaiser, which includes discussions with third parties regarding the Kaiser business.  As the company indicated in May 2005, it wants to remain in the Brazilian market, but only if it can do so on a winning basis.
