Item 1.01.    Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement
 
On July 26, 2006, the Board of Directors of Molson Coors Brewing Company (the "Company") approved the Directors Stock Plan (the "Plan") under the Molson Coors Brewing Company Incentive Compensation Plan. Pursuant to the terms of the Plan, non-employee members of the Board of Directors may elect to receive their annual retainer in one of three forms: (i) 100% in cash; (ii) 50% or 100% in shares of Company stock with the balance in cash; or (iii) 50% or 100% in deferred share units ("DSUs") with the balance in cash. DSUs are deferred until the Director's service on the Board terminates at which time shares of Company stock will be issued. Elections under the Plan will be made on an annual basis. The Chairman of the Board and Vice Chairman of the Board are not eligible to participate in the Plan.
