Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
 
On February 27, 2012, Molson Coors Brewing Company (the "Company") filed a Form 8-K disclosing that the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board") had appointed Douglas Tough to serve as a member of the Board, effective February 23, 2012.  On June 4, 2012, the Company filed a Form 8-K disclosing that the Board had appointed Louis Vachon to serve as a member of the Board, effective May 31, 2012.  At the time of the respective Form 8-K filings, neither Mr. Tough nor Mr. Vachon had been appointed to any committees of the Board.
 
The Company is filing this amended Form 8-K to report that, on July 19, 2012, the Board appointed Mr. Tough to serve on the Company's compensation committee, and Mr. Vachon to serve on the Company's audit committee, effective July 20, 2012.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Date:
July 24, 2012
