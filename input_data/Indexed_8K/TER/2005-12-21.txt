Item 8.01    Other Events.
 
During the fourth quarter of 2005, Teradyne repurchased $71.5 million of its 3.75% Convertible Senior Notes due 2006 (the "Notes") through several privately negotiated transactions, at a weighted average price of 99.11% of the principal amount of the Notes. These repurchases were made pursuant to the Board of Directors' authorization given to management on August 18, 2004 and as amended on October 21, 2005, and represent the remaining amount authorized for repurchase. As of December 20, 2005, $300 million of the principal amount of the Notes remain outstanding.
