Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal  Officers.
 
On August 12, 2005 the Board of Directors elected Mary E. Minnick as a director of Target Corporation.  Ms. Minnick is Executive Vice President and President of Marketing, Strategy and Innovation of The Coca-Cola Company.  Ms. Minnick was also named to the Executive Committee and the Corporate Governance Committee of the Board, and has been designated as a Class II director.  Her initial term will expire in 2008.
