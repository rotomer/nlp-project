On April 12, 2007, Target Corporation ("Target") entered into a Five-Year Credit Agreement with Bank of America, N.A. as Administrative Agent and one of several lending banks, for a $2.0 billion unsecured revolving credit facility. The new facility replaces Target's $1.6 billion Five-Year Credit Agreement dated as of June 9, 2005. The new facility will expire in April 2012, unless the term is extended for up to two additional years under the terms of the facility. Borrowings under the new facility bear interest at the rates specified in the agreement, and the agreement contains one financial covenant (leverage ratio). A copy of the agreement will be filed as an Exhibit to Target's Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended May 5, 2007.
