Item 7.01       
Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
On April 21, 2010, Target Corporation, through its wholly-owned subsidiaries, Target Receivables Corporation and Target National Bank, delivered certain monthly reports relating to the Floating Rate Class A Asset Backed Notes, Series 2005-1, and the Subordinated Interests, Series 2005-1, issued by the Target Credit Card Owner Trust 2005-1 to the Trustee. These reports are being furnished as exhibits to this report.
 
As more fully described in the News Releases issued by Target Corporation on April 6, 2010 and April 14, 2010, Target Corporation's wholly-owned subsidiary, Target Receivables Corporation, purchased on April 14, 2010 all of the outstanding Floating Rate Class A Asset backed Notes, Series 2005-1 pursuant to the terms and conditions of a tender offer commenced on April 6, 2010.  Noteholders were paid a purchase price equal to the principal value along with interest from March 25, 2010 through April 13, 2010.  No further distributions are due the Noteholders.
