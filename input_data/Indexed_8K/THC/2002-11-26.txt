ITEM 5.    
Other Events.
        Tenet Healthcare Corporation, a Nevada corporation ("Tenet"), reaffirming that, as previously announced, its new management team consists of the following individuals:
        On November 22, 2002, Tenet's Board of Directors (the "Board") elected Mr. Schochet as Vice Chairman, a position to which he previously had been appointed. The significance of his election to the position is that, in light of his new responsibilities, he now will be a reporting person under Section 16 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
        On November 22, 2002, Tenet's Board elected Ms. Sulzbach as Chief Corporate Officer, General Counsel and Chief Compliance Officer, promoting her from her previous position as Executive Vice President, General Counsel and Chief Compliance Officer. Ms. Sulzbach already was a reporting person under Section 16 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
