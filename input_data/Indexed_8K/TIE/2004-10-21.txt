October 20, 2004 . . . Titanium Metals Corporation ("TIMET" or the "Company") (NYSE: TIE) announced today that its board of directors, at its most recent meeting, declared a quarterly dividend of $0.84375 per share on its 6-3/4% Series A Preferred Stock, payable on December 15, 2004 to stockholders of record as of the close of business on December 1, 2004.
