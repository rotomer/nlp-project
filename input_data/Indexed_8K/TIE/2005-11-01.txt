Item 8.01 Other Events.
Section 303A.03 of the New York Stock Exchange ("NYSE") Listed Company Manual requires NYSE-listed companies to disclose in their annual proxy statements the non-management director who is chosen to preside at all regularly-scheduled executive sessions of the non-management members of the board of directors. Titanium Metals Company ("TIMET") inadvertently omitted this information from its annual proxy statement dated April 8, 2005, and provides that information in this Current Report on Form 8-K. The Chair of TIMETs Audit Committee presides at all regularly-scheduled executive sessions of the non-management members of the board of directors. The Investor InformationCorporate Governance section of TIMETs website at www.timet.com includes copies of TIMETs Corporate Governance Guidelines, Code of Business Conduct and Ethics, Audit Committee Charter and the Audit Committees Procedures Regarding Accounting Complaints or Concerns.
