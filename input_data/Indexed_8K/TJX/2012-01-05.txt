On January 5, 2012, The TJX Companies, Inc. announced that its Board of Directors approved a two-for-one stock split of its common stock in the form of a stock dividend, payable February 2, 2012 to holders of record as of January 17, 2012. The quarterly cash dividend announced on December 1, 2011 and payable March 1, 2012 to holders of record as of February 9, 2012, will be proportionately adjusted from $0.19 to $0.095 per share to reflect the split.
