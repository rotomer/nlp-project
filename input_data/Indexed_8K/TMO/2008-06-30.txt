Election of Director
     On June 27, 2008, by vote of the Board of Directors of Thermo Fisher Scientific Inc. (the "Company"), William G. Parrett was elected a director of the Company. Mr. Parrett was appointed to the Company's Audit Committee.
     From 2003 to May 2007, Mr. Parrett served as the chief executive officer of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu (DDT). Prior to serving as chief executive officer of DDT, he was managing partner of Deloitte & Touche USA since 1999.
 
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned thereunto duly authorized, on this 30th day of June, 2008.
