(e) Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
On April 16, 2012, the Compensation Committee (the "Committee") of the Board of Directors of TripAdvisor, Inc. (the "Company") approved an increase in the annual base salary of Stephen Kaufer, the Company's President and Chief Executive Officer, from $300,000 to $500,000. The base salary increase for Mr. Kaufer is effective as of February 20, 2012.
Also on April 16, 2012, the Committee approved a base salary for Barry Diller, the Company's Chairman and Senior Executive. Effective as of January 1, 2012, Mr. Diller will receive a base salary of $100,000.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
