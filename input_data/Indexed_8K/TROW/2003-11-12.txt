Item 5. Other Events.
     Effective November 7, 2003, Cristina Wasiak no longer will serve as the Chief Financial Officer of T. Rowe Price Group, Inc. (the "Corporation"), a position she assumed in March 2001. Mr. George Roche, the Chairman and President of the Corporation, will assume responsibility for the Chief Financial Officer functions while the firm conducts a search for a new Chief Financial Officer. Mr. Roche previously served as the Corporation's Chief Financial Officer from 1984 until 1997 and also in 2000, when Ms. Wasiak's predecessor retired.
     Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: November 12, 2003
