Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On January 23, 2007, we entered into a consulting agreement with George A. Roche. Mr. Roche was our Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer prior to his retirement at the end of 2006.
Pursuant to the consulting agreement, Mr. Roche will provide strategic and other consulting services to us, as mutually agreed between Mr. Roche and our Chief Executive Officer, over an aggregate of up to eight weeks of full-time days in each calendar year. The consulting agreement is effective as of January 1, 2007, and thereafter is automatically renewed on January 1 of each year. It can be terminated by either party for any reason at the end of any year or upon at least 30 days prior written notice. The agreement provides for payments to Mr. Roche of $100,000 per year, plus reimbursement for reasonable out-of-pocket expenses.
A copy of the consulting agreement is attached as Exhibit 10 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
