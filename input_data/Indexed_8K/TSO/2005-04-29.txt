Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
Tesoro Corporation (the "Company") previously reported on Form 8-K dated March 21, 2005 the election of Michael E. Wiley to the Company's Board of Directors. As of March 2005, Mr. Wiley had not yet been appointed to any Committees of the Board of Directors. Effective April 4, 2005, Mr. Wiley was appointed by the Board of Directors to the Governance Committee and the Environmental, Health and Safety Committee. Form 8-K dated March 21, 2005 is hereby amended as required under Item 5.02 of Form 8-K to reflect the appointment of Mr. Wiley to these Committees.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Date: April 29, 2005
