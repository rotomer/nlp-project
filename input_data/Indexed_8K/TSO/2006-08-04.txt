Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
In the Current Report on Form 8-K dated July 21, 2006, Tesoro Corporation (the "Company") reported that John F. Bookout III had been elected to the Company's Board of Directors effective July 21, 2006 and would be appointed to one or more committees at a later date. Effective August 1, 2006, Mr. Bookout was appointed to the Audit Committee and Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors. The Current Report on Form 8-K dated July 21, 2006 is hereby amended as required under Item 5.02 to reflect the appointment of Mr. Bookout to these committees.
