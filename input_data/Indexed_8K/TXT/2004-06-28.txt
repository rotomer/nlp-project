Item 5. Other Events
            On June 28, 2004, Textron Inc. announced that Steven R. Loranger, who has served as executive vice president and chief operating officer since October 2002, has been named president and chief executive officer of ITT Industries, effective immediately. Textron also announced that it has streamlined reporting of its manufacturing segments and eliminated the position of chief operating officer. The company's four manufacturing segments - Cessna Aircraft, Bell Helicopter, Textron Fastening Systems and Industrial -- will report directly to Textron Chairman, President and CEO Lewis B. Campbell. Textron Financial Corporation will continue to report to Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer Ted French.
            Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
