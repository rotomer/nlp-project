Item 8.01 Other Events
On June 4, 2009, Textron Inc.s Cessna business unit informed its employees that continued order cancellations and weakness in general aviation markets are forcing further revisions to its aircraft production outlook, which will result in additional reductions in Cessnas workforce.
 
Textron plans to update its operating and financial outlook in conjunction with its second quarter earnings release.
