Item 8.01               Other Events.
 
On May 3, 2010, Tyco International Management Company, an affiliate of Tyco International Ltd. (the "Company"), entered into a consulting agreement with Robert B. Allen, presently the President, Chief Executive Officer and a director of Brink's Home Security Holdings, Inc. ("BHS"). Pursuant to the terms of the consulting agreement, Mr. Allen will, upon the consummation of the Company's acquisition of BHS (which is currently scheduled to take place on May 14, 2010, pending approval of the transaction by BHS shareholders) and for a period of twelve months thereafter, provide the Company with certain consulting services on a monthly, fixed fee basis.  The consulting agreement further provides that in exchange for his services, Mr. Allen will be entitled to receive a gross monthly fee of $72,917 and, during the term of the consultancy, Mr. Allen may not engage in any business that competes with the Company (including its ADT business) or solicit, recruit or hire any of the Company's employees or customers.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
