Item 8.01  Other Events.
 
As previously disclosed, pursuant to proposal number seven included in Tyco International Ltd.'s (the "Company") proxy statement for its annual general meeting of shareholders held on March 9, 2011, shareholders approved the cancellation of 28,088,101 shares held in treasury and a corresponding reduction in the Company's share capital.  In connection with the cancellation of these shares and the reduction in share capital, the Company amended its Articles of Association, effective following the publication of the notice to creditors required by Article 733 of the Swiss Code of Obligations and upon the filing of notarized documents with the commercial register of the Canton of Schaffhausen, Switzerland.
 
A copy of the amended Articles of Association is attached hereto as Exhibit 3.1 and incorporated herein by reference.
