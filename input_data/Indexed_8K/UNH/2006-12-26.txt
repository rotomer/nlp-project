Item 8.01 Other Events.
In April 2006, UnitedHealth Group Incorporated (the "Company") announced that it had received an informal inquiry from the Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC") relating to its stock option practices. On December 19, 2006, the Company received from the SEC staff a formal order of investigation. The Company has cooperated and will continue to cooperate with the SEC.
