Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
Senior members of UnitedHealth Group Incorporateds (the "Company") management team will be making a presentation at the Deutsche Bank 36th Annual Health Care Conference in Boston, Massachusetts on May 3, 2011 at 8:40 a.m., Eastern Time.
