Item 8.01 Other Events.
On December 6, 2012, Amil Participaes S.A. ("Amil"), a majority-owned subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group Incorporated (the "Company"), disclosed, pursuant to Brazilian law, that an affiliate of the Company purchased an additional 17,779,700 Amil shares in open market transactions on the Sao Paulo stock exchange (BM&FBOVESPA) and 8,283,405 newly-issued Amil shares in a private transaction with option holders under Amils employee stock option plan. These purchases took place in Brazil and bring the stake in Amil attributable to the Company as of December 5, 2012 to approximately 64.5% of Amils outstanding shares.
