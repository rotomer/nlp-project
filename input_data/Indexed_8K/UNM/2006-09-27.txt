Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
(b) On September 27, 2006, C. William Pollard, Chairman of the Board of Directors of UnumProvident Corporation, informed the company of his decision to resign from the position of Chairman effective September 30, 2006 and to retire from the Board effective December 31, 2006. The Board of Directors elected Jon S. Fossel to serve as Chairman of the Board effective October 1, 2006.
A copy of the press release announcing Mr. Pollard's decision to resign as Chairman and to retire from the Board, and the election of Mr. Fossel as Chairman of the Board is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
