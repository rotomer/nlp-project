This Current Report on Form 8-K/A amends certain information included in a Current Report on Form 8-K filed by Union Pacific Corporation (the Company) on December 9, 2008, regarding the acceptance by Jose H. Villarreal of an offer to serve on the Board of Directors of the Company (the Board) on December 5, 2008. The Board did not appoint Mr. Villarreal to serve on any of its committees at the time of his election. At a regular meeting held on February 5, 2009, the Board appointed Mr. Villarreal to serve on the Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee and the Finance Committee.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this Report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
Dated: February 5, 2009
