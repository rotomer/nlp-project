Item 8.01 Other Events.
On November 18, 2010, Union Pacific Corporation (the Company) announced that its Board of Directors voted to increase the quarterly dividend on the Company's common stock by 5 cents to 38 cents per share. The increased dividend is payable January 3, 2011, to stockholders of record on November 30, 2010.
