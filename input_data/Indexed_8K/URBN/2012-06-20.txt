Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On June 14, 2012, Urban Outfitters, Inc. (the "Company") executed an amendment to its line of credit facility (the "Line") with Wells Fargo Bank, National Association. As part of the amendment, the Company used the accordion feature of the Line to increase the total available credit under the revolving credit facility to $175.0 million. The amendment also revises certain financial covenants and joins certain subsidiaries of the Company as additional guarantors. The Line has a term of three years from April 25, 2011.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
