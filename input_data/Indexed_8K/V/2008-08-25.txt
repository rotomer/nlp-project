On August 25, 2008, Visa Inc. (the "Company") issued a press release announcing that the Company's 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the "Annual Meeting") will be held on April 21, 2009, with a record date of February 26, 2009. The Company's stockholders as of the record date will be provided notice of Internet availability of proxy materials and entitled to vote at the meeting. Notice of stockholder proposals to be considered at the Annual Meeting must be received by the Corporate Secretary at the principal executive offices of the Company between December 22, 2008 and the close of business on January 21, 2009. NEWS RELEASE
 
 
Visa Inc. Sets 2009 Annual Stockholders Meeting Date
 
 
 Visa Inc. (NYSE:V) announced today that its 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the "Annual Meeting") will be held on April 21, 2009, with a record date of February 26, 2009. Notice of stockholder proposals to be considered at the Annual Meeting must be received by the Corporate Secretary at the principal executive offices of the Company between December 22, 2008 and the close of business on January 21, 2009.
