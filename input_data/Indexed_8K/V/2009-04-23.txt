NEWS RELEASE
Visa Inc. Announces Quarterly Dividend
 Visa Inc. (NYSE:V) announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly dividend in the aggregate amount of $0.105 per share of class A common stock (determined in the case of class B and C common stock on an "as-converted" basis) payable on June 2, 2009, to all holders of record of the Company's class A, class B and class C common stock as of May 15, 2009.
SAN FRANCISCO, CA, April 23, 2009
