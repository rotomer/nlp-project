 Visa Inc. (NYSE:V) announced today that it will host an informational investor call to communicate the following changes that affect the presentation of Visa's financial results:

The call will take place today, October 26, 2010 at 5:00pm Eastern Time, hosted by Byron Pollitt, Chief Financial Officer, with the goal of clarifying these matters before the Company's fiscal fourth quarter and full-year 2010 financial results are released on Wednesday, October 27, 2010.
