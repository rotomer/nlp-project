Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On October 4, 2007, the Board of Directors of VF Corporation ("VF") announced that President and Chief Operating Officer Eric C. Wiseman will become Chief Executive Officer, effective January 1, 2008. Wiseman, 51, will succeed Chief Executive Officer Mackey J. McDonald, who will continue to serve as executive Chairman of VF's Board of Directors.
Mr. Wiseman, who has 29 years of experience in the apparel industry, has served as President and Chief Operating Officer of VF since March 1, 2006. He was elected a director of VF on October 19, 2006. Mr. Wiseman joined VF in 1995 and has held a progression of leadership roles within and across VF's coalitions since that time.
