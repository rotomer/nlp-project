Item 5.02 (b) Departure of Director.
On April 27, 2010, in accordance with the tenure policies of VF Corporation ("VF") set forth in its Corporate Governance Principles and By-laws, Barbara S. Feigin did not stand for reelection to the Board of Directors. VF acknowledged the outstanding service rendered by Ms. Feigin since her election as a director in 1987.
Item 5.02(e). Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
On April 26, 2010, Robert K. Shearer, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of VF, was awarded 10,000 shares of restricted stock. The restricted stock will vest on July 1, 2014, but is subject to forfeiture if Mr. Shearer leaves VF voluntarily or his employment is terminated by VF for any reason prior to the vesting date.
