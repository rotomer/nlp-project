ITEM: Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement
Item 1.02   Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.
Effective as of August 27, 2010, Vulcan Materials Company (Vulcan we or us) terminated its Term Loan  Credit Agreement (the Credit Agreement) with Wachovia Bank, National Association, as Administrative Agent and the other Lender Parties thereto (the Lenders) dated June 23, 2008.
The material terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement are set forth on our Current Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on June 27, 2008.
We chose to terminate the Credit Agreement because we no longer needed the borrowing capacity.
We did not incur any penalties by canceling the Credit Agreement prior to its scheduled Maturity Date of June 23, 2011.
