ITEM: Other Events
 
 
 
 
 
On November 9, 2010, we received an imminent danger order issued by the Mine Safety and Health Administration under section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 at its Occoquan quarry in Graham, Virginia.  The order was issued when a water truck driver was observed standing on the handrails going to the truck cab (approximately 9 feet off the ground) but was not wearing fall protection.
