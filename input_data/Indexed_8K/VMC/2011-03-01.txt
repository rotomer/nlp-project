On February 28, 2011, we received an imminent danger order issued by the Mine Safety and Health Administration under section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 at our Thompson S. Baker Cement Plant in Newberry, Florida. The order was issued when a production employee was observed working on an elevated platform five feet above the ground without fall protection. The employee was in the process of dislodging material from a bin.
