ITEM 5.02. DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS.
VeriSign, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), announced that Len J. Lauer and Gregory L. Reyes have resigned from the Company's Board of Directors, effective July 31, 2006. Mr. Lauer and Mr. Reyes were members of the Compensation Committee of the Board; Michelle Guthrie and Edward A. Mueller have been appointed to the Compensation Committee effective August 1, 2006. Neither Mr. Lauer's nor Mr. Reyes' resignation from the Board of Directors involved any disagreement with the Company.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
