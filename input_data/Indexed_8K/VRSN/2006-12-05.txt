Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On November 30, 2006, VeriSign, Inc. ("") announced that on November 29, 2006 it had received notification that the U.S. Department of Commerce has approved the Registry Agreement (the ""), between Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers ("") and VeriSign.

Item 8.01. Other Events.
As previously reported in VeriSign's prior periodic reports filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission, VeriSign was engaged in a legal dispute with ICANN over disagreements arising under the . Registry Agreement, effective May 2001, and . Registry Agreement, effective May 2001, both with ICANN. In October 2005, VeriSign and ICANN conditioned the proposed settlement of their legal dispute upon the execution of the 2006 .. Registry Agreement and approval of the 2006 . Registry Agreement by the U.S. Department of Commerce. VeriSign and ICANN have finalized a settlement of their legal dispute and, as part of the final settlement, VeriSign and ICANN intend to file dismissals of their pending litigation proceedings.
