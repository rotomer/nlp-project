(NYSE:VTR) ("Ventas" or the "Company") announced today it will issue its 2002 annual earnings on Wednesday, February 26, 2003. A conference call to discuss those earnings will be held that morning at 10:00 a.m. Eastern Time (9:00 a.m. Central Time.) The call is being webcast by CCBN and can be accessed through the Ventas website at www.ventasreit.com or at www.companyboardroom.com. VENTAS TO PRESENT AT UBS WARBURG HEALTHCARE CONFERENCE Ventas also announced that President and CEO Debra A. Cafaro and Chief Financial Officer Richard A. Schweinhart will make a presentation regarding the Company at the UBS Warburg Global Healthcare Services Conferences on Tuesday, February 4, 2003 at 2:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The presentation is being audio webcast and can be accessed at the Ventas website at www.ventasreit.com or at www.ubswarburg.com. The accompanying slides will also be available on Ventas's website at the time of the presentation. The webcast and slides will be archived at www.ventasreit.com for 30 days after the event. Ventas, Inc. is a healthcare real estate investment trust that owns 44 hospitals, 220 nursing facilities and nine other healthcare and senior housing facilities in 37 states. The Company also has investments in 25 additional healthcare and senior housing facilities. More information about Ventas can be found on its website at www.ventasreit.com.
