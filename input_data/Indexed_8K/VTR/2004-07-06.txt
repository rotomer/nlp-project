Schweinhart Senior Vice President and CFO (502) 357-9000 VENTAS CREDIT RATINGS UPGRADED TO 'BB' BY S&P ---------- Ventas Announces 2004 Second Quarter Earnings Release Date and Conference Call Louisville, KY (July 1, 2004) - Ventas, Inc. (NYSE:VTR) ("Ventas" or the "Company") said today that Standard & Poor's Ratings Services ("S&P") raised its corporate credit ratings on Ventas and certain of its affiliates to 'BB' from 'BB-'. S&P also revised the outlook on the Company to stable from positive. In addition, S&P raised its ratings on the Company's senior unsecured debt, which totals $366 million. In a press release issued on June 28, 2004, S&P said the upgrade acknowledges Ventas's steady improvement in both its business and financial profiles. Recent acquisitions have improved Ventas's diversification, and growing cash flow and profits, and lower leverage have contributed to stronger debt protection measures, the rating agency said. A copy of S&P's press release can be obtained at http://www.ventasreit.com/about/VentasSPRating.pdf. VENTAS ANNOUNCES 2004 SECOND QUARTER EARNINGS RELEASE DATE The Company also said it will issue its 2004 second quarter earnings release after the close of trading on Tuesday, July 27, 2004. A conference call to discuss those earnings will be held Wednesday, July 28, 2004 at 9:00 a.m. Eastern Time (8:00 a.m. Central Time). The call will be webcast live by CCBN and can be accessed at the Ventas website at www.ventasreit.com or at www.fulldisclosure.com. Ventas, Inc. is a healthcare real estate investment trust that owns healthcare-related facilities, including hospitals, nursing facilities and senior housing facilities. More information about Ventas can be found on its website at www.ventasreit.com.
