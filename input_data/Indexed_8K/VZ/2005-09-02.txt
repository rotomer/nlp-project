On September 1, 2005, Mr. David H. Benson, senior vice president and controller for Verizon Communications Inc., informed the Verizon Board of Directors of his intention to retire from the company by year-end.
 
 
The Board of Directors of Verizon Communications Inc. has amended the company's Bylaws, effective September 1, 2005, to provide that a director shall retire from the Board of Directors at the board meeting next following his or her 72nd birthday.
