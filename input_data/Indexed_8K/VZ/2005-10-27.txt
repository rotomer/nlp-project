Item 8.01 Other Events.
 
On an October 27, 2005 conference call with financial analysts and investors, Verizon Communications Inc. discussed its third quarter 2005 results and provided an update on its fiber-to-the-premises deployment strategy, known as FiOS. Verizon indicated that the earnings dilution from FiOS is about 10 cents per share for the year to date and will be an estimated four cents per share in the fourth quarter.
