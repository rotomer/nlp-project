Item 8.01 Other Events.
Today a subsidiary of Verizon Communications Inc. ("Verizon") delivered approximately 29.4 million shares of newly-issued Verizon common stock to Price Communications Corporation ("Price") in exchange for a limited partnership interest in Verizon Wireless of the East LP ("VZEast"), pursuant to the Exchange Agreement dated as of December 18, 2001 by and among Price, Price Communications Cellular, Inc., Price Communications Cellular Holdings, Inc. and Verizon, Verizon Wireless, Inc., Cellco Partnership, and VZEast. The issuance of these shares has been reflected in Verizon's previously reported diluted earnings per share as exchangeable equity interest.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned, thereunto duly authorized.
