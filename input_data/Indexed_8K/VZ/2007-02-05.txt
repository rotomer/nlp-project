Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.
In order to take into account the broader scope of responsibilities that Dennis F. Strigl assumed upon his appointment as the President and Chief Operating Officer of Verizon Communications Inc., the Human Resources Committee of Verizon's Board of Directors considered relevant market data and approved an increase in Mr. Strigl's annual base salary from $1.125 million to $1.25 million. In addition, in connection with Mr. Strigl's new position, he has been granted a special restricted stock unit award valued at $3 million. This grant was made on February 1, 2007 and the entire award will vest and become payable two years after the grant date.
