On May 31, 2007, Verizon New York Inc., an indirect, wholly-owned subsidiary of Verizon Communications Inc. (the "Corporation"), received a $2 billion capital contribution from its parent corporation, NYNEX Corporation. This capital contribution was used by Verizon New York to reduce the principal amount outstanding under its short-term notes payable to affiliates of the Corporation, which was approximately $4.15 billion as of March 31, 2007.
