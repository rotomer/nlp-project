On March 10, 2008, at the Bear Stearns Media Conference, Doreen Toben, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Verizon Communications Inc. (Verizon) provided an update of Verizon's initiatives to focus on its networks and strategic assets for sustained organic growth. Ms. Toben stated that Verizon was continuing to invest in networks and new technologies that provide the platform for growth, innovation and productivity. She noted that Verizon expected to deliver strong fundamentals, positive momentum and sustainable growth in 2008 as it had in 2007. She stated that Verizon's revenue outlook remains strong and that Verizon expects that the strong revenue growth trends in 2007 will continue in 2008.
