Item 5.02. DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS.
 
 
(d) On October 4, 2006, Walgreen Co. (the "Company") filed a Current Report on Form 8-K with the Securities and Exchange Commission in connection with the announcement that Nancy M. Schlichting was elected to the Company's Board of Directors effective October 1, 2006.
 
As of October 4, 2006, Ms. Schlichting had not been named to serve on any committee of the Board, nor had it been determined to which committee or committees, if any, she may be named in the future.  On October 11, 2006, the Board
appointed Ms. Schlichting to the Audit Committee of the Board and the Nominating and Governance Committee of the Board.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
