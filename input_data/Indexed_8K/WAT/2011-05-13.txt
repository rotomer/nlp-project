Appointment of William J. Curry as Principal Accounting Officer
     On May 10, 2011, the Board of Directors of Waters Corporation (the "Company") appointed William J. Curry, (55) Vice President, Corporate Controller to serve as the Company's Principal Accounting Officer. Mr. Curry joined the Company in 1999 as Assistant Corporate Controller and was promoted to Vice President, Corporate Controller in 2003. Prior to joining the Company in 1999, Mr. Curry was a Certified Public Accountant with Coopers & Lybrand LLP (NKA PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP) and has held a variety of senior corporate and operational positions with Ciba-Corning Diagnostics Inc. (NKA Siemens Medical Diagnostics), and Millennium Pharmaceuticals Inc.
