Item 8.01.
The annual meeting of stockholders of Western Digital Corporation (the "Company") was held on November 6, 2008. Results of the voting at the meeting are set forth below.
     . The stockholders elected the following ten directors to hold office until the next annual meeting and until their successors are elected and qualified. The voting results were as follows:
Election of Directors
     . The stockholders approved an amendment to the Company's 2005 Employee Stock Purchase Plan to increase by 8,000,000 the number of shares of the Company's common stock available for issuance under the plan. The voting results were as follows:
Approval of amendment to 2005 Employee Stock Purchase Plan
     . The stockholders ratified the appointment of KPMG LLP as the Company's independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending July 3, 2009. The voting results were as follows:
Appointment of KPMG LLP as independent registered public accounting firm
