ITEM 9.  REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE
On August 7, 2002, each of the Principal Executive Officer, Richard A. Abdoo, and Principal Financial Officer, Paul Donovan, of Wisconsin Energy Corporation submitted to the Securities and Exchange Commission sworn statements dated August 6, 2002 pursuant to Securities and Exchange Commission Order No. 4-460.
A copy of each of these statements is attached hereto as an Exhibit (99.1 and 99.2).
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
