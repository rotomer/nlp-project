ITEM 5.  OTHER EVENTS AND REGULATION FD DISCLOSURE.
On March 7, 2003, Standard & Poor's Ratings Services lowered its corporate credit ratings on Wisconsin Energy Corporation from A- to BBB+ and on Wisconsin Electric Power Company and Wisconsin Gas Company, Wisconsin Energy's primary subsidiaries, both from A to A-. Standard & Poor's lowered its ratings on Wisconsin Energy's senior unsecured debt from A- to BBB+; on Wisconsin Electric's senior secured debt from A to A- and on Wisconsin Gas' senior unsecured debt from A to A-. Standard & Poor's affirmed Wisconsin Electric's A- senior unsecured debt rating. Standard & Poor's lowered the rating on Wisconsin Energy's preferred stock from BBB to BBB- and on Wisconsin Electric's preferred stock from BBB+ to BBB. Standard & Poor's affirmed the A-2 short-term rating of Wisconsin Energy and lowered the short-term ratings of both Wisconsin Electric and Wisconsin Gas from A-1 to A-2. Wisconsin Electric's senior secured and senior unsecured debt will both be rated A- by Standard & Poor 's. Standard & Poor's assigned a stable outlook.
