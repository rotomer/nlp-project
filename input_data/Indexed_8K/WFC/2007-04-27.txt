The Company held its annual meeting of stockholders on April 24, 2007. At the meeting, stockholders elected all 16 of the directors nominated by the Board of Directors and ratified the appointment of KPMG LLP as independent auditors for 2007. Each director received a greater number of votes cast "for" his or her election than votes cast "against" his or her election. The stockholder proposal regarding emission reduction goals for the Company and its customers was withdrawn before the meeting. The stockholders rejected the four stockholder proposals presented at the meeting as follows:
Stockholder Proposal Regarding Separation of Board Chair and CEO Positions
 
Stockholder Proposal Regarding an Advisory Vote on Executive Compensation
 
Stockholder Proposal Regarding Adoption of a Policy Limiting Benefits Under Supplemental Executive Retirement Plan
 
Stockholder Proposal Regarding a Report on Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) Data
