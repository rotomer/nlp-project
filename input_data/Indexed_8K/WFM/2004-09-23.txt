Exhibit 99.1 Whole Foods Market Announces Fourth Quarterly Dividend AUSTIN, Texas, Sept. 22 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Whole Foods Market, Inc. (Nasdaq: WFMI) today announced that its Board of Directors declared its fourth quarterly dividend. The Company estimates it will pay out approximately $9 million to shareholders in the $0.15 per share dividend which is payable October 18, 2004 to shareholders of record at the close of business on October 8, 2004. About Whole Foods Market: Founded in 1980 in Austin, Texas, Whole Foods Market(R) (http://www.wholefoodsmarket.com ) is the largest natural and organic foods supermarket retailer. The Company had $3.7 billion in sales for the twelve months ending July 4, 2004 and currently has 163 stores in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
