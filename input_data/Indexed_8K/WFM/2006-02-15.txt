Item 8.01. Other Events.
On February 14, 2006, Hass Hassan resigned from the Compensation Committee of our Board of Directors. Mr. Hassan resigned this position because he did not qualify as an "independent director" (as defined in Rule 4200 of the NASDAQ Marketplace Rules) by virtue of his service as a consultant to Whole Foods Market on matters relating to Fresh & Wild, Ltd., an organic food retailer which Mr. Hassan founded and we acquired in 2004.
Mr. Hassan otherwise remains as a member of our Board of Directors.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
