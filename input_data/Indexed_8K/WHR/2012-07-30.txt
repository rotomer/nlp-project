Item 8.01. Other Events.
On July 30, 2012, the U.S. Department of Commerce ("DOC") issued an affirmative preliminary determination in the anti-dumping investigation of large residential washers from South Korea and Mexico, which was initiated by a petition filed by Whirlpool Corporation. Final decisions on these matters by the DOC and the U.S. International Trade Commission, following completion of their investigations, are currently expected in the first half of 2013.
