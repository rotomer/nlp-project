Item 5.02     (b) Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
The Board of Directors of WellPoint, Inc. (the "Corporation") has received notice from Allan B. Hubbard, a member of the Board of Directors, that Mr. Hubbard has been appointed by the President of the United States of America to the position of director of the White House National Economic Council effective January 18, 2005 and therefore intends to retire from the Board of Directors effective on January 17, 2005.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
Dated: January 14, 2005
