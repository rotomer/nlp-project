Item 8.01. Other Events.
In accordance with Rule 10b5-1 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, The Williams Companies, Inc. ("Williams") Board of Directors approved guidelines which permit its officers and other insiders to enter into 10b5-1 trading plans or arrangements for systematic trading of Williams securities. Williams has been advised that Ralph A. Hill, Senior Vice President, entered into a trading plan on November 8, 2004, that provides for sales of Williams common stock subject to certain sales price limits. Williams anticipates that, as permitted by Rule 10b5-1 and the foregoing guidelines, other officers and insiders may establish similar trading plans in the future.
