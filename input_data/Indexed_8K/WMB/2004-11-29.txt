TULSA, Okla.  Williams (NYSE:WMB) announced today that it plans to present a webcast on its Exploration & Production business for analysts on Nov. 30, beginning at 1 p.m. Eastern.

As previously reported, average daily production volumes in Exploration & Production have increased 18 percent since the beginning of 2004.
