As part of a presentation given at the 2008 Deutsche Bank Media and Telecommunications Conference on June 10, 2008, The Washington Post Company reported that the estimated expense for the Voluntary Retirement Incentive Program offered to some employees of The Washington Post newspaper and Corporate Office will be approximately $80 million. Most of these charges will be recorded in the second quarter of 2008 and funded primarily from the assets of the Company's pension plans.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
