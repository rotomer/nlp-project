Section 8  Other Events
 
On December 23, 2011, the U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia granted with prejudice the Defendants' Motion to Dismiss the lawsuit entitled , Civil Action No. 10-cv-01835 (BJR).
Plumbers Local #200 Pension Fund v. The Washington Post Company, Donald E. Graham, Hal S. Jones
The lawsuit was filed on October 28, 2010 and alleged that The Washington Post Company ("the Company") and certain of its officers made materially false or misleading statements, or failed to disclose material facts, relating to Kaplan Higher Education, in violation of the federal securities laws during the Class Period from July 31, 2009 through August 13, 2010. The Court issued a 23-page opinion granting with prejudice the motion filed by the Company and its officers to dismiss the complaint against them.
The Company has not previously accrued any liability associated with this matter.
For additional information about this lawsuit, see Item 3  Legal Proceedings of the Company's 2010 Annual Report on Form 10-K, filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 2, 2011.
