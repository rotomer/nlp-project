Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers
On December 2, 2011, prior to the date on which WPX Energy, Inc. (the "Company") became subject to the requirements of Section 13(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of the Company increased the size of the Board from one to three members and elected Donald R. Chappel and William G. Lowrie as members of the Board, in each case effective December 11, 2011. Both Messrs. Chappel and Lowrie were also appointed to serve as members of the Audit Committee of the Board.
 
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
 
DATED: December 13, 2011
