ITEM 5.03(a). AMENDMENTS TO ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION OR BYLAWS.
     On October 19, 2006, the Weyerhaeuser Company Board of Directors approved an amendment to the company Bylaws. The Bylaw amendment provides that shares of the corporation do not need to be represented by certificates. A written statement containing the information required on certificates will be sent to shareholders after the issuance or transfer of shares without certificates.
