ITEM 5.02. DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS
Weyerhaeuser Company is filing this amendment to its Current Report on Form 8-K filed on September 11, 2006 which reported the appointment of Kim Williams to the Board of Directors of the company. At the time of her election, no determination had been made as to which committees of the Board Ms. Williams would serve. On December 14, 2006, the Board of Directors appointed Ms. Williams to serve on the Compensation Committee and the Audit Committee.
