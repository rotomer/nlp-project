Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
     The Company announced on January 18, 2007, that Debra A. Cafaro has been elected to the Company's Board of Directors for a term beginning February 15, 2007. Under the terms of the Company's Bylaws, Cafaro will stand for election at the Weyerhaeuser annual meeting of shareholders to be held on April 19, 2007. The board has not yet determined committee assignments for the new director.
