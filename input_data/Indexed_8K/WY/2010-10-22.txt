ITEM 5.02. DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR CERTAIN OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICERS; COMPENSATORY ARRANGEMENTS OF CERTAIN OFFICERS
Effective October 18, 2010, Jeanne Hillman, formerly Vice President and Chief Accounting Officer changed roles and assumes the role of Vice President and Controller  Operations.
Effective October 18, 2010, Jerry Richards, 42, is Chief Accounting Officer. He was Director-Financial Reporting from 2002 to 2005, Controller-Financial Reporting from 2005-2008, and Controller  Corporate and Reporting from 2008 to 2010. Prior to coming to Weyerhaeuser, he spent 12 years in public accounting, including time in the national office of KPMG. Mr. Richards is a CPA licensed in the State of Oregon.
