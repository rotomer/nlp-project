Item 8.01. Other Events. On February 15, 2005, the Registrant announced that it intends to submit a concept proposal for an integrated casino resort project to be built in Singapore. On December 29, 2004, the Government of Singapore invited potential investors to submit concept plans, which will be evaluated by the government. Following its evaluation, the government may issue a formal request for proposal. The Company's concept proposal will be submitted on or before the February 28, 2005 deadline. There can be no assurances regarding the Registrant's business prospects with respect to this opportunity.
