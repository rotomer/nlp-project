Item 8.01 Other Events. Wynn Resorts, Limited announced on February 24, 2006 that an affiliate has submitted an application to the Government of Macau for a land concession for 54 acres on the Cotai Strip. The master plan accompanying the application provides for development of three casino resort hotels and a non-gaming hotel/residential property known as a Taiwanese Guest House.
