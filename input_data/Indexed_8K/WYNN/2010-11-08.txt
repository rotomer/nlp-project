Item 5.02
On November 7, 2010, Andrew Pascal, the President of Wynn Las Vegas and Encore at Wynn Las Vegas, advised Wynn Las Vegas, LLC (the Company), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Wynn Resorts, Limited, that he was resigning his position, effective December 1, 2010.  There are no disagreements between the Company and Mr. Pascal that caused or contributed to his resignation.  Mr. Pascal will be succeeded by Marilyn Winn Spiegel, age 58, who joins the Company from Harrahs Entertainment, Inc., where she spent 22 years in operations, most recently as Regional President of Ballys, Paris Las Vegas and Planet Hollywood Resort and Casino.
