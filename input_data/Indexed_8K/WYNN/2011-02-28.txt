Item 1.01.  Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement.
On February 24, 2011, Wynn Resorts, Limited (the Company) entered into a sixth amendment to the Employment Agreement, dated as of October 4, 2002, as amended, by and between the Company and Stephen A.
