Exhibit 99.1 U. S. STEEL CANADA WITHDRAWS FROM WABUSH MINES SALES NEGOTIATIONS HAMILTON, Ontario, March 4, 2008 - U. S. Steel Canada Inc., a foreign subsidiary of United States Steel Corporation (NYSE: X), announced today that it has withdrawn from negotiations to sell its 44.6 percent interest in the Wabush Mines Joint Venture to ArcelorMittal Dofasco Inc. Cleveland-Cliffs Inc. holds a 26.8 percent interest and Dofasco holds a 28.6 percent interest in the Wabush Mines Joint Venture. -oOo- For more information about U. S.
