Item 5.02
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers: Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers
 
On September 2, 2005, Ronald M. Moquist, 59, a member of the Xcel Energy Inc. Board of directors since May 2005, announced his resignation from the board.  Mr. Moquist told the board his responsibilities as chief executive officer and director of Raven Industries in Sioux Falls, S.D., and association with eight non-profit boards do not allow for a full and fair commitment to his duties as a director of Xcel Energy.  Mr. Moquist's resignation letter stated his resignation would be effective upon acceptance, which is expected within the next week
