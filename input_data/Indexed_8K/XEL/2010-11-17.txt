Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
 
Xcel Energy Inc. management will meet with European investors in London and Paris on Nov. 18 and Nov. 19, 2010.  Management plans to update investors on Xcel Energy Inc.s operations, business plans, reiterate plans for 2010 earnings to be in the upper half of the guidance range of $1.55 to $1.65 per diluted share and reiterate 2011 ongoing earnings guidance of $1.65 to $1.75 per diluted share.
Interested investors may access the presentation slides and a link to the webcast of the conference presentation at the following URL: http://www.xcelenergy.com
