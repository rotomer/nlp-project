(e)           On December 15, 2010, the Xcel Energy Inc. board of directors approved the Xcel Energy Inc. Amended and Restated Executive Annual Discretionary Award Plan (the Amended and Restated Plan), a discretionary cash bonus plan.  The Amended and Restated Plan amends and restates the 2010 Executive Annual Discretionary Award Plan (the 2010 Plan) approved by the board of directors on December 16, 2009.  The Amended and Restated Plan extends the term of the 2010 Plan to March 31, 2016.  Otherwise, the terms of the original 2010 Plan remain unchanged and the Amended and Restated Plan is intended to operate in the same manner as the 2010 Plan.
