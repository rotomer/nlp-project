Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers. On June 8, 2005, John Loudon informed the Registrant that, for personal reasons, he will be resigning as a Director of the Registrant effective immediately. Mr. Loudon will continue as a director of certain U.K. subsidiaries of the Registrant.
