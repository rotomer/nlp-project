Scotton Investor Relations Media Relations (441) 294-7460 (441) 294-7165 XL CAPITAL LTD ANNOUNCES THAT UNDERWRITERS EXERCISED THEIR OPTION TO PURCHASE ADDITIONAL ORDINARY SHARES Hamilton, Bermuda (December 8, 2005) - XL Capital Ltd ("XL" or the "Company") (NYSE: XL) announced today that the underwriters exercised their option to purchase an additional 5,076,900 ordinary shares in connection with XL's previously announced plans to sell 33,846,000 of ordinary shares and $745,000,000 of equity security units. Following closing of both offerings tomorrow, total gross proceeds to XL are expected to be approximately $3.2 billion. The joint book-running managers for the offerings are Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Citigroup Corporate and Investment Banking.
