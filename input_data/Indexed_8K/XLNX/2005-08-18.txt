Item 5.02(d) Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.
 
On August 17, 2005, Xilinx, Inc. issued a press release announcing the election on August 4, 2005 of J. Michael Patterson as a new board member.  Mr. Patterson will begin serving as a director at the next meeting of the Board of Directors.  He has not yet been appointed to any board committees but is a likely candidate for the audit committee given his substantial background in accounting and tax. A copy of this press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.
