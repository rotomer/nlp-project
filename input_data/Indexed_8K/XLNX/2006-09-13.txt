EXHIBIT 99.1
 
XILINX PROVIDES SEPTEMBER QUARTER FY07 BUSINESS UPDATE
 Xilinx, Inc. (Nasdaq: XLNX) today announced its business update for the September quarter of fiscal 2007.  The Company now expects September quarter sales to decline 4% to 7% sequentially.  This is a revision from previous sales guidance of flat to down 5% sequentially, and is a result of lower than anticipated sales from the Asia Pacific region.
SAN JOSE, CA, SEPTEMBER 13, 2006
This release contains forward-looking statements and projections.
