Item 8.01
Other Events
On March 31, 2005, in connection with the Grefer matter involving claims by a landowner in Louisiana for damage to his property, the Louisiana Fourth Circuit Court of Appeals reduced the trial court award of punitive damages from $1 billion to $112 million and left unchanged compensatory damages of $56 million.  The Corporation intends to promptly appeal this decision.
