Item 8.01   Other Events
On September 27, 2006, the registrant amended its Corporate Governance Guidelines by adding the following new provision relating to the election of directors:
In any non-contested election of directors, any director nominee who receives a greater number of votes "withheld" from his or her election than votes "for" such election shall tender his or her resignation.  Within 90 days after certification of the election results the Board of Directors will decide, through a process managed by the Board Affairs Committee and excluding the nominee in question, whether to accept the resignation.  Absent a compelling reason for the director to remain on the Board, the Board shall accept the resignation.  The Board will promptly disclose its decision and, if applicable, the reasons for rejecting the tendered resignation, on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
