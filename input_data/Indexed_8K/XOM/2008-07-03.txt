Item 8.01
Other Events
On July 1, 2008, affiliates of Exxon Mobil Corporation completed the sale of their interests in the natural gas transport business in northern Germany.  This transaction does not affect the exploration, production and natural gas sale and storage activities conducted by ExxonMobil affiliates in Germany.
The positive after-tax earnings of this transaction of approximately $1.6 billion will be reported in third quarter 2008 results.
