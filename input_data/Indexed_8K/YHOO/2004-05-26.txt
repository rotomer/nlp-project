Item 5.  Other Events.
 
On May 24, 2004, Yahoo! Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Yahoo!" or the "Company"), completed its acquisition of an additional interest in Kelkoo S.A. ("Kelkoo").  As a result of the acquisition, Yahoo!'s ownership interest in Kelkoo increased from approximately 90% to approximately 99.9% pursuant to the terms of the previously announced definitive agreement.  The acquisition follows a prior acquisition of a majority interest in Kelkoo by the Company on April 5, 2004.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
