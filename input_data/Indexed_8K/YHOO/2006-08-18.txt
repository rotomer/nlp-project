Item 8.01 Other Events
 
David Filo, co-founder and Chief Yahoo!, has established a plan under Rule 10b5-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), for the trading of Yahoo! stock.  Under the plan, Mr. Filo will sell up to two million shares of Yahoo! common stock in accordance with a prearranged trading schedule over a twelve-month period beginning in October 2006. The shares will be sold on the open market at prevailing market prices.
 
This plan is intended to comply with Rule 10b5-1 of the Exchange Act, and the Company's Rule 10b5-1 Trading Program policy which permits certain corporate officers to establish "blind trusts" or prearranged trading plans to govern the sale of a specified number or dollar amount of shares of stock over a predetermined period of time.
Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
