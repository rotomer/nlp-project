Item 8.01 Other Events
Jerry Yang, co-founder and Chief Yahoo!, has established a plan under Rule 10b5-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), for the trading of Yahoo! stock.  Under the plan, Mr. Yang transferred three million shares of Yahoo! common stock to a blind trust, which has complete discretion to trade the stock.
This plan is intended to comply with Rule 10b5-1 of the Exchange Act, and the Company's Rule 10b5-1 Trading Program policy which permits certain corporate officers to establish "blind trusts" or prearranged trading plans to govern the sale of up to a specified number or dollar amount of shares of stock over a predetermined period of time.
