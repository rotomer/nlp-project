On March 28, 2012, Yahoo! Inc. issued the following press statement:
Yahoo! has been moving aggressively to intensify its focus on delivering shareholder value  reconstituting its Board of Directors and appointing a new CEO who is moving rapidly to re-shape the business. Unfortunately, Mr. Loeb rejected our efforts to bring Third Point's perspective into the board room through the offer of two board seats. Nevertheless, the Board made clear to Mr. Loeb that it remains open to hearing Third Point's ideas and to working constructively with Third Point, as with all of its shareholders.
