     After a considerable period of deliberation, Dr. Regina Herzlinger, a director of Zimmer Holdings, Inc. (the "Company") who has been nominated for re-election to the Board, informed the Company on February 18, 2005 of her decision not to stand for re-election as a director at the next annual meeting of shareholders to be held on May 2, 2005. Dr. Herzlinger indicated her decision was not the result of any disagreement with the Company on any matter relating to the Company's operations, policies or practices. Dr. Herzlinger will serve the remainder of her term as a director.
